# Disguise Kit

## Overview
**Price**: 25 gp

**Weight**: 3 lb.

***Disguise Kit.*** This [[Pouch|pouch]] of cosmetics, hair dye, and small props lets you create disguises that change your physical appearance. Proficiency with this kit lets you add your [[3 - Proficiency Bonus|proficiency bonus]] to any [[4 - Ability Checks|ability checks]] you make to create a visual disguise.
