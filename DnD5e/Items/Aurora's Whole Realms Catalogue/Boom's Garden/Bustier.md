## Description
The bustier is the showpiece of fashion armor, secured upon the [[Broadbelt|broadbelt]] and rising into complementary spiral or floral patterns. We offer this plate mail breastplate in many metals: bronze (30 gp); iron (40 gp); steel (50 gp); silver (75 gp); and gold (95 gp). All sizes are lined with linen padding for comfort. Unique patterns can be special ordered and paid on an individual basis.
