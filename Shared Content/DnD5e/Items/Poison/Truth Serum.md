# Truth Serum
## Overview
*Truth Serum (Ingested).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 hour. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature can't knowingly speak a lie, as if under the effect of a *zone of truth* spell.
