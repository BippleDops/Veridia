# Bullseye Lantern
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: adventuring gear
**Properties**: 
**Weight**: 2 lb.
**Value**: 10 gp
## Description: 
A bullseye lantern casts bright light in a 60-foot cone and dim light for an additional 60 feet. Once lit, it burns for 6 hours on a flask (1 pint) of oil.
