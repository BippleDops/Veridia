# The Gilded Serpent Tavern

## General Info
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Shadowhaven]]

## Description
The Gilded Serpent is a high-class tavern that is popular with the wealthy elite of Shadowhaven. It is known for its expensive drinks, its beautiful decor, and its discreet service.

## GM Notes
- The tavern is a front for the Shadowhaven Smugglers Ring.
- The owner of the tavern, [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Melody Goldtooth]], is a high-ranking member of the Ring.
- The tavern is a good place to gather information about the city's elite, but it is also a dangerous place for those who are not careful.
