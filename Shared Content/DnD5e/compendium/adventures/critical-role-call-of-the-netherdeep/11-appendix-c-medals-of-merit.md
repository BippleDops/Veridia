*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep* 

## Using the Cards

The cards on this page are meant to be photocopied and given to players whose characters win medals of merit in chapter 1 (see that chapter for contest rules).

The cards in appendix D are meant to be given to players whose characters gain Fragments of Suffering in chapter 6 (see that chapter for more information).
