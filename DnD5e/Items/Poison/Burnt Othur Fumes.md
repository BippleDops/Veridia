# Burnt Othur Fumes
## Overview
*Burnt Othur Fumes (Inhaled).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or take 10 (`dice: 3d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage, and must repeat the saving throw at the start of each of its turns. On each successive failed save, the character takes 3 (`dice: 1d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage. After three successful saves, the [[Poisoned|poison]] ends.
