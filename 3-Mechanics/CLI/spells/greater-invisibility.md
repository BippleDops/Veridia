# Greater Invisibility
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*4th-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You or a creature you touch becomes [invisible](/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#invisible) until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is [invisible](/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#invisible) as long as it is on the target's person.

**Classes**: [Bard](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/bard.md), [Druid (Circle of the Land)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid-circle-of-the-land.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/sorcerer.md), [Warlock (The Archfey)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/warlock-the-archfey.md), [Wizard](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 246. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
