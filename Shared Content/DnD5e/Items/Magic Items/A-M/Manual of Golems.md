## Description

This tome contains information and incantations necessary to make a particular type of golem. The GM chooses the type or determines it randomly. To decipher and use the manual, you must be a spellcaster with at least two 5th-level spell slots. A creature that can’t use a *manual of golems* and attempts to read it takes `dice: 6d6` (6d6) [[17 - Damage and Healing|psychic]] damage.

`dice: [[Manual of Golems]]^ManualOfGolems]]`

| **d20** | **Golem** | **Time** | **Cost**   |
| ------- | --------- | -------- | ---------- |
| 1–5     | Clay      | 30 days  | 65,000 gp  |
| 6–17    | Flesh     | 60 days  | 50,000 gp  |
| 18      | Iron      | 120      | 100,000 gp |
| 19–20   | Stone     | 90       | 80,000 gp  |
^ManualOfGolems

To create a golem, you must spend the time shown on the table, working without interruption with the manual at hand and [[10 - Resting|resting]] no more than 8 hours per day You must also pay the specified cost to purchase supplies.

Once you finish creating the golem, the [[Book|book]] is consumed in eldritch flames. The golem becomes animate when the ashes of the manual are sprinkled on it. It is under your control, and it understands and obeys your spoken commands.
