# Aarakocra
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 282*  


- **Ability Scores**: dex +2 wis +2
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: walk: 20 ft.; fly: 50 ft.

## Traits

### Dive Attack

If you are flying and dive at least 30 ft. straight toward a target and then hit it with a melee weapon attack, the attack deals an extra `d6` damage to the target.

### Talons

You are proficient with your unarmed strikes, which deal `d4` slashing damage on a hit.

### Language

You can speak, read, and write Auran.
