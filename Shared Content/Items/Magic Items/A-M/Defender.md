## Description
You gain a +3 bonus to [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]].

The first time you [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] with the sword on each of your turns, you can transfer some or all of the swordâ€™s bonus to your [[2 - Armor|Armor Class]], instead of using the bonus on any attacks that turn. For example, you could reduce the bonus to your attack and damage rolls to +1 and gain a +2 bonus to [[2 - Armor|AC]]. The adjusted bonuses remain in effect until the start of your next turn, although you must hold the sword to gain a bonus to [[2 - Armor|AC]] from it.
