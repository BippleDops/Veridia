## Quick Rules
- **Surprise**: Can't move or act first turn, no reactions
- **Cover**: Half (+2 AC), 3/4 (+5 AC), Total (can't target)
- **Flanking** (optional): Advantage when opposite ally
- **Opportunity Attacks**: Leave reach without Disengage
