## Description
While you wear these boots, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across. You also have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Dexterity|Dexterity]] ([[Dexterity|Stealth]]) checks that rely on moving silently.
