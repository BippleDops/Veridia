# S-102 Downburst Hammer

## Read-Aloud
> The clouds above fist tight—and punch. The world drops with you.

## Setup
- Band: Mid • Category: Weather

## Mechanics
- Helm DC 16 to skirt core
- Structure Save DC 15 on failure

## Outcomes
- Success: Clean pass
- Partial: 1d6 damage; lose one altitude band
- Fail: 2d6 damage; crew Dex save DC 13 or prone; panic check for nervous crew

## Integrations
- Altitude pressure inversion; crew events
