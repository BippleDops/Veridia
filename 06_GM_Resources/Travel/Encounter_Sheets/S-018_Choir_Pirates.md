# S-018 Choir Pirates

## Read-Aloud
> Their hulls glitter with prismatic scars. As they close, a chord thrums across the sky—your rigging hums in sympathy.

## Setup
- Band: Any • Category: Faction/Combat

## Mechanics
- Sensor DC 14; Gunnery DC 14; Group Sanity DC 13 vs. resonance stun
