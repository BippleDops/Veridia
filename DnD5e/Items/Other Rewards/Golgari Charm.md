# Golgari Charm
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
When you activate this charm, you can cast the [[dispel magic]], [[false life]] (3rd-level version), or [[stinking cloud]] spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.
