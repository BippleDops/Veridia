## Description
We offer clean- and hotburning anthracite coal (1 gp per pound) in blocks for use in braziers and other non-vented fires as well as the more common bituminous coal (1 gp per 20 pounds), which must be vented.

## Additional Details
10 pounds of anthracite and 200 pounds of bituminous are required for a complete laboratory
