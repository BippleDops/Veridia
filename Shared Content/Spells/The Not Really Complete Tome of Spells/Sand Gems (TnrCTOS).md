# Sand Gems (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *8th-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a handful of sand)

**Duration**: Permanent


## Effects
You turn a handful of sand into 2d4 cut and polished gemstones. Roll d20 to deter-mine the type and value. The transformation is permanent and the gemstones continue to radiate magic.

**d20 Gemstone.**

1-4 White agate or tiger eye (10 gp)

5-8 Chalcedony or citrine (50 gp)

9-12 Moonstone or sardonyx (50 gp)

13-14 Amber or coral (100 gp)

15-16 Red or red-brown garnet, spinel, or tourmaline (100 gp)

17-19 Topaz (500 gp)

19 Fire opal or yellow sapphire (1,000 gp)

20 Jacinth or ruby (5,000 gp)
