# Potion of Speed
*Potion, minor, very rare*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [haste](/compendium/spells/haste.md) spell for 1 minute (no concentration required). The potion's yellow fluid is streaked with black and swirls on its own.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 188*
