# High Inquisitor Valerius

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 50
**Occupation**: High Inquisitor of the Order of the Azure Flame
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/The Citadel of Azure Flame]]

## Description
High Inquisitor Valerius is a charismatic and driven man who is the leader of the Order of the Azure Flame. He is a brilliant tactician and a powerful warrior, and he is utterly convinced of the righteousness of his cause.

## Backstory
Valerius was once a promising young mage, but he was forced to watch as his family was killed by a rogue sorcerer. This event turned him against magic, and he has dedicated his life to eradicating it from the world.

## GM Notes
- Valerius is a complex and nuanced antagonist. He is not evil for the sake of being evil, but he is willing to do whatever it takes to achieve his goals.
- He is a master of rhetoric and is able to sway even the most skeptical of audiences to his cause.
- He is a powerful warrior and a skilled magic-user, but he only uses his magic to hunt down and destroy other magic-users.
