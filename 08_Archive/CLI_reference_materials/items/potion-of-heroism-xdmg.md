# Potion of Heroism
*Potion, rare*  


- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you gain 10 [Temporary Hit Points](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) that last for 1 hour. This potion's blue liquid bubbles and steams as if boiling.
