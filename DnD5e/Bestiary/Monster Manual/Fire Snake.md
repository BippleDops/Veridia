## Special Traits
***Heated Body.*** A creature that touches the snake or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 ft. of it takes 3 (`dice: 1d6`) fire damage.

## Actions
***Multiattack.*** The snake makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its tail.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (`dice: 1d4+1`) piercing damage plus 3 (`dice: 1d6`) fire damage.

***Tail.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (`dice: 1d4+1`) bludgeoning damage plus 3 (`dice: 1d6`) fire damage.

## Description
***Fire Snakes.*** [[Salamander|Salamanders]] hatch from eggs that are two-foot-diameter spheres of smoldering obsidian. When a [[Salamander|salamander]] is ready to hatch, it melts its way through the egg's thick shell and emerges as a fire snake. A fire snake matures into a [[Salamander|salamander]] adult within a year.
