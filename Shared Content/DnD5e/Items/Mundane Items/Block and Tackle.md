# Block and Tackle

## Overview
**Price**: 1 gp

**Weight**: 5 lb.

**Block and Tackle.** A set of pulleys with a cable threaded through them and a hook to attach to [[11 - Objects|objects]], a block and tackle allows you to hoist up to four times the weight you can normally [[Strength|lift]].
