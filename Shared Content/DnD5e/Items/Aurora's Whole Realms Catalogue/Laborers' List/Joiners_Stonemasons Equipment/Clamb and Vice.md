## Description
When a joiner or bricksman needs an additional set of hands, our clamps and vices can be invaluable. We supply clamps that can hold up to 4" (2 gp), 8" (5 gp), and 16" (10 gp), as well as a 10" tablemounted vice (20 gp) for securing heavy materials.
