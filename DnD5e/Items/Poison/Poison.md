# Poison

## Overview

Given their insidious and deadly nature, poisons are illegal in most societies but are a favorite tool among assassins, draw, and other evil creatures. Poisons come in four types.

## **PURCHASING [[Poisoned|POISON]]**

In some settings, strict laws prohibit the possession and use of [[Poisoned|poison]], but a black-market dealer or unscrupulous apothecary might keep a hidden stash. Characters with criminal contacts might be able to acquire [[Poisoned|poison]] relatively easily. Other characters might have to make extensive inquiries and pay bribes before they track down the [[Poisoned|poison]] they seek.

The Poisons table gives suggested prices for single doses of various poisons.

## **CRAFTING AND HARVESTING [[Poisoned|POISON]]**

During downtime [[7 - Between Adventures|between adventures]], a character can use the crafting rules in the *Player's Handbook* to create [[Basic Poison|basic poison]] if the character has proficiency with a [[Poisoner's kit|poisoner's kit]] At your discretion, the character can craft other kinds of [[Poisoned|poison]]. Not all [[Poisoned|poison]] ingredients are available for purchase, and tracking down certain ingredients might form the basis of an entire adventure.

A character can instead attempt to harvest [[Poisoned|poison]] from a poisonous creature, such as a [[Snake|snake]], [[Wyvern|wyvern]], or [[Carrion Crawler|carrion crawler]]. The creature must be [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] or dead, and the harvesting requires `dice: 1d6` minutes followed by a DC 20 Intelligence (Nature) check. (Proficiency with the [[Poisoner's kit|poisoner's kit]] applies to this check if the character doesn't have proficiency in Nature.) On a successful check, the character harvests enough [[Poisoned|poison]] for a single dose. On a failed check, the character is unable to extract any [[Poisoned|poison]]. If the character fails the check by 5 or more, the character is subjected to the creature's [[Poisoned|poison]].
