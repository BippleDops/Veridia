# +1 Rod of the Pact Keeper
*Rod, uncommon (requires attunement by a warlock)*  

- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

While holding this rod, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your Warlock spells.

In addition, you can regain one spell slot as a [[actions#Magic|Magic]] action while holding the rod. You can't use this property again until you finish a [[long-rest-xphb]].

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 301*
