## Description
These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. While wearing them, you have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Wisdom|Wisdom]] ([[Wisdom|Perception]]) checks that rely on sight. In conditions of clear visibility, you can make out details of even extremely distant creatures and [[11 - Objects|objects]] as small as 2 feet across.
