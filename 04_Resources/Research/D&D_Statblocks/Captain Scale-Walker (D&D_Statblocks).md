Captain Scale-Walker is a weathered lizardfolk mariner who commands respect in the deepest waters of [[Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos. Born in the twilight depths where light barely penetrates, he has spent decades mapping the treacherous passages between the surface waters and the abyssal plains.
