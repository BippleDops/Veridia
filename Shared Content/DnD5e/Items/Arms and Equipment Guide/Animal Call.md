# Animal Call
## Overview
Animal Call: Crafted out of bone, metal, or wood, these special whistles are designed to [[Mimic|mimic]] a specific animal voice—usually game animals and birds. An animal call provides a +1 circumstance bonus on Wilderness Lore checks when hunting for food. Calls exist only for creatures that are type “animal.”

**Cost:** 20gp

**Weight:** -
