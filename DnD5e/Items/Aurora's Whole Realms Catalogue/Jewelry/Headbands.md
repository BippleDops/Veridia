## Description
I am pleased to offer two styles of headbands: one suitable for men, the other for women. The men’s style is engraved with zoomorphic designs popular in the Moonshaes, such as birds and deer. The women’s style is buffed to a beautiful matte finish, and set with a single cabochon aquamarine. Fine chains depend from the sides of this headband, to frame the face with glittering strands. Men’s headband: 25 gp. Women’s headband: 40 gp.
