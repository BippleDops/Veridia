## Description
With all the clarity and beauty of a common [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Bards' Emporium/Common Strings/Dulcimer|dulcimer]] and the flexibility of a [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Bards' Emporium/Common Strings/Yarting|yarting]], the zither is fast becoming a fixture in the streets and courts of the land. Its lower strings are fretted to allow the left hand to stopper them, and the upper strings, tuned in fourths, can be plucked by the right thumb. The resulting music suits rigorous peasant dances as well as recitation of ancient lore. (65 gp)
