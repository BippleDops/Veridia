# Astral Awareness (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (30-foot radius)

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 6 hours


## Effects
This spell grants you awareness of nearby movement on the Astral or Ethereal plane. Should any creature or “natural” phenomena from those planes approach you, this spell will alert you.
