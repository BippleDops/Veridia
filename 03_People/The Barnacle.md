**Symbiotic Host.** Crust's body hosts dozens of living organisms. As a bonus action, he can activate one of the following symbiotic abilities (3/day each):
- **Healing Polyps**: Regain 15 hit points
- **Toxic Barnacles**: Next melee attack deals additional `dice: `dice: 2d6`` poison damage

**Living Armor.** Crust's natural armor is composed of living barnacles, coral, and other sea creatures. When he takes damage, roll a d6. On 5-6, the armor absorbs the damage and he takes none.

**Depth Adaptation.** Crust suffers no penalties from water pressure at any depth and can see clearly in the deepest ocean trenches.

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Crust makes two attacks: one with his Symbiotic Strike and one with either his Spine Launcher or a spell.

**Symbiotic Strike.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (`dice: `dice: 2d8`` + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (`dice: `dice: 2d6``) acid damage from barnacle secretions.

**Spine Launcher.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (`dice: `dice: 2d6`` + 1) piercing damage. The target must succeed on a **DC 16** Constitution save or be paralyzed for 1 minute (save ends).

**Colony Burst (1/Day).** Crust releases a swarm of symbiotic creatures in a 20-foot radius. All creatures in the area must make a **DC 16** Dexterity saving throw. On failure, they take 21 (`dice: `dice: 6d6``) piercing damage and are restrained by clinging organisms for 1 minute. On success, half damage and no restraint.

**Merge with Environment (1/Day).** Crust becomes one with the surrounding coral, rock, or ship hull for up to 1 hour. He gains resistance to all damage types and can move through solid objects, but cannot attack or cast spells.

## Description

Crust Deepmeld represents the ultimate evolution of symbiotic adaptation in Aquabyssos's deepest trenches. What was once a typical merfolk has become a living ecosystem, his body serving as host to hundreds of different sea creatures that have formed a permanent, mutually beneficial relationship.

Standing 5'8" but appearing much bulkier due to his living armor, Crust's original merfolk features are barely visible beneath layers of barnacles, coral, sea anemones, and other organisms. His skin has taken on a mottled gray-green hue, and bioluminescent patches pulse rhythmically across his form. Most striking are his eyes, which have adapted to complete darkness and now glow with an eerie blue light.

His movements are deliberate and somewhat mechanical, as if coordinating the actions of his many symbiotic partners requires conscious effort. When he speaks, his voice carries harmonic undertones from the creatures living within his throat and chest cavity.

Crust dwells in the Symbiosis Depths, an area of the deep ocean where the boundaries between individual organisms have broken down entirely. Here, entire ecosystems exist as single entities, and the concept of "self" becomes fluid and negotiable.
