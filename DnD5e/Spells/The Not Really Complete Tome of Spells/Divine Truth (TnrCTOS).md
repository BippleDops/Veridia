# Divine Truth (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *8th-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Casting Time**: 10 minutes

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 round


## Effects
You ask your god, patron, or the universe at large a question and make a DC 18 spellcasting ability check. On a success, you learn the utter truth of the matter. This can-not be a question about the future; it can only concern something that has happened or is currently happening.
