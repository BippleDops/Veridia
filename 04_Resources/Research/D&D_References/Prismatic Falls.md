Prismatic Falls represents a unique underwater phenomenon where concentrated beams of surface sunlight, filtered and concentrated through a series of natural crystal formations, create cascading waterfalls of pure magical light that flow downward through the ocean depths. These luminous cascades appear as streams of liquid rainbow that maintain their coherent form despite the surrounding water, creating one of the most spectacular and magically significant locations in Aquabyssos. ## Historical Background

Discovered during early deep-sea expeditions, Prismatic Falls initially defied explanation. Surface scholars theorized it was impossible, while underwater mages recognized it as evidence of the deep ocean's capacity to concentrate and transform magical energy. The falls became a pilgrimage site for light-based magic practitioners. ## Current State

The falls now serve multiple purposes: religious site, magical research location, and strategic landmark for underwater navigation. Recent dimensional instabilities have caused the falls' patterns to shift unpredictably, creating both opportunities and hazards for visitors. ### Aquabyssos

Considered sacred by followers of light-based deities, the falls are protected by religious orders and studied by magical researchers. The shifting patterns are interpreted as omens by underwater fortune-tellers. ### Aethermoor
