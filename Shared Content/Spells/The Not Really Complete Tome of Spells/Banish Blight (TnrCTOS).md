# Banish Blight (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *2nd-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You touch a single normal plant of Huge size or smaller and this spell wipes out all diseases in that plant permanently, restores any withered or shriveled fruit or leaves, and if you desire, causes it to produce new leaves or flowers. It does not restore heavily dam-aged or destroyed plants.
