# Fist of Stone (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
One of your hands turns into flexible stone. Although you retain your sense of touch, that hand does not feel pain. You have a Strength of 16 with that hand (only). Un-armed attacks do 1d4+2 bludgeoning dam-age.
