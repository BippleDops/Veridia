# Charm of Restoration

This Charm has 3 charges. You can expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells: [[greater-restoration-xphb|Greater Restoration]] (2 charges) or [[lesser-restoration-xphb|Lesser Restoration]] (1 charge). Once all its charges have been expended, the Charm vanishes from you.
