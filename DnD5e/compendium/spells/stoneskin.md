# Stoneskin
*4th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, diamond dust worth 100 gp, which the spell consumes
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

This spell turns the flesh of a willing creature you touch as hard as stone. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

**Classes**: Artificer, Artificer (Revisited), Cleric (Strength (PSA)), Cleric (War), Druid, Fighter (Eldritch Knight), Monk (Four Elements), Paladin (Ancients), Paladin (Conquest), Paladin (Redemption), Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

Source: Player's Handbook p. 278
