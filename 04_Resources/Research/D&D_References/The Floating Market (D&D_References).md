The Floating Market exists in a carefully maintained dimensional pocket that touches both [[Aethermoor|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor and [Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos]] while belonging fully to neither. The Floating Market exists in a carefully constructed pocket dimension that maintains physical contact points with both [Aethermoor]] and [[Aquabyssos. The market doesn't truly "move" in a conventional sense, but rather shifts its access points between the realms on a precisely timed schedule. #### Movement Schedule

- Both realms have full portal access
- Peak trading hours with maximum merchant activity
- Cultural exchange programs most active
- Neutral diplomatic meetings scheduled **Evening Phase (6 hours)**: Primary access to [Aquabyssos]]
- Major portal opens to [[The Memories Nexus
