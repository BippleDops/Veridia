In the floating realm of Aethermoor, enchantment magic takes on a musical quality, resonating through the crystalline structures that support the sky cities. [[02_Worldbuilding have mastered techniques where enchantments are literally sung into existence, their spells harmonizing with the natural frequencies of the [Seven Shards]].
