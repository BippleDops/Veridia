The Drowned Embassy stands partially submerged in the perpetual twilight waters of [[The Shadowmere|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Shadowmere, serving as [Aquabyssos]]'s official diplomatic presence in Aethermoor. Half above water, half below, the building exists in two worlds simultaneously - much like its inhabitants. ## Description

The Embassy appears as a once-grand marble structure that seems to have been pulled partially into the swamp. The upper floors maintain an illusion of normalcy with dry meeting rooms and offices, while the lower levels are completely flooded, accessible only to those who can breathe water. Strange bioluminescent coral grows along the waterline, pulsing with an rhythm that matches the tides of distant seas. ### The Tide Pools
