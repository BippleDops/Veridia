# Descenders
## Overview
**Price**: 15 gp each.

**Weight**: -

### Descenders (Pic)
![[le2vxtro.c4l.png|Descenders (Pic)]]
[[le2vxtro.c4l.png|open outside]]

## Description
These are also called brake bars, and are used to halt a person who is sliding down a rope. The rope is woven into five or six crossbars, and adjustments to the level of friction can be controlled during descent. 15 gp each.
