# Essence of Ether
## Overview
*Essence of Ether (Inhaled).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 8 hours. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature is [[Unconscious|unconscious]]. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
