# Millbrook

## General Info
**Location**: North of [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/Shadowhaven.md]]

## Description
Millbrook is a small farming community that is known for its high-quality grain and its delicious apple cider.

## GM Notes
- The people of Millbrook are simple, hardworking folk who are wary of outsiders.
- The town is a good place to stock up on supplies before heading into the wilderness.
- There is a rumor of a hidden temple in the nearby hills.
