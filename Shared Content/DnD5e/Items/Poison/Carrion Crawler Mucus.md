# Carrion Crawler Mucus
## Overview
*[[Carrion Crawler]]** Mucus (Contact).* This [[Poisoned|poison]] must be harvested from a dead or [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] [[Carrion Crawler|carrion crawler]]. A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 minute. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature is [[Paralyzed|paralyzed]]. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
