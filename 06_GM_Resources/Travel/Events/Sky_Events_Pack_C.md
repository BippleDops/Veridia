# Sky Events Pack C — 50 Ready-to-Run Encounters (S-201–S-250)

Complements Packs A and B. Use with core systems and vehicle rules.

### S-201: Eclipse Raid Window
- Band: High • Category: Reality/Stealth
- Trigger: Eclipse lowers detection.
- Checks: Stealth DC 16; Anchorite DC 15 to keep Integrity.
- Outcomes: Success: Undetected passage; boon. Partial: Minor notice. Fail: Ambush by eclipse raiders.

### S-202: Gravity Tide Surge
- Band: Mid • Category: Physics
- Trigger: Seasonal gravity tide.
- Checks: Helm DC 16 ride; Engineer DC 15 damp.
- Outcomes: Success: Time -25%. Partial: -1 Maneuver 1h. Fail: Stall and 2d6.

### S-203: Sky Abbey Schism
- Band: Any • Category: Social/Religious
- Trigger: Monks split doctrine.
- Checks: Persuasion DC 15; Sanity DC 14 vs. void-visions.
- Outcomes: Success: Standing +1; Sanity +1. Partial: Neutral. Fail: Short-term madness; minor riot.

### S-204: War-Balloon Bombardment
- Band: Low • Category: Combat/Terrain
- Trigger: Bombardment over city.
- Checks: Tactics DC 16; Gunnery DC 15 intercept; Helm DC 15 evasive.
- Outcomes: Success: Civilians saved; Faction +2. Partial: Damage 1d6. Fail: 2d6; scandal risk.

### S-205: Stratos Kite Sabotage
- Band: Stratos • Category: Sabotage
- Trigger: Saboteurs cut kite array.
- Checks: Perception DC 15; Thieves’ Tools DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Prevented; reward 200–600 gp. Partial: Partial cut; -10 kn 1h. Fail: Array tear; 2d6.

### S-206: Platform Quarantine
