# Comfort (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *abjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 hour


## Effects
You sing a pleasant song and all allies within range will cease feeling nonmagically-caused pain, heat, cold, hunger, thirst, or ex-haustion. This spell does not negate normal damage, but affected creatures have ad-vantage on all saving throws against magical effects that cause pain, negatively affect emo-tions (such as by causing fright), or that cause hunger, thirst, or exhaustion.
