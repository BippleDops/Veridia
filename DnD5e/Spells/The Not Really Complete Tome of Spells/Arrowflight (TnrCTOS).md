# Arrowflight (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a bit of down from a flying bird)

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
You touch a bow or crossbow. For the duration of this spell, the range for this weapon is doubled.

***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you may affect one additional bow or cross-bow for each slot level above 1st.
