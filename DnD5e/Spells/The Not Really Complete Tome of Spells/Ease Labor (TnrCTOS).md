# Ease Labor (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M

**Duration**: Special


## Effects
You touch a female creature in labor and ease the pain she is feeling for as long as you remain in physical contact with her.
