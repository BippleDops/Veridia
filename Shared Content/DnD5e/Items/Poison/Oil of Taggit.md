## Becoming Afflicted
*Contact*

## Effects
A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 24 hours. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature is [[Unconscious|unconscious]].

## Prevention and Recovery
The creature wakes up if it takes damage.
