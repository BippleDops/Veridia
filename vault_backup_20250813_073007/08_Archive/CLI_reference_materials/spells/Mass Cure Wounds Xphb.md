# Mass Cure Wounds
*5th-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes out from a point you can see within range. Choose up to six creatures in a 30-foot-radius [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb|Sphere]] centered on that point. Each target regains [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb|Hit Points]] equal to `5d8` plus your spellcasting ability modifier.
