# Mass Contagion (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *8th-level*

**School**: *necromancy*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 60 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 7 days


## Effects
You inflict a horrible disease on up to 12 creatures you can see within range. Each target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it contracts a disease of your choice described in the *contagion* spell.
