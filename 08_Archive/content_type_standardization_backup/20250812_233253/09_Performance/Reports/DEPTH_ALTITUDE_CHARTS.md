*Standardized Elevation and Pressure Reference*

## Aethermoor Altitude Zones

### Sky Cities (8,000-15,000 feet)
**Environmental Conditions**:
- **Temperature**: -10°F to 20°F
- **Atmosphere**: 0.5-0.7 standard pressure
- **Weather**: Crystal storms, high winds
- **Magic**: Enhanced crystal resonance
- **Travel**: Airship required, flying mounts

**Locations**:
- **Crystal Peaks** (12,000+ ft): Pure crystal sources
- **Sky Fortress Valdris** (10,000 ft): Military outpost
- **Resonance Towers** (8,000-15,000 ft): Communication network

**Survival Requirements**:
- Cold weather gear mandatory
- Altitude sickness at 10,000+ ft
- Crystal exposure protection

### Highland Settlements (3,000-8,000 feet)
**Environmental Conditions**:
- **Temperature**: 20°F to 50°F
- **Atmosphere**: 0.7-0.9 standard pressure
- **Weather**: Mountain weather, occasional crystal storms
- **Magic**: Strong crystal influence
- **Travel**: Mountain paths, short-range flight

**Major Locations**:
- **Ironhold** (3,000-8,000 ft): Dwarven stronghold, Pop: 280,000
- **Crystalhaven** (3,000 ft): Capital city, Pop: 45,000
- **Mining Outposts** (4,000-6,000 ft): Crystal extraction sites

**Survival Requirements**:
- Warm clothing
- Mountain climbing gear
- Crystal corruption monitoring

### Lowland Cities (0-3,000 feet)
**Environmental Conditions**:
- **Temperature**: 40°F to 80°F (seasonal)
- **Atmosphere**: Standard pressure
- **Weather**: Temperate, influenced by crystal fields
- **Magic**: Moderate crystal influence
- **Travel**: Roads, rivers, short flights

**Major Locations**:
- **Port Meridian** (Sea level): Major port, Pop: 85,000
- **Lumengarde** (2,000 ft): Regional capital, Pop: 45,000
- **Sylvanheart** (500-1,500 ft): Elven city, Pop: 180,000

**Standard Conditions**: Most adventure activity occurs here

## Pressure Adaptation Stages

### Stage 0: Surface Dweller
- **Depth Limit**: 100 feet
- **Time Limit**: 1 hour
- **Equipment**: Basic diving gear
- **Risk**: Drowning, pressure sickness

### Stage 1: Basic Adaptation
- **Depth Limit**: 500 feet
- **Time Limit**: 8 hours
- **Changes**: Rudimentary gills, enhanced lung capacity
- **Equipment**: Pressure stabilization gear

### Stage 2: Intermediate Adaptation  
- **Depth Limit**: 2,000 feet
- **Time Limit**: Indefinite
- **Changes**: Functional gills, bone density increase, enhanced circulation
- **Equipment**: Pressure suit for deeper excursions

### Stage 3: Advanced Adaptation
- **Depth Limit**: 6,000 feet
- **Time Limit**: Indefinite
- **Changes**: Full aquatic respiratory system, pressure-resistant organs
- **Equipment**: Minimal technological support

### Stage 4: Complete Adaptation
- **Depth Limit**: 10,000+ feet
- **Time Limit**: Indefinite
- **Changes**: Fundamental physiological transformation
- **Limitation**: Cannot survive surface pressure without aid

### Stage 5: Transcendent Form
- **Depth Limit**: Unlimited
- **Time Limit**: Indefinite
- **Changes**: Post-human aquatic entity
- **Cost**: Loss of original species identity

## Environmental Hazards by Zone

### High Altitude (Aethermoor 8,000+ ft)
- **Crystal Storms**: Magical weather (DC 15 CON save or crystallization)
- **Altitude Sickness**: DC 12 CON save above 10,000 ft
- **Temperature**: Cold damage without protection
- **Thin Air**: Exhaustion on exertion

### Deep Ocean (Aquabyssos 4,000+ ft)
- **Pressure Crush**: DC 16 CON save or 2d6 damage per 10 minutes
- **Depth Madness**: WIS saves against psychological pressure
- **Temperature**: Cold damage in deep waters
- **Decompression**: Rapid ascent causes death

### Transition Zones (Dimensional Overlap)
- **Reality Flux**: Random magical effects
- **Temporal Displacement**: Time moves differently
- **Pressure Variance**: Sudden environmental changes
- **Identity Confusion**: Memory and self-awareness issues
