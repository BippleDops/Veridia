The Waking Prophets believe that sleep is a curse that separates consciousness from divine truth. This ascetic order practices extreme sleep deprivation, claiming that exhaustion-induced visions reveal authentic prophecy. Members undergo rigorous training to function without sleep while maintaining prophetic clarity. ## Practices and Rituals

**The Vigil:** New members stay awake for 72 consecutive hours
**Stimulant Ceremonies:** Consumption of magical alertness enhancers
**Prophecy Sessions:** Group meditation during extreme exhaustion
**Sleep Denial:** Advanced members sleep only 1-2 hours per week
**Vision Recording:** Detailed documentation of exhaustion-induced revelations ## Hierarchy

**The Sleepless One:** Leader who hasn't slept in over five years
**Senior Prophets:** Members maintaining wakefulness for months
**Vision Keepers:** Specialists recording and interpreting prophecies
**Initiates:** New members learning sleep denial techniques
**The Fallen:** Former members who succumbed to sleep ## Prophectic Accuracy
