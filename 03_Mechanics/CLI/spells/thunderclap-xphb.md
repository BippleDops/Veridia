# Thunderclap
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (5-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Each creature in a 5-foot [Emanation](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take `d6` Thunder damage. The spell's thunderous sound can be heard up to 100 feet away.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `d6` when you reach levels 5 (`2d6`), 11 (`3d6`), and 17 (`4d6`).
