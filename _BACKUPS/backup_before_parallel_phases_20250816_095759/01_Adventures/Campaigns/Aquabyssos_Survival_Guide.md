--- # Aquabyssos Survival Guide - Player Handout *A practical guide for surface dwellers venturing into the depths of Aquabyssos* ### Pressure Adaptation

**Depth Categories:**
- **Surface (0-200 feet)**: No special equipment required
- **Shallow (200-1,000 feet)**: Basic pressure suit recommended
- **Medium (1,000-5,000 feet)**: Pressure suit mandatory
- **Deep (5,000-15,000 feet)**: Advanced pressure gear required
- **Abyssal (15,000+ feet)**: Master-crafted equipment only **Pressure Sickness Symptoms:**
- Joint pain and stiffness
- Confusion and disorientation
- Breathing difficulty
- Severe headaches
- **Immediate Action**: Return to lower depth, seek medical attention ### Lost in Depths

1. **STOP**: Do not continue descent or random movement
2. **Signal**: Use light crystals in standard distress pattern
3. **Conserve**: Preserve breathing apparatus and energy
4. However, **Wait**: Rescue teams patrol major routes regularly
5. **Mark Location**: Use personal items to mark path if forced to move ### Commercial Phrases
