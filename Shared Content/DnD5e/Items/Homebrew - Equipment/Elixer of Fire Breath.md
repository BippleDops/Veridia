# Elixer of Fire Breath
## Overview
**Elixir of Fire Breath:** This strange elixir bestows upon the drinker the ability to spit gouts of flame. He can breathe fire up to three times, each time dealing `dice: 4d6` points of fire damage to a single target up to 25 feet away. The victim can attempt a [DC](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:DC) 13 Dexterity save for half damage. Unused blasts dissipate 1 hour after the liquid is consumed.

Moderate [[Evocation|evocation]]; CL 11th; [Craft Wondrous Item](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:Craft_Wondrous_Item), *[scorching ray](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:Scorching_Ray)*; Price 1,100 gp.
