## Description
As ancient as faith itself, [[Braziers|braziers]] serve the trifold role of providing heat, supplying light, and consuming [[Incense|incense]] and other aromatics cast into them. We offer traditional 6-foot-diameter temple [[Braziers|braziers]] and 4-foot-diameter chapel [[Braziers|braziers]], as well as field [[Braziers|braziers]], which are 18 inches in diameter. Though not ornate like the large and mid-sized [[Braziers|braziers]], the field [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Priests' Alcove/Brazier|brazier]] can be easily strapped to a pack and even double as a buckler for fighting priests.

**Large:** gold—110 gp, silver—70 gp, bronze—30 gp

**Medium:** gold—70, silver—30 gp, bronze—17 gp

**Field:** silver—15 gp; bronze—4 gp.

## Additional Details
When used provides 10% reduction in spell failure percentage for spells using [[Incense|incense]]; can function in all ways as a buckler.
