## Description
“Beneath robes of kings and yokes of slaves there lies a [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Clothes and Shoes/Overclothes/Chemise|chemise]].” Standard apparel from the rich down to the poor, the [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Clothes and Shoes/Overclothes/Chemise|chemise]] holds universal sway throughout the Heartlands. All our chemises are attractive and durable enough to be worn as an overgarment, as the peasantry prefers, and comfortable enough to serve as a nobleman’s undergarment. Prices for an elf-size [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Clothes and Shoes/Overclothes/Chemise|chemise]] vary according to material: sackcloth (8 sp); linen (2 gp); Maztican cotton (3 gp); silk (6 gp). Comes only in white, beige, or gray.
