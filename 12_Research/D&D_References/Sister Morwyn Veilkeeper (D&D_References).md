A enigmatic member of the Order of the Shattered Crown who serves as the order's primary researcher into the Shadow Shard's properties. Sister Morwyn operates from a hidden sanctum within [[The Shadowmere|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Shadowmere]], where the boundary between reality and shadow grows thin.
