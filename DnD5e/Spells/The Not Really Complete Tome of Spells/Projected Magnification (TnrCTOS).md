# Projected Magnification (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *4th-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 10 miles

**Components**: V, S, M (a shard of glass)

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes


## Effects
You pick a distant object or place you can see or are aware of that is within range. An image of that object or place is then projected onto a flat surface or in the air within 10 feet of you.
