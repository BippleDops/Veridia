# Vial of Stardust
*Other, unknown*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

Any creature that sprinkles the contents of a Vial of Stardust over itself gains the ability to cast the [dream](/compendium/spells/dream.md) spell once as an action (spell save DC 15), requiring no components.

*Source: Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage p. 251*
