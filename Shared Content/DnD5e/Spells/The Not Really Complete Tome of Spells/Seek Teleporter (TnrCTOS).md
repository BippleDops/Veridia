# Seek Teleporter (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Casting Time**: 1 reaction, which you take when someone you can see has teleported away.

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You discern the destination of another individual who used spells such as *teleport, dimension door,* or *arcane gate*.
