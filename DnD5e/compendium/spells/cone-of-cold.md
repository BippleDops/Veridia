# Cone of Cold
*5th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (60-feet cone)
- **Components:** V, S, a small crystal or glass cone
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A blast of cold air erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 8d8 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws.

**Classes**: Artificer (Artillerist), Druid, Fighter (Eldritch Knight), Monk (Four Elements), Sorcerer, Warlock (Fathomless), Warlock (Genie), Warlock (Hexblade), Wizard

Source: Player's Handbook p. 224
