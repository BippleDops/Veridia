# Crystal Festival

*"Where light dances through crystal, two worlds become one."*

## Overview
The Crystal Festival is the premier annual celebration uniting both Aquabyssos and Aethermoor in a spectacular display of crystalline artistry, magical demonstrations, and inter-realm cultural exchange. Held simultaneously across multiple locations, the festival showcases the beauty and power of crystals while fostering understanding between surface and underwater civilizations.

## Mechanics
### Requirements
- Coordination between both realm governments
- Extensive security arrangements for mixed populations
- Crystal artist participation from both realms
- Magical infrastructure for simultaneous celebrations

### Effects
- **Cultural Unity**: Promotes understanding between realms
- **Economic Boost**: Major increase in trade and tourism
- **Artistic Innovation**: Showcases new crystal-working techniques
- **Diplomatic Opportunity**: Facilitates political negotiations

### Duration
Week-long celebration held annually during optimal magical alignment.

## Adventure Hooks
1. **Festival Security**: Protect important guests during celebrations
2. **Crystal Competition**: Participate in artistic or magical contests
3. **Diplomatic Crisis**: Prevent incident that could damage relations

## Campaign Use
Provides colorful setting for social encounters, cultural exploration, and diplomatic adventures.
