# Book

## Overview
**Price**: 25 gp

**Weight**: 5 lb.

**Book.** A book might contain poetry, historical accounts, information pertaining to a particular field of lore, diagrams and notes on gnomish contraptions, or just about anything else that can be represented using text or pictures. A book of spells is a [[Spellbook|spellbook]].
