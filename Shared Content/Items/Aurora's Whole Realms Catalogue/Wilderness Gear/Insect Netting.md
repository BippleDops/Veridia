## Description
The jungles of Chult are teeming with biting insects, but it’s too hot to sleep in a [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Wilderness Gear/Bedroll|bedroll]] in a tent. Use this material to set up a canopy to protect yourself from constant aggravation, without losing the benefit of the breeze. The net is made of silk, so it’s very light in your pack. 300 gp for a section 10' by 20'.
