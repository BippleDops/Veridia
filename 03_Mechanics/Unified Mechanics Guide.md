## Core Difficulty Classes

### Standard DC Progression
All systems use this unified progression:

| Difficulty | DC | When to Use |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| **Trivial** | 5 | Routine tasks with tools/expertise |
| **Easy** | 10 | Basic challenges, favorable conditions |
| **Moderate** | 13 | Standard difficulty, normal conditions |
| **Hard** | 16 | Challenging tasks, adverse conditions |
| **Very Hard** | 20 | Extremely difficult, multiple complications |
| **Legendary** | 25 | Near-impossible, world-class difficulty |
| **Mythic** | 30 | Beyond mortal capability |

### Environmental DC Modifiers
Apply these modifiers to base DCs:

| Condition | Modifier | Examples |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| **Ideal conditions** | -2 | Perfect tools, expert guidance, calm weather |
| **Favorable conditions** | -1 | Good tools, clear instructions, mild weather |
| **Normal conditions** | +0 | Standard equipment, basic knowledge |
| **Adverse conditions** | +2 | Poor tools, distractions, rough weather |
| **Extreme conditions** | +5 | No tools, hostile environment, time pressure |
| **Impossible conditions** | +10 | Actively opposed, catastrophic environment |

## Unified Pressure System

### Depth Zones & Consistent Effects
| Depth Range | Zone Name | Base Pressure DC | Adaptation Rate | Environmental |
|-------------|-----------|------------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **0-500 ft** | Surface Zone | 10 | 1 AP/week | Normal visibility |
| **501-1,500 ft** | Twilight Zone | 13 | 1 AP/day | Dim light, -2 Perception |
| **1,501-4,000 ft** | Pressure Zone | 16 | 1 AP/4 hours | Darkness, crushing silence |
| **4,001-10,000 ft** | Crush Zone | 20 | 1 AP/hour | Bioluminescence, reality flux |
| **10,001-20,000 ft** | Hadal Zone | 25 | 2 AP/hour | Memory tides, time distortion |
| **20,001+ ft** | Void Zone | 30 | 5 AP/hour | Complete unreality |

### Pressure Damage (Standardized)
| Failed Saves | Effect | Recovery Time |
|--------------|--------|---------------|
| **1st failure** | 1 exhaustion level | Short rest |
| **2nd failure** | 1d6 damage + 1 AP | Long rest |
| **3rd failure** | 2d6 damage + stunned 1 round | 24 hours |
| **4th failure** | 3d6 damage + unconscious | 1 week |
| **5th failure** | Death (pressure implosion) | Resurrection |

### Adaptation Point System (Unified)
| Adaptation Points | Stage | Benefits | Drawbacks |
|-------------------|-------|----------|-----------|
| **0-10** | Surface Normal | None | None |
| **11-25** | Shallow Adapted | Water breathing 1hr/day, +5 swim speed | Sunlight sensitivity |
| **26-50** | Depth Walker | Permanent water breathing, darkvision 60ft | Must submerge every 4 hours |
| **51-75** | Pressure Born | Resistance to pressure damage, telepathy 30ft | Cannot breathe air |
| **76-99** | Abyss Touched | Immunity to pressure, regeneration underwater | Alien appearance, -4 CHA |
| **100+** | Deep Merged | Transcendent abilities | NPC control, surface = death |

## Shadow Separation Rules

### Shadow Autonomy Progression
| Autonomy Level | Behavior | Control DC | Manifestation |
|----------------|----------|------------|---------------|
| **0** | Normal shadow | None | Standard shadow |
| **1** | Delayed mimicry | 10 | 1-second delay |
| **2** | Different gestures | 12 | Wrong movements |
| **3** | Independent action | 15 | Acts without owner |
| **4** | Communication | 18 | Whispers, gestures |
| **5** | Opposition | 20 | Actively resists |
| **6** | Full separation | 25 | Physical independence |

### Shadow Separation Triggers
- High stress or fear (WIS save DC 15)
- Adaptation points > 50 (daily check)
- Deep Mother influence > 30 (weekly check)
- Reality merger exposure (automatic check)
- Memory tide contact (immediate check)

## Sanity & Madness Framework

### Unified Sanity System
**Starting Sanity**: 10 + Wisdom modifier + level
**Maximum Sanity**: 20 (can be exceeded through special means)
**Minimum Sanity**: 0 (complete madness)

### Sanity Loss Events (Standardized)
| Event Type | Sanity Loss | Save DC |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **Minor horror** | 1 point | 10 |
| **Moderate horror** | 1d4 points | 13 |
| **Major horror** | 1d6 points | 16 |
| **Extreme horror** | 2d6 points | 20 |
| **Cosmic horror** | 3d6 points | 25 |

### Madness Thresholds
| Sanity Range | Mental State | Mechanical Effect |
|--------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **16-20** | Stable | No penalties |
| **11-15** | Unsettled | Disadvantage on social checks with normal people |
| **6-10** | Unstable | Disadvantage on Concentration saves |
| **3-5** | Fragmented | Daily mental state roll |
| **1-2** | Shattered | 25% chance to act in different reality |
| **0** | Transcended/Lost | NPC control |

## Combat Modifiers

### Underwater Combat (Standardized)
| Attack Type | Modifier | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| **Melee (slashing/bludgeoning)** | Disadvantage | Unless adapted or special weapon |
| **Melee (piercing)** | Normal | Works normally underwater |
| **Ranged (bows)** | Impossible | Beyond 20 feet |
| **Ranged (crossbows)** | Disadvantage, half range | Heavy bolts only |
| **Ranged (thrown)** | Disadvantage, quarter range | Javelins and spears work best |
| **Spells** | Normal | Unless specified otherwise |

| **Deep** | 1.5× walking speed | -20 feet | Water breathing |
