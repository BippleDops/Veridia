# M-L-05 The Dream Sovereign

## Read-Aloud
> Sleep leans over the worlds with a mother’s patience and a god’s hunger.

## Setup
- Global dream incursion; entity claims sovereignty via sleep

## Mechanics
- Group Sanity DC 18 endure dreaming
- Religion DC 18 parley/bargain or banish
- Optional: Memory sacrifice for leverage

## Outcomes
- Success: Bargain or banish; gain unique boon/curse
- Partial: Stalled reign; recurring dream hooks
- Fail: Reign of dreams; day-night inversion in zones

## Integrations
- [[Deep Mother]]; Sanity; Memory economy

## Quick-Run
- Sanity→Religion→decide bargain/banish; set world tag
