***Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The dragon exhales fire in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 56 (`dice: 16d6`) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
