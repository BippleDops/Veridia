## Description
These sachets combine spices, herbs, and flowers from all lands— Shou Lung, Kara-Tur, the Heartlands, Maztica—to add fine scents to your clothing. Specify desired scent: Celestial Seaweed, Shaar Scents, Moonsea, Chessentan Lotus, or Far Payit Palm. (3 gp)
