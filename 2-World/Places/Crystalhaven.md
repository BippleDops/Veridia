# Crystalhaven
*The Crystal Throne, Heart of Aethermoor*

## Overview
Crystalhaven stands as the shining jewel of [[2-World/Places/Aethermoor]], a city where crystal spires reach toward the heavens and aether-powered technology lights every street. Built around the largest Aether Crystal formation on the continent, the city serves as the seat of power for [[2-World/People/Queen Seraphina Lumengarde]] and the center of crystal innovation.

## Districts

### The Radiant Quarter
- **The Crystal Palace** - Home to [[2-World/People/Queen Seraphina Lumengarde]]
- **The Hall of Resonance** - Parliament building
- **Embassy Row** - Foreign dignitaries and [[2-World/People/Prince Caspian Duskwater]]'s former residence

### The Forge District
- **[[The Crystalline Foundry]]** - Largest crystal refinement facility
- **Artificer's Row** - Magical item creation
- **The Aether Works** - Run by [[2-World/People/Master Artificer Korvin Gearwright]]

### The Scholar's Sanctum
- **The Grand Archive** - Overseen by [[Keeper Elara Moonwhisper]]
- **The Crystal Academy** - Where [[Professor Aldric Crystalweaver]] teaches
- **The Observatory** - Studies crystal formations

### The Market Spiral
- **[[The Gilded Shard]]** - Upscale inn run by [[Madame Rosalind Goldleaf]]
- **Crystal Exchange** - Controlled by [[Merchant Lord Silas Copperfield]]
- **The Singing Crystal Tavern** - Information hub, owned by [[Jasper "Three-Eyes" Flint]]

### The Shadow Warren
- **The Undercity** - Controlled by [[2-World/Groups/The Whisper Syndicate]]
- **Black Shard Market** - Illegal crystal trade
- **The Corroded Crown** - Tavern frequented by [[2-World/People/Vex Shadowthorn]]'s agents

## Notable Locations

### The Crystal Throne
Massive throne carved from a single Aether Crystal, slowly corrupting anyone who sits upon it. Currently occupied by [[2-World/People/Queen Seraphina Lumengarde]], who uses illusion magic to hide her advancing corruption.

### The Resonance Spire
Tallest structure in the city, used for long-distance magical communication. Maintained by [[The Crystal Wardens]].

### The Purification Gardens
Where those suffering from crystal corruption seek healing. Run by [[High Priestess Celeste Dawnbringer]].
