# Nectar (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *conjuration*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 round


## Effects
You “milk” a living flower and squeeze out up to a quart of nectar. This nectar is mildly alcoholic and tastes of the plant from which it was produced, and is generally con-sidered to be on par with decent wine.

The nectar can be bottled; it will last for a week before spoiling.
