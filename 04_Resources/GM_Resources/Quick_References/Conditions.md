# Condition Reference

## Conditions
- **Blinded**: Can't see
- **Charmed**: Can't attack charmer
- **Deafened**: Can't hear
- **Frightened**: Disadvantage when source in sight
- **Grappled**: Speed 0
- **Incapacitated**: No actions/reactions
- **Invisible**: Can't be seen
- **Paralyzed**: Incapacitated, can't move
- **Petrified**: Transformed to stone
- **Poisoned**: Disadvantage on attacks/checks
- **Prone**: Disadvantage on attacks
- **Restrained**: Speed 0, disadvantage
- **Stunned**: Incapacitated
- **Unconscious**: Incapacitated, unaware
