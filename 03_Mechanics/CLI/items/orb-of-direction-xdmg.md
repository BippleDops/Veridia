# Orb of Direction
*Wondrous item, common*  
![](/03_Mechanics/CLI/items/img/orb-of-direction.webp#right)

While holding this orb, you can take a [Magic](/03_Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Magic) action to determine which way is magnetic north. Nothing happens if the orb is used in a location that has no magnetic north.
