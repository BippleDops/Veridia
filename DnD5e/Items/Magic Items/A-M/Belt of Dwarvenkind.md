## Description

While wearing this belt, you gain the following benefits:

- Your [[Constitution|Constitution]] score increases by 2, to a maximum of 20.
- You have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Charisma|Charisma]] ([[Charisma|Persuasion]]) checks made to interact with dwarves.

In addition, while attuned to the belt, you have a 50 percent chance each day at dawn of growing a full beard if you’re capable of growing one, or a visibly thicker beard if you already have one.

If you aren’t a dwarf, you gain the following additional benefits while wearing the belt:

- You have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]] against [[17 - Damage and Healing|poison]], and you have [[17 - Damage and Healing|resistance]] against poison damage.
- You have [[9 - The Environment|darkvision]] out to arange of 60 feet.
- You can speak, read, and write Dwarvish.
