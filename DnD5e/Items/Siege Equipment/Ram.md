# Ram
## Overview
*Large object*

**Armor Class**: 15

**Hit Points**: 100

**Damage Immunities**: poison, psychic

A [[Ram|ram]] consists of a movable gallery equipped with a [[Heavy|heavy]] log suspended from two roof beams by chains. The log is shod in iron and used to batter through doors and barricades.

It takes a minimum of four Medium creatures to operate a [[Ram|ram]]. Because of the gallery roof, these operators have total cover against attacks from above.

**[[Ram]]**. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one object. Hit: 16 (`dice: 3d10`) bludgeoning damage.
