# Reed Staff (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, M (a splinter of wood)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
You turn a normal blade of grass into a magical quarterstaff that does 1d10 bludg-eoning damage. If anyone other than you touches the staff, it turns back into grass.
