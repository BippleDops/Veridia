## Description
These tight-wound tops from Calimshan have a lock and spring mechanism. When thrown, they hit the ground and whirl in a loud, clattering fashion. (3 gp each)

## Additional Details
Provide a 1 round diversion; they work only once per encounter, only on creatures unaware of the user
