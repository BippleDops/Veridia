# Giant Slayer

**Type**: Magic Item

**Rarity**: *rare*

## Description
You gain a +1 bonus to [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]].

When you hit a giant with it, the giant takes an extra `dice: 2d6` (2d6) damage of the weapon’s type and must succeed on a [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 15 [[Strength|Strength]] saving throw or fall [[13 - Movement and Position|prone]]. For the purpose of this weapon, “giant” refers to any creature with the giant type, including [[Ettin|ettins]] and [[Troll|trolls]].

** Base items.** This item variant can be applied to the following base items:

- [[Battleaxe]] (Giant Slayer Battleaxe)
- [[Double-Bladed Scimitar]] (Giant Slayer Double-Bladed Scimitar)
- [[Greataxe]] (Giant Slayer Greataxe)
- [[Greatsword]] (Giant Slayer Greatsword)
- [[Handaxe]] (Giant Slayer Handaxe)
- [[Longsword]] (Giant Slayer Longsword)
- [[Rapier]] (Giant Slayer Rapier)
- [[Scimitar]] (Giant Slayer Scimitar)
- [[Shortsword]] (Giant Slayer Shortsword)
