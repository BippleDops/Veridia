# Potion of Healing
*Potion, common*  

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

This potion is a magic item. As a [[bonus-action-xphb|Bonus Action]], you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself.
