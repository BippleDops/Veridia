This cataclysmic event occurred in 847 BCE when the ruling **Arcanum Supreme** - a council of the nine most powerful mages in the world - attempted the **Godhead Ritual**, seeking to transcend mortal limitations and achieve collective divinity. The ritual's failure released raw magical energies equivalent to tearing holes in reality itself.
