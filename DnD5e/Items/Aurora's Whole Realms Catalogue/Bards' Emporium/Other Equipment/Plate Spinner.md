## Description
Among the easiest but most impressive tricks of [[Balance|balance]] is plate spinning. We supply 8-inch long pins for spinning plates (3 cp each), unbreakable plates of wood set with a minuscule divot for easy spinning (2 sp each), and even a drilled stand that can hold four spinning plates at once (1 sp).

## Additional Details
provides a +2 to Juggling proficiency when used
