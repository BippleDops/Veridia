### Key Locations

**[A] Embassy Quarter - Foreign Missions**
- Aethermoor Embassy (Level 1)
- Neutral Territory Offices (Level 2)
- Trade Mission Headquarters (Level 3)

**[D] Grand Bazaar**
- Main Market (Central Plaza)
- Exotic Goods Section (East Wing)
- Food Courts (West Wing)
- Information Brokers (Basement)

**[J] Parliament Buildings**
- Main Assembly Hall
- Committee Chambers
- Public Galleries
- *Warning: Security checkpoints at all entrances*

**[K] Royal Palace Complex**
- Throne Room (Restricted)
- Royal Gardens
- Guest Quarters
- *Note: Invitation required beyond public areas*

**[M] Cathedral of Depths**
- Main Sanctuary
- Healing Pools
- Meditation Chambers
- Oracle's Tower

### Navigation Notes

**Current Flows:**
- Main Current: North to South, all levels
- Cross Currents: East-West connections between districts
- Express Channels: Direct routes for emergency/VIP transport

**Pressure Zones:**
- Safe for surface dwellers: Levels A-I
- Adaptation required: Levels J-O
- Extreme pressure gear: Levels P-U

**Travel Times:**
- Between adjacent districts: 15-30 minutes
- Surface to Deep Zone: 45 minutes
- Surface to Abyss: 2+ hours

### Emergency Locations

**Medical Centers:**
- Level F: Merchant District Clinic
- Level N: Temple Healing Sanctuary
- Level Q: Deep Research Medical Bay

**Safety Stations:**
- Every 2 city blocks
- Pressure equalization chambers
- Emergency communication crystals

**City Watch Posts:**
- Level E: Guild Hall Security
- Level K: Palace Guard
- Level R: Prison Complex

*Keep this map with you at all times while in the city*
*Report any suspicious Shadow Conspiracy activity to authorities*
