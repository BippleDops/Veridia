# M-102 Time Skip Round

## Read-Aloud
> A bell rings—and then hasn’t yet. Orders hang half-spoken while the next moment decides whether it exists.

## Setup
- Type: Oscillating • Category: Time/Combat

## Mechanics
- Group Sanity DC 15
- Tactics DC 15 to capitalize

## Outcomes
- Success: One role gains an extra action this round
- Partial: No net change
- Fail: One role loses its action to the skip
