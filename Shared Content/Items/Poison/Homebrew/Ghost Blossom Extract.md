# Ghost Blossom Extract
## Overview
Created from the juice of a pale white flower that only blossoms at night, the [[Poisoned|poison]] illicits violent muscle contractions, often causing broken bones and shattered teeth.

Knowledge Check: DC 12

Category: [[Ingested]] or [[Injury]]

Saving Throw: DC 12 Constitution

Frequency: one Minute

Primary Effect: 6 (`dice: 2d4`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage

Secondary Effect: 6 (`dice: 2d4`) bludgeoning damage

Cost: 200 gold pieces
