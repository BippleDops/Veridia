# Lightning Rod (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch (150-foot radius)

**Components**: V, S, M (a metal object weigh-ing no more than 100 pounds)

**Duration**: 24 hours


## Effects
You touch a metal object of any sort that weighs less than 100 pounds. Then, whenev-er a stroke of lightning (natural, magical, or created from a breath weapon) occurs within 150 feet of the rod, the lightning is redirected to the rod, which destroys it. If a creature is in physical contact with the rod when it is struck, that creatures takes full damage.
