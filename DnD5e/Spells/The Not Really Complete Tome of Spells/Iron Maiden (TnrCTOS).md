# Iron Maiden (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *4th-level*

**School**: *conjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V, S, M (a piece of metal from a suit of armor)

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
You summon a suit of animated armor (*Monster Manual*, page 19) that fights for you. This armor wields a greatsword and uses your spell attack modifier to fight. On a hit, it does 2d6+2 slashing damage.

***At Higher Levels.*** You conjure additional iron maidens when you use a 6th or 7th-level spell slot (two iron maidens) or an 8th-or 9th-level spell slot (four iron maidens).
