# Chime Lamp
## Overview
**Price**: 20 gp.

**Weight**: -

### Chime Lamp (Pic)
![[2ogrripg.tah.png|Chime Lamp (Pic)]]
[[2ogrripg.tah.png|open outside]]

## Description
The chime [[Lamps|lamps]] of Evermeet have delighted many a person with their pleasant tinkling and rosy glow. Constructed of brass with brass or crystal [[Chimes|chimes]], the heated air created by this lamp turns a delicate vented wheel, the blades of which strike tiny [[Chimes|chimes]] arrayed around the lamp (20 gp).

## Additional Details
Lights a 360-degree area out to 15 feet. Burns 6 hours per half-pint of oil. Holds a half pint.
