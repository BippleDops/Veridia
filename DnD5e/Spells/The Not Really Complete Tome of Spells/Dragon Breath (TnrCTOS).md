# Dragon Breath (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (30-foot cone)

**Components**: V, S, M (a dragon’s tooth)

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You breathe a 30-foot cone of dangerous energy, just like a dragon’s breath weapon, doing acid, cold, fire, lightning, necrotic, or poison damage. Each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 5d10 damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the spell does an additional 1d10 damage per spell slot above 3rd level.
