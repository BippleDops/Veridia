# Poisoner's Kit

## Overview
**Price**: 50 gp

**Weight**: 2 lb.

***Poisoner’s Kit.*** A poisoner’s kit includes the [[Vial|vials]], chemicals, and other equipment necessary for the creation of poisons. Proficiency with this kit lets you add your [[3 - Proficiency Bonus|proficiency bonus]] to any [[4 - Ability Checks|ability checks]] you make to craft or use [[12 - Poisons|poisons]].
