## Description
While wearing this necklace, you can breathe normally in any environment, and you have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]] made against harmful gases and vapors (such as **[[Cloudkill|cloudkill]]** and **[[Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]]** effects, inhaled poisons, and the breath weapons of some dragons).
