# Forgery Kit

## Overview
**Price**: 15 gp

**Weight**: 5 lb.

***Forgery Kit.*** This small box contains a variety of papers and parchments, pens and inks, seals and [[Sealing wax|sealing wax]], gold and silver leaf, and other supplies necessary to create convincing forgeries of physical documents. Proficiency with this kit lets you add your [[3 - Proficiency Bonus|proficiency bonus]] to any [[4 - Ability Checks|ability checks]] you make to create a physical forgery of a document.
