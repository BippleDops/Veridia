## Description
An old halfling saying states that “A cave with a hatch is only a cave; a cave with a hinged door is a home.” We stock iron hinges of all varieties: [[Jewelry|jewelry]]-box (5 cp), cabinet (7 cp), door (2 sp), barn (3 sp), gate (4 sp), massive door (5 gp). These sizes are also available in steel for twice the price. [[Jewelry]]-box and cabinet hinges also come in silver (ten times price) and gold (100 times price).
