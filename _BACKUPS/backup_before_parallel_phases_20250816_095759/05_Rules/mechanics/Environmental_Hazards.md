# ⚠️ Environmental Hazards

## Natural Hazards

### Quicksand
- **DC 10** Perception to notice
- **DC 10** STR to escape
- Sinking: `dice: 1d4`+1 rounds to submerge

### Avalanche
- **DC 15** DEX save
- `dice: 4d10` bludgeoning damage
- Buried on failed save

### Forest Fire
- `dice: 1d6` fire damage per round
- Smoke: CON save or blinded
- Spreading: 20 ft per round

## Dungeon Hazards

### Pit Trap
- **DC 15** Perception to find
- **DC 12** DEX save to avoid
- `dice: 2d6` falling damage

### Poison Darts
- **DC 15** Investigation to find
- +8 to hit
- `dice: 1d4` piercing + `dice: 2d4` poison

### Collapsing Ceiling
- **DC 15** Perception for warning
- **DC 15** DEX save
- `dice: 4d10` bludgeoning damage
