## Description
Carved of rich woods from Sembia and Cormyr, these twelve-inch long [[Toys|toys]] are fitted with wheels so a child can pull them along. Expertly crafted with internal hinges that allow the mouth to open and close and the tail to wag as it moves, each is a delight for the younger ones. Recommended ages 2-5. Specify animal when ordering. All styles available for 1 gp each. Each model weighs around 5 lbs.
