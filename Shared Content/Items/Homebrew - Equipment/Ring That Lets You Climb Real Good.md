# Ring That Lets You Climb Real Good
## Overview
### Ring of Climbing (Pic)
![[auz1w3bk.bex.png|Ring of Climbing (Pic)]]
[[auz1w3bk.bex.png|open outside]]

**Climbing:** This ring is actually a magic leather cord that ties around a finger. It continually grants the wearer a +5 competence bonus on [Climb](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:Climb_Skill) checks.

Faint transmutatation; CL 5th; [Forge Ring](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:Forge_Ring), creator must have 5 ranks in the [Climb skill](http://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/SRD:Climb_Skill); Price 2,500 gp.
