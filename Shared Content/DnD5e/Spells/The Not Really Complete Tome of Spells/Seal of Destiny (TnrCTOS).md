# Seal of Destiny (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *7th-level*

**School**: *abjuration*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 10 minutes

**Range**: 100 feet

**Components**: V, M (1 ounce of your blood)

**Duration**: Permanent


## Effects
You seal a creature or object against any sort of divination that would reveal its destiny, fate, or role in epic events.
