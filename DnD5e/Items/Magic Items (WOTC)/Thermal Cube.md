# Thermal Cube

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items

**Rarity:** common

## Description

This 3-inch cube of solid brimstone generates enough dry heat to keep the temperature within 15 feet of it at 95 degrees Fahrenheit (35 degrees Celsius).
