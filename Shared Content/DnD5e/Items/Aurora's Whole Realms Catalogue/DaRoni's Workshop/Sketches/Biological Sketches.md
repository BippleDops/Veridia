## Description
Much can be learned of the worlds outer workings by looking at what its doing on the inside. I have done so. King Azoun IV of cormyr has agreed to let me dissect the bodies of enemy dead. My [[Sketches|sketches]] show all the systems of the body: skeletal, muscular, ligamentary, visceral, vital, lymphatic, sanguine, auditory, optic, olfactory, and palatal. The [[Sketches|sketches]] show theory for working of all systems. The [[Sketches|sketches]] also have studies to [[Balance|balance]] the four humorsblood, phlegm, choler, and black bile to thereby correct saguine, phlegmatic, cholic, and bilious natures. Aurora will reproduce. All [[Sketches|sketches]] 4 gp per page. Some more.
