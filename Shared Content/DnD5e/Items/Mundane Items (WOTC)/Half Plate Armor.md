# Half Plate Armor
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: medium armor
**Properties**: AC 15 + Dex (max 2)
**Weight**: 40 lb.
**Value**: 750 gp
## Description: 
Half plate consists of shaped metal plates that cover most of the wearer's body. It does not include leg protection beyond simple greaves that are attached with leather straps.The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
