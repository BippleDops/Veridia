# Ear Horn of Hearing
*Wondrous item, common*  



While held up to your ear, this horn suppresses the effects of the [Deafened](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Deafened) condition on you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 256*
