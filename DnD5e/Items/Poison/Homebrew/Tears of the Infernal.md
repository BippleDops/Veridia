# Tears of the Infernal
## Overview
Harvested and distilled from the oozing sap of the Weeping Cactus that dwells in the most desolate deserts in the world, this oily [[0. DM Screen/Weapons, Armor, Items, Spells/Poison|poison]] dissolves organic material upon [[Contact|contact]] . Assassins often utilize the [[0. DM Screen/Weapons, Armor, Items, Spells/Poison|poison]] on their blades as it has a high enough viscousity not to drip onto their own exposed flesh, plus the rarity often causes medical [[Aid|aid]] to be less than effective, as healers are unaware of a cure.

Knowledge Check: DC 20

Category: [[Injury]]

Saving Throw: DC 16 Constitution

Frequency: one Minute

Primary Effect: 5 (`dice: 1d6+1`) [[Acid|acid]] damage

Secondary Effect: 5 (`dice: 1d6+1`) [[Acid|acid]] damage

Cost: 250 gold pieces
