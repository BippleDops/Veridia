# Equipment Packs for Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD

## Overview
The articles in this grouping comprise the new equipment packs introduced in Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD.

The starting equipment you get from your class includes a collection of useful [[4 - Adventuring Gear|adventuring gear]], put together in a pack. The contents of these packs are listed here. If you are buying your starting equipment, you can purchase a pack for the price shown, which might be cheaper than buying the items individually.
