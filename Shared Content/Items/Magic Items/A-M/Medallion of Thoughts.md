# Medallion of Thoughts

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *uncommon*

**Attunement**: *requires attunement*

**Weight**: 1 lb.

![[Medallion of Thoughts.jpg| Medallion of Thoughts]]

## Description
The medallion has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the **[[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]]** spell (save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 13) from it. The medallion regains `dice: 1d3` (1d3) expended charges daily at dawn.
