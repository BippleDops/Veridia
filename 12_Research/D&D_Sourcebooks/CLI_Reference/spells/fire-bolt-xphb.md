# Fire Bolt
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or an object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `d10` Fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell starts [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/traps-hazards/burning-xphb|burning]] if it isn't being worn or carried.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `d10` when you reach levels 5 (`2d10`), 11 (`3d10`), and 17 (`4d10`).
