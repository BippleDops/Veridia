## Description
With properly-strung block and tackle, one man can lift a giant from the ground. All of our block and tackle sets include a sturdy steel pulley and 100 ft of rope. We supply [[Pulleys|pulleys]] for capacities of 100-lb (3 gp), 200-lb (7 gp), 500-lb (15 gp), 1000-lb (25 gp), and even a two-pulley ton (35 gp) load capacity, with the appropriate rope.
