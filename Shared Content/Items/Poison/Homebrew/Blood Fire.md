# Blood Fire
## Overview
A reddish-brown sticky paste, this [[Poisoned|poison]] has seen a fair amount of use. Once injured, the [[Poisoned|poison]] begins igniting the blood, causing a creature to quickly burn from within. Often the toxin burns through the victim so quickly and intensly that the remains are little more than charred bones.

There are several methods of creating Blood Fire [[Poisoned|poison]], although the most [[Common|common]] involves the fruit of a desert cactus soaked in the saliva of giant [[Lizard|lizards]] mixed with distilled alcohols. After several days of soaking the fruit is removed from the mixture and fround into a paste.

Knowledge Check: DC 12

Category: [[Injury]]

Saving Throw: Constitution DC 12

Frequency: one Round

Primary Effect: 5 (`dice: 1d6+1`) fire damage

Secondary Effect: 5 (`dice: 1d6+1`) fire damage

Cost: 150 gold pieces
