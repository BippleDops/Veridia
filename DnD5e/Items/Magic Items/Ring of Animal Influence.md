## Description
This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `dice: 1d3` (1d3) expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast one of the following spells:
- **[[Animal friendship]]** (save DC 13)
- **[[Fear]]** (save DC 13), targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or lower
- **[[Speak with Animals|Speak with Animals]]**
