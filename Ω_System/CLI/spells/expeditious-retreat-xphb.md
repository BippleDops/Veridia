# Expeditious Retreat
*1st-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You take the [Dash](/03_Mechanics/CLI/actions.md#Dash) action, and until the spell ends, you can take that action again as a [Bonus Action](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md).
