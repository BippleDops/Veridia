# Resistances
*Source: Monster Manual p. 180* 

| Color | Damage Resistance |
|-------|-------------------|
| Black or copper | Acid |
| Blue or bronze | Lightning |
| Brass, gold, or red | Fire |
| Green | Poison |
| Silver or white | Cold |
