# Spring (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V, S, M (a small forked stick, which is thrust into the ground)

**Duration**: 24 hours


## Effects
You create a temporary freshwater spring that flows at a rate of 2d6 gallons per minute; if the area is overly arid, then it only produc-es 1d4 gallons per minute.
