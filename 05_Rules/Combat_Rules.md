## Examples of Play

### Basic Example
Sara wants to jump across a 10ft chasm. She rolls Athletics: d20+3 vs DC 10.

### Intermediate Example
During combat, Marcus attempts to jump the chasm while being shot at. He rolls with disadvantage.

### Advanced Example
Elena tries to jump while carrying an unconscious ally in heavy rain. DC increases to 15, disadvantage, and STR check to not drop ally.

## Edge Cases & Rulings

### Q: What if a player has multiple inspiration sources?
A: Maximum of 2 inspiration dice, must declare which before rolling

### Q: Can this stack with similar abilities?
A: No, use the higher bonus only

### Q: What about antimagic fields?
A: Supernatural abilities suppressed, mundane portions still function
