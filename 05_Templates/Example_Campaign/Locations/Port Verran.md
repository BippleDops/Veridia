## 🗺️ Description

Port Verran is a bustling port city built on the largest island of the Cerulean Archipelago. It is a hub of trade and commerce, with ships from all over the Shattered Isles docking at its busy harbor. The city is a melting pot of cultures, with a diverse population of humans, elves, dwarves, and other races.
