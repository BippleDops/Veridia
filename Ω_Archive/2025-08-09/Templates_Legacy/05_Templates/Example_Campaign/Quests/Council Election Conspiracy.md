# Council Election Conspiracy

## Quest Info
**Quest Giver**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Councillor Elara Brightwater]]
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Shadowhaven]]

## Quest Details
The party is hired by Councillor Brightwater to investigate a conspiracy to rig the upcoming city council election.

## GM Notes
- The conspiracy is being orchestrated by [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Councillor Marcus Greywind]] and the Zhentarim.
- The party will have to use all of their skills of stealth, subterfuge, and diplomacy to uncover the truth and expose the conspirators.
- The party's actions will have a major impact on the future of Shadowhaven.
