# Banner
## Overview
**Banner/Standard**: A banner is a large cloth emblazoned with a heraldic device. It is used to display a personal, family, or royal crest, or as identification on the battlefield. The price given is for a simple banner made from cotton and cheaper materials; the fancier the banner, the higher the cost.

A banner grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Spot checks to be seen from a distance.

**Cost**: 30gp+

**Weight**: 10lb.
