# Merger Events Pack C — 50 Ready-to-Run Encounters (M-201–M-250)

Complements Packs A and B. Use with core systems and vehicle rules.

---

### M-201: Event Horizon Shear
- Type: Paradox • Category: Physics
- Trigger: Horizon ring slices rigging from reality.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 16; Engineer DC 16; Sanity DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Integrity +1; safe pass. Partial: Rigging 1d6. Fail: 2d6; role stunned 1 round.

### M-202: Mnemosyne Market
- Type: Anchored • Category: Economy/Mystic
- Trigger: Bazaar trades memories only.
- Checks: Economy (Int) DC 15; Sanity DC 14; Insight DC 15 for scams.
- Outcomes: Success: Gain Epic chart for costly memory; or refuse with no harm. Partial: Unfair trade. Fail: Identity drain (minor memory lost).

### M-203: Aleph Sensor Overload
- Type: Overlapping • Category: Navigation
- Trigger: Sensor sees all points at once.
- Checks: Sensor DC 16 filter; Sanity DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Perfect course; time -25%. Partial: Noise; delay. Fail: Disorientation; -1 Maneuver 1h.

### M-204: Glitch Junker Chase
- Type: Flicker • Category: Pursuit
- Trigger: Pixelating smuggler ship.
- Checks: Tactics DC 15; Helm DC 16 jumps.
- Outcomes: Success: Capture; loot 200–600 gp. Partial: Lose track; clue remains. Fail: Ambushed by accomplice.

### M-205: Palinode Regret Ritual
- Type: Anchored • Category: Ritual
- Trigger: Zone asks you to take back an action.
- Checks: Religion/Arcana DC 15; Sanity DC 14.
- Outcomes: Success: Undo one minor scene effect. Partial: Uncertain—GM discretion. Fail: Sanity cost; paradox scar.
