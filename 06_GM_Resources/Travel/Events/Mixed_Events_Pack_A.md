# Mixed Events Pack A — 50 Ready-to-Run Encounters

These blend depth, sky, and merger motifs. Use all core systems (Pressure/Altitude, Reality, Sanity, Memory, Factions) with vehicle modules relevant to the route.

---

### X-001: Trench-to-Cloud Transfer
- Trigger: Use a merger portal to emerge above Aethermoor mid-storm.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 15 stabilize; Helm DC 15 adapt controls from sub → airship profile.
- Outcomes: Success: Seamless transition; no time loss. Partial: Turbulence (1d6); -1 Maneuver 1 hour. Fail: Stall/spin; crew Con DC 14 vs. vertigo.
- Integration: Cross-vehicle procedures; Anchors.
- Variants: Transfer occurs into gravity well.

### X-002: Memory-Chart Toll
- Trigger: Faction demands payment in memory charts for safe passage.
- Checks: Persuasion DC 14; Deception DC 14 if forging; Insight DC 14 to judge fairness.
- Outcomes: Success: Reduced toll; minor faction +. Partial: Pay full toll (1 Uncommon chart). Fail: Conflict or detour; time +1d4 hours.
- Integration: Memory Economy; Faction law.
- Variants: Toll-takers are pirates posing as officials.

### X-003: Anchor Storm
- Trigger: Multiple anchors from both worlds resonate.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 15 orchestration; Engineer DC 15 buffering; Sanity DC 14 for choir aboard.
- Outcomes: Success: Integrity +2; free route boon. Partial: +1 only; Sensor -1. Fail: Integrity -1; one short-term madness.
- Integration: Reality; Sanity; Fleet doctrines.
- Variants: Cult attempts to hijack storm.

### X-004: Trade War Skirmish
- Trigger: Merchant guild vs. choir pirates at a portal.
- Checks: Tactics DC 15; Gunnery DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Secure lane; faction +1; salvage 200–600 gp. Partial: Stalemate. Fail: Forced retreat; -1 to faction standing.
- Integration: Faction Warfare; Economic Warfare.
- Variants: Privateer letters complicate legality.

### X-005: Dream Sailing Convoy
- Trigger: Night convoy uses lullaby navigation.
- Checks: Performance DC 14; Sanity DC 14.
- Outcomes: Success: Travel time -25%; Sanity +1. Partial: No gain. Fail: Dream incursion; identity bleed.
- Integration: Sanity; Memory; Deep Mother.
- Variants: Lullaby is a trap set by cult.

### X-006–X-050
- Additional blended set pieces: pressure-to-altitude inversions, sky leviathan reflected as trench titan, paradox doors between tube stations and cloud canyons, anchor duels across worlds, refugee transfers via portal, star anchor plus seafloor anchor harmonic puzzles, crew crises that play differently in depth vs. sky, and mixed piracy/privateering actions under cross-world treaties, each with:
  - Checks/DCs for both vehicle profiles
  - Outcomes with Sanity, Integrity, faction standing, memory rewards
  - Scaling by zone/band and merger type
  - Variants that hook into current campaign arcs
