The Eel Keepers are a specialized guild of animal handlers who manage, train, and breed the various eel species used throughout Aquabyssos for communication, transportation, and defense. Their expertise with these creatures makes them indispensable to the city's infrastructure. This ancient guild has served the underwater kingdom for over 300 years, developing techniques passed down through generations of master trainers who understand the subtle art of eel communication and control. ## Organization
