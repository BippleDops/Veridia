## Description: 
A druidic focus might be a sprig of mistletoe or holly, a wand or scepter made of yew or another special wood, a staff drawn whole out of a living tree, or a totem object incorporating feathers, fur, bones, and teeth from sacred animals. A druid can use such an object as a spellcasting focus.Multiple variations of this item exist, as listed below:Sprig of mistletoeTotemWooden staffYew wand
