# Tooth Wrench
## Overview
**Price**: 5 gp.

**Weight**: -

### Tooth Wrench (Pic)
![[nd2bblyu.nag.png|Tooth Wrench (Pic)]]
[[nd2bblyu.nag.png|open outside]]

## Description
Why bother with the torture of strings and slamming doors when you can pull a tooth the safe way? With this tooth wrench, a strong person can rip out even well-rooted molars. Developed by craftsmen of Thay, this ingenious device clamps in place and can pry against the opposing gum for extra leverage. (5 gp)

## Additional Details
combined Str roll must equal patient’s Con to remove tooth; patient rolls ½ Con to remain conscious
