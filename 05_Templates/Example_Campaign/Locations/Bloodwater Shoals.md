# Bloodwater Shoals

## General Info
**Location**: [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/The Contested Waters.md]]

## Description
Bloodwater Shoals is a dangerous reef system that is known for its treacherous currents and its population of bloodthirsty sharks.

## GM Notes
- The shoals are a dangerous and difficult place to navigate.
- The shoals are home to many dangerous creatures, including sharks, sahuagin, and sea hags.
- There is a rumor of a hidden treasure in the shoals, but it is guarded by a powerful sea monster.
