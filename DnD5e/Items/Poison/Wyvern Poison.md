# Wyvern Poison
## Overview
*[[Wyvern]]* *[[Poisoned|Poison]]** (Injury).* This [[Poisoned|poison]] must be harvested from a dead or [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] [[Wyvern|wyvern]]. A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (`dice: 7d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
