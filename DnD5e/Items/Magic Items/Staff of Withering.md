## Description
This [[Staff|staff]] has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.

The [[Staff|staff]] can be wielded as a magic [[Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]. On a hit, it deals damage as a normal quarterstaff, and you can expend 1 charge to deal an extra `dice: 2d10` (2d10) [[17 - Damage and Healing|necrotic]] damage to the target. In addition, the target must succeed on a [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 15 [[Constitution|Constitution]] saving throw or have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|disadvantage]] for 1 hour on any ability check or saving throw that uses [[Strength|Strength]] or [[Constitution|Constitution]].
