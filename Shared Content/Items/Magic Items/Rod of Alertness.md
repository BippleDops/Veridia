# Rod of Alertness

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Rods*

**Rarity**: *Very Rare*

**Attunement**: *requires attunement*


## Description
This rod has a flanged head and the following properties.

***Alertness.*** While holding the rod, you have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Wisdom|Wisdom]] ([[Wisdom|Perception]]) checks and on rolls for [[12 - The Order of Combat|initiative]].

***Spells.*** While holding the rod, you can use an action to cast one of the following spells from it: **[[Detect Evil and Good]],** **[[Detect Magic|Detect Magic]],** **[[Detect Poison and Disease|Detect Poison and Disease]],** or **[[See Invisibility|see invisibility]].**


***Protective Aura.*** As an action, you can plant the haft end of the [[Rod|rod]] in the ground, whereupon the rod’s head sheds bright light in a 60-foot radius and [[9 - The Environment|dim light]] for an additional 60 feet. While in that [[9 - The Environment|bright light]], you and any creature that is friendly to you gain a +1 bonus to [[2 - Armor|AC]] and [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]] and can sense the location of any [[Invisible|invisible]] hostile creature that is also in the bright light.

The [[Rod|rod]]’s head stops glowing and the effect ends after 10 minutes, or when a creature uses an action to pull the rod from the ground. This property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
