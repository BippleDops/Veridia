# Perfume
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 5 gp

Perfume comes in a 4-ounce vial. For 1 hour after applying Perfume to yourself, you have [Advantage](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Charisma ([Persuasion](/03_Mechanics/CLI/skills.md#Persuasion)) checks made to influence an Indifferent Humanoid within 5 feet of yourself.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 227. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
