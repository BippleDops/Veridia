# The Crimson Tide Pirates

## General Info
**Location**: [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/The Contested Waters.md]]

## Description
The Crimson Tide is the most feared pirate crew on the seas. They are led by the ruthless and charismatic [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/NPCs/Pirate King Razor Blackheart.md]], and they are known for their brutality and their love of treasure.

## GM Notes
- The Crimson Tide is a major antagonist for the party, but they are also a potential ally if the party can earn their respect.
- The Crimson Tide is a rival of the [[02_Worldbuilding/Factions/Shadowhaven Merchant Marine.md]] and the [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/NPCs/Admiral Cassius Stormhaven.md|Shadowhaven Navy]].
- The Crimson Tide has a secret base on [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/Merchant's Rest Island.md]].
