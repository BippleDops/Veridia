# Aid
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a strip of white cloth)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's [Hit Point](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum and current [Hit Points](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) increase by 5 for the duration.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** Each target's [Hit Points](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 2.
