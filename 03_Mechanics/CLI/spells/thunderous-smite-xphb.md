# Thunderous Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Your strike rings with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the target takes an extra `2d6` Thunder damage from the attack. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and have the [Prone](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Prone) condition.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `d6` for each spell slot level above 1.
