# Component Pouch

## Overview
**Price**: 25 gp

**Weight**: 2 lb.

**Component Pouch.** A component [[Pouch|pouch]] is a small, watertight leather belt pouch that has compartments to hold all the [[2 - Casting a Spell|material]] components and other [[3 - Weapons|special]] items you need to cast your spells, except for those components that have a specific cost (as indicated in a spell's description).
