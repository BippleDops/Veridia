# Admiral Cassius Stormhaven

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 60
**Occupation**: Admiral of the Shadowhaven Navy
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Shadowhaven]]

## Description
Admiral Stormhaven is a veteran naval officer who has dedicated his life to protecting Shadowhaven from threats at sea. He is a brilliant strategist and a charismatic leader, and he is respected by his sailors.

## GM Notes
- He is a powerful ally for the party, but he is also very busy and will not suffer fools gladly.
- He is a rival of [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Captain Blackwater]] and the Blackwater Raiders.
- He is secretly a member of the Lords' Alliance.
