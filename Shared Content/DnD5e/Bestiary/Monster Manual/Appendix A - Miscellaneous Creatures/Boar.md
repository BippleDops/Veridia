## Special Traits
***Charge.*** If the boar moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with a tusk attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 3 (`dice: 1d6`) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

***Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).*** If the boar takes 7 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.
