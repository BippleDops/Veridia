# Goggles of Night

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *uncommon*


## Description
These gloves are [[invisible]] while worn. While wearing them, you gain a +5 bonus to [[Dexterity]] ([[Sleight of Hand]]) checks and Dexterity checks made to pick locks.
