# Elixir of Health

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *Rare*


## Description
When you drink this potion, it cures any disease afflicting you, and it removes the [[blinded]], [[deafened]], [[paralyzed]], and [[poisoned conditions]]. The clear red liquid has tiny bubbles of light in it.
