# Enspelled Weapon (Level 7)
*Legendary (requires attunement)*  

Bound into this weapon is a level 7 spell. The spell is determined when the weapon is created and must belong to the Conjuration, Divination, Evocation, Necromancy, or Transmutation school of magic. The weapon has 6 charges and regains `d6` expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the weapon, you can expend 1 charge to cast its spell.

The spell's saving throw DC is 18, and its attack bonus is `+10`.
