# Heal
*6th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature that you can see within range. A surge of positive energy washes through the creature, causing it to regain 70 hit points. This spell also ends [blinded](/rules/conditions.md#blinded), [deafened](/rules/conditions.md#deafened), and any diseases affecting the target. This spell has no effect on constructs or undead.

**Classes**: Cleric, Druid

Source: Player's Handbook p. 250
