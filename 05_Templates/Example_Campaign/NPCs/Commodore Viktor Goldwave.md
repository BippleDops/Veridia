# Commodore Viktor Goldwave

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 40
**Occupation**: Commodore of the Port Sorrow Trading Company
**Location**: [[Port Sorrow]]

## Description
Commodore Goldwave is a ruthless and ambitious man who is determined to make the Port Sorrow Trading Company the most powerful mercantile force on the seas. He is a skilled negotiator and a cunning strategist, and he is not afraid to use dirty tricks to get what he wants.

## GM Notes
- He is a major antagonist for the party, and he will stop at nothing to achieve his goals.
- He is a rival of [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/NPCs/Admiral Cassius Stormhaven.md]] and the Shadowhaven Navy.
- He is secretly in league with the Zhentarim.
