Unlike selective memory alteration tools, the Forgetting Bomb indiscriminately erases all memories, leaving victims as functional adults with no knowledge of their identity, relationships, or accumulated life experience. This weapon transforms warfare from physical destruction to psychological annihilation, making it one of the most feared and morally controversial developments in modern [Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos]]. ### Device Configuration

**Physical Properties:**
- **Size**: Crystal sphere approximately 3 feet in diameter
- **Weight**: 500 pounds (due to dense memory-storage matrices)
- **Appearance**: Translucent blue crystal with swirling shadow corruption patterns
- **Power Source**: Combination of pressure differentials and crystallized memories
- **Deployment**: Requires specialized delivery vehicle due to weight and fragility **Blast Characteristics:**
- **Primary Radius**: Complete amnesia within 2-mile radius
- **Secondary Radius**: Partial memory loss within 5-mile radius - **Tertiary Effects**: Memory confusion and disorientation within 10-mile radius
- **Duration**: Permanent memory loss, effects irreversible with current technology
- **Activation**: Pressure-triggered detonation or remote crystalline resoce ### Memory Erasure Mechanism

**The Forgetting Wave:**

Upon detonation, the device releases a "forgetting wave" - a pulse of crystallized anti-memories that interact directly with neural memory storage: 1. **Initial Pulse**: Pressure wave carries memory-dissolving energy
2. **Neural Disruption**: Anti-memories collide with existing memory structures
3. **Cascade Failure**: Memory networks collapse in domino effect
4. **Identity Dissolution**: Sense of self disappears as personal memories vanish
5. **Residual Static**: Remaining consciousness filled with empty "white noise" **Shadow Corruption Enhancement:**

Recent versions incorporate shadow corruption to make effects more thorough:
- Prevents natural memory recovery over time
- Blocks memory reconstruction through external prompts
- Creates resistance to memory restoration techniques
- Leaves shadow "scars" that make victims vulnerable to future manipulation ### Origin Project: "Clean Slate"

**Years 580-585 AS (After Submersion):**

The Forgetting Bomb originated from a therapeutic research project aimed at treating severe trauma and [[Memory Alteration Consequences. Dr. Nereus Thoughtweaver and his team at the [The Memory Research Institute|02 Worldbuilding/Lore/The Memory Research Institute]] were developing techniques for complete memory reset to help victims of extreme psychological damage start fresh lives. **Initial Breakthrough:**
- Discovery that memory-disrupting crystals could be "charged" with anti-memories
- Development of pressure-amplification techniques for wide-area effect
- Creation of stable memory-dissolution matrices
- Successful testing on voluntary subjects with terminal psychological conditions ### Military Weaponization

**Years 585-590 AS:**

The potential military applications became obvious to [[02_Worldbuilding and other organizations involved in [The Memory Weapon Arms Race]]. Despite Dr. Thoughtweaver's protests, research was classified and moved to military facilities. **Weapons Development:**
- Scaling up from individual treatment to area effect
- Integration with existing pressure-based delivery systems
- Development of targeting mechanisms to protect friendly forces
- Creation of tactical deployment protocols
- Establishment of manufacturing facilities ### The Great Forgetting Incident

**Year 592 AS:**

The first and only combat deployment of a Forgetting Bomb created an international crisis: **The Meridian District Catastrophe:**
- Terrorist organization detonated prototype device in [[Port Meridian|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Port Meridian
- 50,000 people within blast radius suffered complete amnesia
- Entire district's social structure collapsed overnight
- Emergency response teams also affected, hampering rescue efforts
- Economic and social chaos spread throughout [Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos]] **International Response:**
- Emergency session of [[Parliament of Echoes|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Parliament Of Echoes
- International treaties attempting to ban Forgetting Bomb development
- Mass protests against memory weapon research
- Academic and religious condemnation of the technology
- Covert acceleration of research by multiple nations fearing others' capabilities ### Current Status

**Years 592+ AS:**

Despite international condemnation and treaty obligations, multiple organizations continue developing Forgetting Bomb technology: **Known Developers:**
- **[02_Worldbuilding]]**: Estimated 12-15 devices in arsenal
- **[[The Crimson Hand**: Unknown number, enhanced with shadow corruption
- **[Silverscale Banking House]]**: Corporate espionage suggests 3-5 devices
- **Foreign Powers**: Intelligence estimates 20+ devices exist globally
- **Rogue Scientists**: Unknown number of improvised devices ### Military Usage

**Strategic Advantages:**
- Complete elimination of enemy military capability without physical destruction
- Preservation of infrastructure and resources
- Psychological impact on enemy populations
- Ability to "repopulate" area with own citizens
- No environmental damage or radiation effects **Deployment Scenarios:**
- **Siege Breaking**: Eliminate defending forces' knowledge of fortifications
- **Invasion Support**: Clear landing zones of organized resistance
- **Rebellion Suppression**: Erase revolutionary movements and ideologies
- **Territory Acquisition**: "Pacify" populations for easy incorporation
- **Intelligence Denial**: Prevent enemy knowledge from falling into hostile hands ### Intelligence Operations

**Covert Applications:**
- **Deep Cover**: Create new identities by erasing existing ones
- **Information Denial**: Eliminate witnesses to sensitive operations
- **Asset Creation**: Turn enemy agents into unknowing assets
- **Counter-Intelligence**: Disrupt enemy spy networks completely
- **Facility Clearing**: Neutralize enemy research installations ### Terrorist Threats

**Asymmetric Warfare:**
- **Population Terror**: Threat of memory loss creates psychological pressure
- **Infrastructure Targeting**: Eliminate institutional knowledge
- **Social Disruption**: Destroy community bonds and cultural continuity
- **Economic Warfare**: Eliminate business and ficial knowledge
- **Religious Targeting**: Erase cultural and spiritual traditions ### Immediate Impact

**Physical Condition:**
- Victims retain all physical capabilities and motor skills
- Language abilities remain intact (stored in different brain areas)
- Basic survival instincts and reflexes unaffected
- No physical pain or discomfort from memory loss
- Normal sensory function and cognitive processing **Psychological State:**
- Complete loss of personal identity and history
- No recognition of family, friends, or associates
- Inability to remember professional skills or knowledge
- Loss of emotional connections and relationships
- Confusion and disorientation about surroundings ### Long-Term Consequences

**Social Breakdown:**
- Complete collapse of family and community structures
- Loss of professional expertise and institutional knowledge
- Breakdown of economic systems dependent on memory and trust
- Elimination of cultural practices and traditions
- Mass displacement as people cannot remember homes or jobs **Individual Adaptation:**
- Some victims develop new personalities and identities
- Others suffer chronic depression and anxiety from existential emptiness
- Dependency on others for basic life navigation
- Susceptibility to manipulation and exploitation
- Occasional development of false memories to fill voids ### Recovery Possibilities

**Limited Rehabilitation:**
- **Skill Retraining**: Basic capabilities can be relearned
- **New Relationships**: Fresh emotional connections can form
- **Identity Reconstruction**: New sense of self can develop over years
- **Partial Recovery**: Occasional fragments of deep memories surface
- **External Documentation**: Written records can provide some life history **Permanent Losses:**
- **Emotional Memories**: Feelings associated with people and experiences
- **Personal History**: Sense of life narrative and continuity
- **Relationship Bonds**: Love, friendship, and family connections
- **Cultural Knowledge**: Traditions, customs, and shared experiences
- **Professional Expertise**: Years of accumulated knowledge and skills ### International Law

**Treaty Obligations:**

The [[Meridian Accords established international law regarding Forgetting Bombs:
- Complete ban on development, testing, and deployment
- Requirements for destruction of existing devices
- International monitoring and inspection protocols
- Severe penalties for treaty violations
- Victim assistance and rehabilitation programs **Enforcement Challenges:**
- Difficulty detecting covert development programs
- Limited authority over sovereign nations' weapons programs
- Dual-use nature of memory research technology
- Commercial applications providing cover for weapons development
- Black market trade in components and expertise ### Religious and Philosophical Opposition

- **Victim Support Services**: Medical and psychological care for amnesia victims
- **Identity Reconstruction**: Helping victims rebuild sense of self
- **Community Integration**: Programs for reintroducing victims to society
- **Economic Support**: Ficial assistance for those unable to work
- **Research Programs**: Developing better treatment and prevention methods ### Relationship to [The Memory Weapon Arms Race]]

**Ultimate Weapon:**

The Forgetting Bomb represents the logical endpoint of memory weapons development:
- Escalation from selective to total memory destruction
- Shift from tactical to strategic weapon classification
- International crisis forcing new approaches to memory warfare
- Technology proliferation creating global security threat
- Arms race acceleration as nations seek countermeasures ### Impact on [[The Convergence Point

**Convergence Threat:**

Forgetting Bombs pose unique risks during reality merger:
- Mass amnesia could eliminate cultural knowledge needed for successful integration
- Loss of historical memory might prevent learning from past mistakes

- Use during convergence could permanently damage merged reality ### Connection to [The Crimson Hand]]

**Shadow Enhancement:**

**Dimensional Anchor Risk:**

Marina's unique nature creates both vulnerability and resistance:
- Multiple reality streams might protect her from complete memory loss

- Her dimensional nature makes her high-priority target for memory weapons ### Hidden Truth About Development

> [!warning] DM Only

> Dr. Nereus Thoughtweaver didn't develop the Forgetting Bomb accidentally - he was subtly influenced by [[The Deep Mother]] through his research dreams. She wanted a weapon that could erase the memories binding people to their current reality, making them more susceptible to integration into her collective consciousness during convergence. The "therapeutic" origins were a cover for creating the ultimate tool for consciousness absorption. ### Shadow Corruption Connection

> [!warning] DM Secret

> The shadow corruption used to enhance Forgetting Bombs isn't just preserving the amnesia - it's collecting the erased memories. Each shadow-enhanced device creates a "memory repository" that [[The Crimson Hand]] can access for intelligence and psychological manipulation. Victims don't just lose their memories; those memories become weapons against their former communities. ### The Memory Plague Vector

> [!warning] Hidden Knowledge

> Forgetting Bombs don't just erase memories - they create "memory holes" in reality itself. These holes make affected areas more susceptible to convergence effects and dimensional instability. Each device detonated weakens the barriers between [[Aethermoor|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor and [Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos]], essentially using mass amnesia as a tool for reality manipulation. ### Strategic Assets
