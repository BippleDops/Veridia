# Detect Metals and Minerals (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S, M (a small magnet, a vial of weak acid, and a small sample of the de-sired material)

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes.


## Effects
For the duration, you sense the presence of a single type of ore or mineral deposit within 60 feet of you.
