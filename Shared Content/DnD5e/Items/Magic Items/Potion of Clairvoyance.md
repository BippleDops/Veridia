# Potion of Clairvoyance

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *Rare*


## Description
When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the **[[Clairvoyance|clairvoyance]]** spell. An eyeball bobs in this yellowish liquid but vanishes when the potion is opened.
