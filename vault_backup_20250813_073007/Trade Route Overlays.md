## Primary Trade Arteries

### The Great Circuit (Multi-Realm)
**Route**: Port Meridian (Surface) → Port Meridian Harbor (847 ft) → Abyssos Prime (1,000-3,000 ft) → Nereidios (2,000-4,000 ft) → New Tethys (1,500-3,000 ft) → Return
**Total Distance**: ~800 nautical miles
**Transit Time**: 12-15 days full circuit
**Primary Goods**: Crystals, memory pearls, knowledge, luxury items
**Traffic Volume**: 200+ vessels daily

### The Kelp Highway (Aquabyssos Primary)
**Route**: Abyssos Prime → Free Current → Corallion → Nereidios → Crystallium
**Depth Range**: 200-4,000 feet
**Distance**: 1,200 miles
**Transit Method**: Current-riding, pressure tubes
**Primary Goods**: Food (kelp, fish), bio-materials, living coral
**Traffic Volume**: 50+ convoys daily

### The Crystal Express (Aethermoor Primary)
**Route**: Crystalhaven → Ironhold → The Sundered Peaks → Resonance Peaks → Return
**Elevation Range**: Sea level to 8,000+ feet
**Distance**: 600 miles
**Transit Method**: Airships, crystal flight, mountain passes
**Primary Goods**: Raw crystals, refined aether, sky metals
**Traffic Volume**: 30+ airships daily

1. **Port Meridian Dual Harbor**: 60% of inter-realm trade

3. **Crystal Flight Hub** (Crystalhaven): 50% of high-value Aethermoor trade

**Aethermoor**:
- Crystal storms: Close airship routes for 2-5 days
- Mountain weather: Block passes for 1-10 days seasonally
- Magical fluctuations: Disrupt crystal flight networks

**Aquabyssos**:
- Memory tides: Cause navigation confusion for 1-3 days

**Cross-Realm**:
