# Wooden Staff

## Overview
**Price**: 5 gp

**Weight**: 4 lb.

**Druidic Focus.** A [[Druidic Focus|druidic focus]] might be a [[Sprig of Mistletoe|sprig of mistletoe]] or holly, a [[Wand|wand]] or scepter made of yew or another [[3 - Weapons|special]] wood, a [[Staff|staff]] drawn whole out of a living tree, or a [[Totem|totem]] object incorporating feathers, fur, bones, and teeth from sacred animals. A [[Druid|druid]] can use such an object as a [[7 - Spellcasting|spellcasting]] focus.
