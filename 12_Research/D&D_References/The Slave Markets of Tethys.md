# The Slave Markets of Tethys
*Where Human Lives Become Currency*

## Overview
The Slave Markets of Tethys serve as the primary hub for human trafficking operations controlled by [[The Crimson Fleet|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Crimson Fleet]], supplying forced labor to [[The Iron Pits of Malachar|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Iron Pits Of Malachar]], [[The Crimson Foundries|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Crimson Foundries]], and [[The Bone Colosseum|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Bone Colosseum]].
