## Overview
[[Vex Shadowthorn|02 Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn]]'s apprentices represent the future of [[The Whisper Syndicate|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Whisper Syndicate]]. Handpicked for their unique talents and moral flexibility, these five individuals have been shaped by Vex's ruthless mentorship and complex philosophy. Each apprentice specializes in different aspects of criminal enterprise while sharing core skills in deception, combat, and survival.

### Kira "Bloodrose" Nightfall
*Half-Elf Rogue (Arcane Trickster) Level 8*

**Appearance**: Striking red hair, golden eyes, intricate rose tattoos that seem to move
**Specialty**: Infiltration and seduction
**Background**: Former noble's daughter who faked her death to escape an arranged marriage

**Stats**: AC 16, HP 52, Speed 30 ft
**STR** 10, **DEX** 18, **CON** 14, **INT** 16, **WIS** 12, **CHA** 15

**Key Abilities**:
- Sneak Attack 4d6
- Mage Hand Legerdemain
- Enchantment magic specialization
- Perfect memory for faces and conversations

**Relationships**:
- [[Vex Shadowthorn|02 Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn]] - Surrogate parent figure, complex devotion
- [[Prince Caspian Duskwater|02 Worldbuilding/People/Prince Caspian Duskwater]] - Former lover, now information source
- [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/The Gilded Serpent|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Gilded Serpent]] - Manages operations there
- **Marcus "The Shade" Blackwood** - Mentor in combat techniques

**Secrets**:
- Still legally alive and heir to a major fortune
- Has feelings for Vex that go beyond professional admiration
- Maintains contact with her "dead" identity through coded letters

### Thane Deepwatch
*Triton Warlock (Great Old One) Level 7*

**Appearance**: Pale blue skin, silver gills, eyes that reflect depths
**Specialty**: Aquabyssos operations and eldritch magic
**Background**: Exiled from [[Aquabyssos|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aquabyssos]] for forbidden research

**Stats**: AC 15, HP 45, Speed 30 ft, Swim 30 ft
**STR** 12, **DEX** 14, **CON** 16, **INT** 15, **WIS** 13, **CHA** 17

**Key Abilities**:
- Eldritch Blast with repelling and agonizing invocations
- Telepathic communication with aquatic creatures
- Create Water and Control Water spells
- Pressure resistance and underwater breathing

**Relationships**:
- [[Ambassador Nerida Deepcurrent|02 Worldbuilding/People/Ambassador Nerida Deepcurrent]] - Former academic colleague
- [[The Biomancers|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Biomancers]] - Uneasy alliance through research
- [[Duchess Marina Ever-Drowning|02 Worldbuilding/People/Duchess Marina Ever Drowning]] - Secretly reports to her
- Deep sea entities - Patron relationship

**Secrets**:
- His exile was staged to provide Syndicate access to Aquabyssos
- Patron is connected to the same entity that empowers Vex
- Has discovered the location of three sunken treasure ships

### "Whisper" (True name unknown)
*Changeling Monk (Shadow) Level 6*

**Appearance**: Changes constantly, default form is androgynous with silver hair
**Specialty**: Assassination and information gathering
**Background**: Orphan raised by the Syndicate from infancy

**Stats**: AC 16, HP 42, Speed 40 ft
**STR** 8, **DEX** 18, **CON** 14, **INT** 14, **WIS** 16, **CHA** 12

**Key Abilities**:
- Shapechanger ability with perfect mimicry
- Shadow Step teleportation
- Stunning Strike (save DC 13)
- Pass Without Trace and Silence spells

**Relationships**:
- [[Vex Shadowthorn|02 Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn]] - Only parental figure they've ever known
- **Kira Nightfall** - Closest thing to a sibling
- [[The Shadow Warren|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Shadow Warren]] - Operates primarily from here
- Unknown biological family - searching for origins

**Secrets**:
- Has adopted over 200 different identities
- Believes they might be the child of a high-ranking noble
- Keeps a journal of every person they've killed, with drawings

### Jin "Sparkfingers" Goldwright
*Halfling Artificer (Alchemist) Level 7*

**Appearance**: Curly brown hair, burn scars on hands, always smells of chemicals
**Specialty**: Explosives, poisons, and magical devices
**Background**: Former apprentice to a legitimate alchemist until an "accident"

**Stats**: AC 17, HP 49, Speed 25 ft
**STR** 8, **DEX** 16, **CON** 16, **INT** 18, **WIS** 14, **CHA** 10

**Key Abilities**:
- Experimental Elixirs with various effects
- Magical Tinkering for surveillance devices
- Expertise in Alchemist's Supplies and Thieves' Tools
- Immunity to own poisons

**Relationships**:
- [[Master Artificer Korvin Gearwright|02 Worldbuilding/People/Master Artificer Korvin Gearwright]] - Former master, now enemy
- [[The Current Merchants|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Current Merchants]] - Supplies exotic materials
- **Dr. Helena Voidwalker** - Respects her work but opposes her ethics
- Underground fighting rings - Provides enhancement drugs

**Secrets**:
- The "accident" that ended their legitimate career was intentional
- Has been slowly poisoning a rival gang leader for six months
- Keeps detailed notes on everyone's weaknesses, including other apprentices

### Raven Stormcrow
*Aarakocra Ranger (Gloom Stalker) Level 6*

**Appearance**: Black feathers with silver edges, piercing yellow eyes
**Specialty**: Reconnaissance, tracking, and long-range elimination
**Background**: Sole survivor of a tribe destroyed by crystal corruption

**Stats**: AC 16, HP 48, Speed 30 ft, Fly 50 ft
**STR** 14, **DEX** 18, **CON** 14, **INT** 12, **WIS** 16, **CHA** 8

**Key Abilities**:
- Favored Enemy: Corrupted creatures and nobles
- Natural Explorer: Urban and coastal environments
- Umbral Sight for invisible-in-darkness stealth
- Archery fighting style with +2 to ranged attacks

**Relationships**:
- [[Vex Shadowthorn|02 Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn]] - Saved their life, fierce loyalty
- [[The Verdant Accord|02 Worldbuilding/People/The Verdant Accord]] - Secret sympathy for their cause
- **Admiral Cassandra Stormwind** - Respects her anti-corruption stance
- Sky traders and message services - Information network

**Secrets**:
- Can understand the speech of corrupted crystals (traumatic ability)
- Has mapped every rooftop and sky-accessible window in three cities
- Plans to eventually kill everyone responsible for their tribe's destruction

### Hierarchy
1. **Kira "Bloodrose" Nightfall** - Unofficial leader and Vex's favorite
2. **Thane Deepwatch** - Strategic planner and Aquabyssos specialist
3. **"Whisper"** - Most trusted for sensitive operations
4. **Jin "Sparkfingers" Goldwright** - Technical support and logistics
5. **Raven Stormcrow** - Reconnaissance and backup muscle

### Active Assignments
1. **The Noble's Gambit** - Blackmailing [[Lord Aurelius Goldwave|02 Worldbuilding/People/Lord Aurelius Goldwave]]
2. **Deep Current Smuggling** - Moving corrupted crystals through Aquabyssos
3. **Festival Preparation** - Setting up for major heist during Crystal Festival
4. **Rival Elimination** - Systematic destruction of competing criminal organization
5. **Information Harvest** - Infiltrating [[The Crown Authority|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Crown Authority]]
