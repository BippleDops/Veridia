## Description
While incense is burning in this censer, you can use an action to speak the censer’s command word and summon an [[Elemental, Air|air elemental]], as if you had cast the **[[Conjure Elemental|conjure elemental]]** spell. The censer can’t be used this way again until the next dawn.

This 6-inch-wide, 1-foot-high vessel resembles a chalice with a decorated lid. It weighs 1 pound.
