## Special Traits
***Dive Attack.*** If the quetzalcoatlus is flying and dives at least 30 feet toward a target and then hits with a bite attack, the attack deals an extra 10 (`dice: 3d6`) damage to the target.

***Flyby.*** The quetzalcoatlus doesn't provoke an opportunity attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

## Actions
***Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 12 (`dice: 3d6+2`) piercing damage.

## Description
This giant relative of the pteranodon has a wingspan exceeding 30 feet. Although it can move on the ground like a quadruped, it is more comfortable in the air.
