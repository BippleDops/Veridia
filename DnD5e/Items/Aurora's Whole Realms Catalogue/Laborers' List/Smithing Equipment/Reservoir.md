**Weight**: -

## Description
In a work room filled with fire and red-hot [[Metal|metal]], a [[Water Reservoir|water reservoir]] is a welcome tool. Our smithing reservoir is topped by a 50-gallon water barrel that empties into a broad [[Clay|clay]] trough long enough for a two-handed sword to be submerged. The pooling end of the trough contains a valve that allows the water to be drained after it becomes fouled. (50 gp)

## Additional Details
requisite for using one of the three smithing proficiencies
