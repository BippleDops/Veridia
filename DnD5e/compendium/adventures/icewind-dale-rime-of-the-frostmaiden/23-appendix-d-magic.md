# Appendix D: Magic
*Source: Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden, p. 314* 

This appendix describes the new magic items, spellbooks, and spells found in this adventure.

New magic items found in this adventure are presented below in alphabetical order.

- [Abracadabrus](/compendium/items/abracadabrus-idrotf.md)
- [Cauldron of Plenty](/compendium/items/cauldron-of-plenty-idrotf.md)
- [Hook of Fisher's Delight](/compendium/items/hook-of-fishers-delight-idrotf.md)
- [Lantern of Tracking](/compendium/items/lantern-of-tracking-idrotf.md)
- [Professor Orb](/compendium/items/professor-orb-wdmm.md)
- [Professor Skant](/compendium/items/professor-skant-idrotf.md)
- [Psi Crystal](/compendium/items/psi-crystal-idrotf.md)

- [Scroll of the Comet](/compendium/items/scroll-of-the-comet-idrotf.md)
- [Thermal Cube](/compendium/items/thermal-cube-idrotf.md)
- [Ythryn Mythallar](/compendium/items/ythryn-mythallar-idrotf.md)

## Books

Two books are described in this section. Characters can find [The Codicil of White](/compendium/items/the-codicil-of-white-idrotf.md) in Auril's abode (see chapter 5) and [The Incantations of Iriolarthas](/compendium/items/the-incantations-of-iriolarthas-idrotf.md) in the Netherese necropolis hidden under the Reghed Glacier (see chapter 7).

## Wizard Spells

Characters who obtain [The Codicil of White](/compendium/items/the-codicil-of-white-idrotf.md) from Auril's abode or [The Incantations of Iriolarthas](/compendium/items/the-incantations-of-iriolarthas-idrotf.md) in Ythryn can discover one or more of the following wizard spells.

- [Blade of Disaster](/compendium/spells/blade-of-disaster-tce.md)
- [Create Magen](/compendium/spells/create-magen-idrotf.md)
- [Frost Fingers](/compendium/spells/frost-fingers-idrotf.md)
