## Description
While you wear this cloak, it projects an [[Illusion|illusion]] that makes you appear to be standing in a place near your actual location, causing any creature to have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|disadvantage]] on [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] rolls against you. If you take damage, the property ceases to function until the start of your next turn. This property is suppressed while you are [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]], [[Restrained|restrained]], or otherwise unable to move.
