# Phantom Plow (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *cantrip*

**School**: *Evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: Self (50-foot line)

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You create a deep furrow in the ground, suitable for planting, that extends from your feet to up to 50 feet away. It will not work on earth that is consecrated or under a long-term spell.

You create additional furrows when you reach 5th level (2 furrows), 11th level (4 fur-rows), and 17th level (8 furrows).
