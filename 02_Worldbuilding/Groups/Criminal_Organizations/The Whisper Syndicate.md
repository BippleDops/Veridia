# The Whisper Syndicate
*In Shadows We Trust, In Secrets We Trade*

## Overview
The Whisper Syndicate is [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor]]'s most powerful criminal organization, dealing in corrupted crystals, blackmail, and forbidden knowledge. Under [[02_Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn|02 Worldbuilding/People/Vex Shadowthorn]]'s leadership, they've evolved from common criminals into a shadow government that knows everyone's secrets and profits from the kingdom's corruption.

## Criminal Empire

### Core Operations
- **Crystal Trafficking**: Corrupted and illegal crystals
- **Blackmail**: Information on every important figure
- **Assassination**: Selective and expensive
- **Smuggling**: Bypass all trade restrictions
- **Corruption**: Bribe officials at every level

### Legal Fronts
- **The Gilded Serpent**: Casino in [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Port Meridian|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Port Meridian]]
- **Shadowthorn Trading**: Import/export company
- **The Velvet Room**: High-end entertainment
- **Crystal Remedies**: "Medical" crystal supplies
- **The Quiet Hand**: "Security" services

## The Shadow Code

### Rules
1. Never betray the Syndicate
2. The Whisper's word is law
3. Pay your debts or pay in blood
4. Information is more valuable than gold
5. Dead men tell no tales - unless we need them to

### Punishments
- **Betrayal**: Death, family killed
- **Failure**: Demotion, torture
- **Skimming**: Loss of hand
- **Talking**: Tongue removed
- **Desertion**: Hunted forever
