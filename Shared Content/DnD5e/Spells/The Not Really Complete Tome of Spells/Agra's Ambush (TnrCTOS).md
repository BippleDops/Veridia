# Agra's Ambush (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *2nd-level*

**School**: *abjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (roughly-ground glass, which must be sprinkled in a circle around the recipient)

**Duration**: 1 hour


## Effects
You provide protection for yourself or one willing creature against unwanted mental intrusion. The trap will be triggered if a protected creature be affected by a spell or effect that would sense its emotions, read its thoughts, or put the charmed condition on it. The attacker must make a Wisdom saving throw. The attacker takes 3d10 psychic dam-age on a failed saving throw, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Once this spell has been triggered, it ends.
