# Fertility (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a seed)

**Duration**: 1 week


## Effects
You touch a willing creature; for the du-ration, that creature becomes incredibly fertile. You may not use this spell on yourself, although you can use it on your partner.
