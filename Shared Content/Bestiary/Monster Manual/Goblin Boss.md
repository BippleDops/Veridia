## Special Traits
***Nimble Escape.*** The goblin can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

## Actions
***Multiattack.*** The goblin makes two attacks with its scimitar. The second attack has disadvantage.

***Scimitar.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (`dice: 1d6+2`) slashing damage.

***Javelin.*** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (`dice: 1d6`) piercing damage.

## Reactions
***Redirect Attack.*** When a creature the goblin can see targets it with an attack, the goblin chooses another goblin within 5 feet of it. The two goblins swap places, and the chosen goblin becomes the target instead.
