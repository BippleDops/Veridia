# Lolth's Sting
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/03_Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 200 gp

A creature subjected to Lolth's Sting must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
