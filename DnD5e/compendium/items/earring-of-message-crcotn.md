# Earring of Message
*Wondrous Item, common*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

The blue crystal of this earring is wrapped with delicate copper wire. The earring has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and cast the [message](/compendium/spells/message.md) spell. The earring regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep p. 212*
