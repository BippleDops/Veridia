The whispers saved William's life by guiding him to safety, but they never stopped. Over the months following his rescue, William discovered that he could no longer distinguish between his own thoughts and the voices that constantly murmured in his mind. In exchange for this burden, he gained the ability to access information from across time and space, making him one of the most valuable and dangerous oracles in [[Aquabyssos. ### The Whispering Chamber

The heart of the shrine, where William conducts his most important prophetic sessions. This circular room has been acoustically designed to amplify and focus the whispers that only William can fully hear. The walls are inscribed with fragments of prophecies, creating a constantly changing tapestry of past predictions and future warnings. **Unique Features:**
