# Vex Nightwhisper

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 30
**Occupation**: Leader of the Shadowhaven Smugglers Ring
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Shadowhaven]]

## Description
Vex Nightwhisper is the charismatic and cunning leader of the Shadowhaven Smugglers Ring. He is a master of disguise and is known for his elaborate schemes and his ability to stay one step ahead of the law.

## GM Notes
- His true identity is Lord Vexton Shadowmere, the presumed dead heir of a noble family.
- He is a rival of [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Whisper Jack]] and the Thieves Guild.
- He is a potential ally for the party, but he is also very dangerous and should not be trusted.
