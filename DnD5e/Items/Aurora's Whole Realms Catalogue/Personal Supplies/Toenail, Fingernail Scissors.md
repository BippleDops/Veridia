## Description
These fine steel scissors, perfect for precise cutting tasks, are crafted by the metalsmiths of Procampur. A set costs just 4 gp. Decorative handles cost extra: mother-of-pearl (add 1 gp), silver (add 1 gp), gold (add 2 gp), or platinum (add 5 gp). Decorative etching costs 1 gp more, and small gems add 2 gp.
