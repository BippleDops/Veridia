# The Bone Palace of Sorrows
*Where Death Holds Court and Eternity Begins*

## Overview
The Bone Palace serves as the seat of [[The Carrion Court]], an elaborate fortress constructed from the bones of ancient leviathans and ruled by undead nobility who maintain the twisted traditions of aristocratic society beyond death.
