# Marianne Crowley

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Female
**Age**: 35
**Occupation**: Tavern Keeper
**Location**: [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/The Salty Anchor Tavern.md]]

## Description
Marianne Crowley is a tough, no-nonsense woman who has seen it all. She is the owner of The Salty Anchor Tavern and is fiercely protective of her customers and her business.

## GM Notes
- She is a good source of information, but she will not give it away for free.
- She has a soft spot for underdogs and will often help those in need.
- She is secretly a retired adventurer.
