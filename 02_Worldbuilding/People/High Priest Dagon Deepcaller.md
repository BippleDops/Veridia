# High Priest Dagon Deepcaller
*Prophet of the Depths, Voice of the Leviathans*

## Overview
High Priest Dagon Deepcaller is the supreme leader of [[The Leviathan Cult|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Leviathan Cult]], a horrifying hybrid of human and aboleth created through dark rituals and generations of selective breeding. He serves as the primary conduit between the sleeping Leviathan Lords and their mortal worshippers, using his telepathic abilities and divine magic to coordinate cult operations across the oceans. His ultimate goal is to awaken the primordial sea gods and return the world to an aquatic paradise.

## Physical Appearance
- **Base Form**: Humanoid with slimy, translucent skin
- **Size**: 7 feet tall with elongated limbs
- **Tentacles**: Four facial appendages around a lamprey-like mouth
- **Eyes**: Large, black, and capable of seeing in absolute darkness
- **Skin**: Constantly secretes mucus that allows underwater breathing
- **Clothing**: Ceremonial robes made from living coral and sea anemones

## Hybrid Abilities
- **Amphibious**: Can survive indefinitely on land or underwater
- **Telepathic Communication**: 120-foot range, can contact other aboleth hybrids globally
- **Mucus Cloud**: Secretes disease-causing slime in water
- **Tentacle Strike**: Melee attacks that can enslave targets
- **Deep Speech**: Speaks the primordial language of sea monsters

## Background & History
- Born from union of cult priestess and captured aboleth
- Raised in underwater temple complexes since birth
- First hybrid to successfully channel divine magic from Leviathan Lords
- Unified scattered sea cults under single organization
- Has been alive for 200+ years due to aboleth longevity

### Driving Goals
1. **Primary**: Awaken all four Leviathan Lords simultaneously
2. **Secondary**: Convert surface populations to hybrid servants

### Fears & Weaknesses

- [[Crystal Wardens]]' holy magic can disrupt his divine connections
- Potential rebellion from fully-converted aboleth hybrids
- Ancient binding magics that keep Leviathan Lords dormant
