## Description
An old saying from the Pirate Isles goes, “The oars in me lifeboat’s worth their weights in gold.” But we don’t charge that much. Our paddles and oars come in 5 ft (1 gp) and 8 ft lengths (2 gp). We also sell oarlocks (1 gp/pr) that can accommodate any of our oars.
