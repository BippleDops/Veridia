# Charm of Biting Cold
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
This charm has 3 charges. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 of the charm's charges to wreathe your weapon attacks with biting cold for 1 minute. Until this effect ends, you deal an extra `dice: 1d6` cold damage when you hit with a melee or ranged weapon attack. Once all its charges have been expended, this charm vanishes from you.
