**Weight**: -

## Description
These fine, braided linen wicks come in two sizes, thin, for [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue/Household Accoutrements/Candles|candles]], and thick, for oil [[Lanterns|lanterns]] (1 cp per yard for thin, and 1 cp per 2 feet for thick). At least four feet of the thin wicking is required for the [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items (WOTC)/Candle|candle]] mold.
