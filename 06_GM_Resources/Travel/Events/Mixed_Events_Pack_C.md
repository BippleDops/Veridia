# Mixed Events Pack C — 50 Ready-to-Run Encounters (X-201–X-250)

Complements Packs A and B. Use with core systems and vehicle rules.

---

### X-201: Synchronized Festival Across Worlds
- Trigger: Shared holiday; parallel parades.
- Checks: Diplomacy DC 15; Tactics DC 14 for crowd control; Sanity DC 13 vs. crowd dreams.
- Outcomes: Success: Faction +2; Sanity +1. Partial: Minor scuffle. Fail: Riot; scandal risk.

### X-202: Starfall Through Trench Window
- Trigger: Meteor shower falls into sea via portal.
- Checks: Helm DC 16; Gunnery DC 15; Anchorite DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Harvest shards (200–600 gp); Integrity stable. Partial: Minor damage 1d6. Fail: 2d6; Integrity -1.

### X-203: Anchor Duel Across Portal
- Trigger: Two factions tune anchors in contest.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 15; Performance DC 14; Tactics DC 14.
- Outcomes: Success: Ally wins; Integrity +1; standing +1. Partial: Draw. Fail: Opponent wins; -1 standing.

### X-204: Parasite Cloud Shepherded into Jetstream
- Trigger: Pirates guide bloom into air lane.
- Checks: Engineer DC 15 purge; Helm DC 15 avoid.
- Outcomes: Success: Safe; samples 100–300 gp. Partial: Minor infections. Fail: Boarders and sickness.

### X-205: Dream Barge Convoy Exchange
- Trigger: Sea convoy trades with sky dream barges.
- Checks: Performance DC 14; Sanity DC 14; Economy DC 14.
- Outcomes: Success: Route time -25%; Sanity +1; profit. Partial: No gain. Fail: Identity bleed.
