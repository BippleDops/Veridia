--- # Cone [Area of Effect]

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 364. Available in the SRD and the Free Rules (2024)* A Cone is an area of effect that extends in straight lines from a point of origin in a direction its creator chooses. A Cone's width at any point along its length is equal to that point's distance from the point of origin. For example, a Cone is 15 feet wide at a point along its length that is 15 feet from the point of origin. The effect that creates a Cone specifies its maximum length.
