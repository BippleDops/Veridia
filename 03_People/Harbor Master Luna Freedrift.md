## Overview
Harbor Master Luna Freedrift controls [[Places, the largest free port in [Aquabyssos World Overview]]. Officially appointed by the [[Parliament of Echoes, she secretly serves as a key leader in [Free Current|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/Free Current]], using her position to protect refugees, coordinate resistance activities, and maintain the only truly neutral trading post in the underwater kingdom. Her harbor serves as sanctuary for those fleeing political persecution, while her information network rivals that of the greatest spymasters.

### Special Abilities
**Harbor Authority.** Within Freedom Harbor, Luna can command the assistance of harbor guards, customs officials, and loyal merchants. She has legal authority to detain ships and arrest individuals.

**Information Network.** Luna can use her action to consult her network of informants, functioning as a *legend lore* spell with no casting time or material components (3/day).

**Sea-Born Heritage.** Luna can breathe underwater indefinitely and has advantage on all swimming checks. She can communicate simple concepts with sea creatures.

**Sneak Attack.** Luna deals an extra 21 (`dice: `dice: 6d6``) damage when she hits with a weapon attack and has advantage on the roll, or when her target is within 5 feet of an ally.

**Cunning Action.** Luna can take a Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as a bonus action.

**Evasion.** When Luna is subjected to an effect that allows a Dexterity save for half damage, she takes no damage on a success and half damage on a failure.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Luna makes two attacks with her rapier or shortbow, or she can make one attack and use a cunning action.

**Rapier of Harbor Authority.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (`dice: `dice: 1d8`` + 5) piercing damage plus 7 (`dice: `dice: 2d6``) additional damage if target is trespassing in her harbor.

**Shortbow +1.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (`dice: `dice: 1d6`` + 6) piercing damage.

**Harbor Master's Command (3/Day).** Luna issues an authoritative command to one creature within 60 feet. Functions as *command* spell (save **DC 18**) but works on any creature that recognizes her authority.

**Call for Backup (1/Day).** Luna signals for assistance. `dice: `dice: 2d4`` harbor guards arrive in `dice: `dice: 1d4`` rounds to assist her.
