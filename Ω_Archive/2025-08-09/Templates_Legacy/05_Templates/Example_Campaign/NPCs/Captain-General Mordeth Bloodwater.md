# Captain-General Mordeth Bloodwater

## General Info
**Race**: Half-Orc
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 50
**Occupation**: Captain-General of the Ironhold Fortress
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Ironhold Fortress]]

## Description
Captain-General Bloodwater is a brutal and efficient military commander who is responsible for the defense of the Ironhold Fortress. He is a man of few words, but his actions speak for themselves.

## GM Notes
- He is a potential ally for the party, but he is also very suspicious of outsiders.
- He is a rival of [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Admiral Cassius Stormhaven]] and the Shadowhaven Navy.
- He is secretly a member of the Lords' Alliance.
