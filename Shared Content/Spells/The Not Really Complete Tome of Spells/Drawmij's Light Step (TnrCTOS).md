# Drawmij's Light Step (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a bit of cat fur and a duck feather)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
You or one willing creature you touch is granted a the ability to walk at a normal pace without leaving tracks or disturbing the ground below the it. The creature can also walk across relatively calm fluids at normal speed.
