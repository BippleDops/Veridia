# Acid (vial)

## Overview
**Price**: 25 gp

**Weight**: 1 lb.

**Acid.** As an action, you can splash the contents of this [[Vial|vial]] onto a creature within 5 feet of you or throw the vial up to 20 feet, shattering it on impact. In either case, make a ranged [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] against a creature or object, treating the acid as an [[3 - Weapons|improvised]] weapon. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 [[17 - Damage and Healing|acid]] damage.
