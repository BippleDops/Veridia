## Description
Not only smiths, but anyone who would [[Forge|forge]] his or her own armor or [[Metal|metal]] weaponry needs a solid anvil. We offer a solid steel 70 lb anvil (45 gp) suitable for smaller weapons and armor plates as well as a full sized 160 lb anvil (95 gp) for any smithing task.

## Additional Details
access to one such anvil is vital for the three proficiencies listed above
