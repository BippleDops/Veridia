## Description
These [[Crystal|crystal]] lenses fit over the eyes. They have 3 charges. While wearing them, you can expend 1 charge as an action to cast the **[[Charm Person|charm person]]** spell (save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 13) on a humanoid within 30 feet of you, provided that you and the target can see each other. The lenses regain all expended charges daily at dawn.
