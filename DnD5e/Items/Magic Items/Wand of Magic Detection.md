# Wand of Magic Detection

**Type**: Magic Item

## Description
This [[Wand|wand]] has 3 charges. While holding it, you can expend 1 charge as an action to cast the **[[Detect Magic|Detect Magic]]** spell from it. The wand regains `dice: 1d3` (1d3) expended charges daily at dawn.
