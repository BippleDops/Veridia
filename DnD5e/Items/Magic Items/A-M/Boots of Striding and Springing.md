## Description
While you wear these boots, your walking speed becomes 30 feet, unless your walking speed is higher, and your speed isn’t reduced if you are [[Strength|encumbered]] or wearing [[3 - Weapons|heavy]] [[2 - Armor|armor]]. In addition, you can [[Jump|jump]] three times the normal distance, though you can’t jump farther than your remaining [[8 - Movement|movement]] would allow.
