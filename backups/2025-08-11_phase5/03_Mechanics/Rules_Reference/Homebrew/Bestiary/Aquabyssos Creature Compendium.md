# Aquabyssos Creature Compendium
## Unique Horrors of the Drowned Realm

> *"The creatures of Aquabyssos aren't just adapted to the depths—they're symptoms of a reality gone wrong."*

## Shadow Entities

### Shadow Separator
*Medium aberration, neutral evil*

**Armor Class** 16 (incorporeal)
**Hit Points** 84 (13d8 + 26)
**Speed** 0 ft., fly 40 ft. (hover)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 6 (-2) | 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 15 (+2) | 17 (+3) |

**Damage Resistances** acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Damage Immunities** necrotic, poison
**Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** telepathy 60 ft.
**Challenge** 7 (2,900 XP)

***Incorporeal Movement.*** The separator can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

***Shadow Sight.*** Magical darkness doesn't impede the separator's darkvision.

***Shadow Liberation.*** When the separator hits a creature with its Shadow Pull, that creature's shadow gains independence for 1 hour.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The separator makes two shadow pull attacks.

***Shadow Pull.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) necrotic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or have its shadow partially separated. A creature with a separated shadow has disadvantage on Charisma checks and saving throws.

***Complete Separation (Recharge 5-6).*** The separator targets one creature within 30 feet whose shadow is partially separated. The target must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature's shadow becomes completely independent for 24 hours, acting as a Shadow (MM p.269) that is initially hostile to its original owner.

### Memory Shepherd
*Large aberration, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 127 (15d10 + 45)
**Speed** 30 ft., swim 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) |

**Skills** Insight +9, Perception +9
**Damage Resistances** psychic
**Senses** truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 19
**Languages** telepathy 120 ft., but cannot speak
**Challenge** 9 (5,000 XP)

***Memory Form.*** The shepherd appears as a translucent humanoid filled with swirling images of memories. Any creature that can see the shepherd can glimpse random memories within it.

***Memory Sense.*** The shepherd knows the location of any creature within 120 feet that has memories.

***Psychic Defense.*** The shepherd has advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The shepherd makes two memory drain attacks or one memory drain and one memory flood.

***Memory Drain.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 14 (2d10 + 3) psychic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or lose one significant memory (DM's choice). The shepherd gains temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt.

***Memory Flood.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +8 to hit, range 60 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target experiences 1d4 random memories from the shepherd's collection and must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be stunned until the end of the shepherd's next turn.

***Harvest Memory (Recharge 5-6).*** The shepherd targets one creature within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw or have all memories of the last 24 hours transferred to the shepherd. The creature forgets everything that happened in that time period.

**Legendary Actions**

The shepherd can take 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The shepherd regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

**Project Memory.** The shepherd forces one creature within 60 feet to experience a traumatic memory. The target must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of the shepherd's next turn.

**Memory Shield.** The shepherd gains resistance to all damage until the start of its next turn.

## Depth Horrors

### Pressure Wraith
*Medium undead, chaotic evil*

**Armor Class** 14
**Hit Points** 67 (9d8 + 27)
**Speed** 0 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover), swim 60 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 6 (-2) | 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 15 (+2) |

**Damage Resistances** acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Damage Immunities** necrotic, poison
**Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** understands all languages it knew in life but can't speak
**Challenge** 6 (2,300 XP)

***Depth Dweller.*** The wraith can exist at any depth without suffering pressure effects.

***Pressure Aura.*** Any creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the wraith must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or suffer one level of exhaustion from pressure sickness.

***Incorporeal Movement.*** The wraith can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain.

**Actions**

***Life Drain.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 21 (4d8 + 3) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or gain 1d4 Adaptation Points as its body begins transforming to survive the depths.

***Pressure Burst (Recharge 5-6).*** The wraith releases a burst of crushing pressure in a 20-foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 28 (8d6) force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Creatures that fail the save are also stunned until the end of the wraith's next turn.

### Leviathan Parasite
*Huge monstrosity, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 184 (16d12 + 80)
**Speed** 10 ft., swim 50 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 23 (+6) | 11 (+0) | 21 (+5) | 4 (-3) | 12 (+1) | 6 (-2) |

**Skills** Perception +5
**Damage Immunities** acid
**Senses** blindsight 30 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 15
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 11 (7,200 XP)

***Amphibious.*** The parasite can breathe air and water.

***Divine Flesh Eater.*** The parasite has advantage on attack rolls against creatures made of divine flesh or those with divine heritage.

***Legendary Resistance (2/Day).*** If the parasite fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Blood Frenzy.*** The parasite has advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its hit points.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The parasite makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its tentacles.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 25 (3d12 + 6) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) acid damage. If the target is a Huge or larger creature or structure, the parasite can choose to attach itself. While attached, the parasite doesn't attack but deals 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of its turns.

***Tentacle.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 10 feet toward the parasite.

***Acid Spray (Recharge 5-6).*** The parasite spits acid in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 56 (16d6) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

### Consciousness Coral
*Large plant, neutral*

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 114 (12d10 + 48)
**Speed** 0 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 1 (-5) | 1 (-5) | 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) |

**Damage Vulnerabilities** fire
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing
**Damage Immunities** psychic
**Condition Immunities** blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, prone
**Senses** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 12
**Languages** telepathy 120 ft.
**Challenge** 8 (3,900 XP)

***False Appearance.*** While the coral remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from normal coral.

***Collective Consciousness.*** The coral contains the consciousnesses of 3d6 absorbed creatures. It can communicate using their voices and memories.

***Psychic Absorption.*** When a creature dies within 30 feet of the coral, that creature's consciousness is absorbed into the coral collective.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The coral makes two psychic lash attacks.

***Psychic Lash.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +7 to hit, range 60 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 18 (4d8) psychic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or have disadvantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws until the end of the coral's next turn.

***Consciousness Overload (Recharge 5-6).*** The coral bombards all creatures within 30 feet with the thoughts and memories of its absorbed consciousnesses. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 45 (10d8) psychic damage and is stunned for 1 minute. On a success, a creature takes half damage and isn't stunned. A stunned creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

***Spawn Polyp (1/Day).*** The coral creates a smaller version of itself within 10 feet. The polyp has 25 hit points, AS 11, and can make one psychic lash attack per turn.

## Recursion Entities

### Fractal Spider
*Medium aberration, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 93 (11d8 + 44)
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 19 (+4) | 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 8 (-1) |

**Skills** Stealth +8
**Senses** blindsight 10 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** —
**Challenge** 7 (2,900 XP)

***Recursive Existence.*** The spider exists at multiple scales simultaneously. When it takes damage, roll a d6. On a 5-6, a smaller version of the spider (Tiny, 1/4 its stats) appears in an adjacent space.

***Spider Climb.*** The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

***Web Sense.*** While in contact with a web, the spider knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.

***Dimensional Web.*** The spider's webs exist across multiple recursion levels simultaneously.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The spider makes two bite attacks.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target shrinks to 1/10 its size and is stable but paralyzed for 1 hour.

***Fractal Web (Recharge 5-6).*** The spider creates a 20-foot cube of webbing at a point it can see within 60 feet. The webs are difficult terrain and lightly obscure the area. Each creature that starts its turn in the webs or enters them during its turn must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is restrained and experiences recursive vision, seeing infinite smaller versions of itself trapped in infinite smaller webs (disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks while restrained).

## Memory Entities

### The Forgotten One
*Medium aberration, neutral*

**Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 135 (18d8 + 54)
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 16 (+3) | 20 (+5) | 18 (+4) | 21 (+5) |

**Saving Throws** Int +9, Wis +8, Cha +9
**Skills** Deception +9, Insight +8, Persuasion +9
**Damage Immunities** psychic
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened
**Senses** truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 14
**Languages** all, telepathy 120 ft.
**Challenge** 12 (8,400 XP)

***Antimemetic Nature.*** The Forgotten One cannot be remembered. Creatures forget its existence 1 minute after encountering it unless they succeed on a DC 17 Intelligence saving throw. Written records of the creature fade within 1 hour.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If the Forgotten One fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Memory Thief.*** The Forgotten One knows all forgotten memories within 1 mile of its location.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The Forgotten One makes three attacks: one with memory erasure and two with psychic blast.

***Memory Erasure.*** *Melee Spell Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target must make a DC 17 Intelligence saving throw. On a failure, the target forgets the last 10 minutes of their life. On a critical failure, they forget the last 24 hours.

***Psychic Blast.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +9 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 22 (5d8) psychic damage.

***Erase from History (Recharge 6).*** The Forgotten One targets one creature it can see within 60 feet. The target must make a DC 17 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, all creatures except the target forget the target exists for 1 hour. During this time, the target is invisible to all creatures affected by this ability and cannot interact with them in any way.

**Legendary Actions**

The Forgotten One can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below.

**Move.** The Forgotten One moves up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Psychic Attack.** The Forgotten One makes one psychic blast attack.

**Forget Me (Costs 2 Actions).** All creatures within 30 feet must make a DC 17 Intelligence saving throw or forget the Forgotten One's location until the start of its next turn.

**Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned
**Senses** blindsight 60 ft., passive Perception 10

***Immutable Form.*** The titan is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If the titan fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Siege Monster.*** The titan deals double damage to objects and structures.

**Move.** The titan moves up to half its speed.

**Slam (Costs 2 Actions).** The titan makes one slam attack.

**Immune Surge (Costs 3 Actions).** The titan regains 50 hit points and removes any conditions affecting it.

## Unique Bosses

### The Infinite Gardener
*Large aberration, lawful neutral*

**Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 172 (15d10 + 90)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 22 (+6) | 25 (+7) | 21 (+5) | 19 (+4) |

**Saving Throws** Int +12, Wis +10, Cha +9
**Skills** Arcana +12, Nature +12, Perception +10
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Damage Immunities** psychic
**Senses** truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 20
**Languages** all, telepathy 120 ft.
**Challenge** 16 (15,000 XP)

***Recursive Existence.*** The Gardener exists at all recursion levels simultaneously. It cannot be killed unless destroyed at all levels in the same round.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If the Gardener fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

***Scale Mastery.*** The Gardener can change its size category as a bonus action, becoming Tiny, Small, Medium, Large, or Huge.

***Reality Shaper.*** The area within 120 feet of the Gardener follows dream logic. The Gardener can reshape terrain, create or destroy objects, and alter physical laws as an action.

**Actions**

***Multiattack.*** The Gardener makes three attacks with its pruning shears.

***Pruning Shears.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (3d8 + 4) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage. On a critical hit, the target loses a memory, ability, or feature (DM's choice) for 24 hours.

***Plant Recursion Seed (Recharge 5-6).*** The Gardener plants a seed that immediately grows into a crystal tree. Any creature within 20 feet of the tree must make a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or be drawn into the tree's fruit, entering a recursion level where they must face a smaller version of the current encounter.

***Harvest Reality (1/Day).*** The Gardener attempts to harvest a 30-foot cube of reality, including all creatures and objects within it. Everything in the area must make a DC 20 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, they are transported to a recursion level of the Gardener's choice. On a success, they take 44 (8d10) force damage instead.

**Legendary Actions**

The Gardener can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below.

**Prune.** The Gardener makes one pruning shears attack.

**Recursive Step.** The Gardener teleports to any location it can see, appearing from a different recursion level.

**Reality Warp (Costs 2 Actions).** The Gardener changes one aspect of reality within 60 feet (gravity direction, time flow, physical laws).

**Infinite Garden (Costs 3 Actions).** The Gardener creates 1d4+1 copies of itself from different recursion levels. These copies have 50 hit points each and disappear after 1 minute.
