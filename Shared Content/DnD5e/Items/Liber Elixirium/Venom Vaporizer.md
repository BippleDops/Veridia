# Venom Vaporizer
## Overview
For the effective and timely removal of poisons and venoms caused by bites, stings, mephitic vapors, toxic weapons and projectiles.

**Hue:** Violet

**Aroma:** Sharp

**Texture:** Viscous

**Taste:** Sweet and Tart

**Recipe**

1 oz. Bearberry leaves, dried

3/4 oz. Opal, ground

6 oz. blackberry brandy

1 oz. horse blood

(2 drops Imbiber’s blood)

1. Place Bearberry leaves and Opal in a mortar and grind together aggressively with the pestle.
1. Place ground ingredients into a small [[Cauldron|cauldron]]. Add blackberry brandy and place over flame.
1. Heat mixture until it steams and takes on a red hue. Add horse blood and stir for 30 seconds, Liquid will immediately turn violet.
1. Remove from heat and quench [[Cauldron|cauldron]] in cold water.
1. Pour liquid into [[Vial|vial]] and place stopper in the [[Vial|vial]] immediately.
1. Before imbibing, prick the victim’s index finger and squeeze two drops of blood directly into the [[Vial|vial]].
