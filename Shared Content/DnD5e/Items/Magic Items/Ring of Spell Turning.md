## Description
While wearing this ring, you have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]] against any spell that targets only you (not in an area of effect). In addition, if you roll a 20 for the save and the spell is 7th level or lower, the spell has no effect on you and instead targets the caster, using the slot level, spell save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]], [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] bonus, and [[7 - Spellcasting|spellcasting]] ability of the caster.
