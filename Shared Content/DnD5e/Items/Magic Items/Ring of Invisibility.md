## Description
While wearing this ring, you can turn [[Invisible|invisible]] as an action. Anything you are wearing or carrying is invisible with you. You remain invisible until the ring is removed, until you [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] or [[14 - Actions in Combat|cast a spell]], or until you use a [[12 - The Order of Combat|bonus action]] to become visible again.
