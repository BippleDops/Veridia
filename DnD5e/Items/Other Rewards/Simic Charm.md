# Simic Charm
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
When you activate this charm, you can cast the [enhance ability]] (3rd-level version), [[Enlarge_Reduce|Enlarge/Reduce]], or [[gaseous form]] spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.
