# Visual Style Guide

- Color palettes per realm: Aquabyssos (teals, deep blues, biolum glow), Aethermoor (navy, gold, cloud-white), Void (indigos, violets, starfield accents).
- Art styles: painterly realism for portraits; concept art realism for environments; blueprint clean-line for vehicles; flat vector for heraldry.
- Lighting: cinematic key/fill/rim; underwater caustics; storm rim light for sky scenes.
- Mood: heroic but grounded; wonder with subtle dread at the edges.
- Consistency tags: painterly, cinematic, high detail, cohesive palette.
