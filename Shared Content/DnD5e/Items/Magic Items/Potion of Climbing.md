# Potion of Climbing

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *Common*


## Description
When you drink this potion, you gain a [[8 - Movement|climbing]] speed equal to your walking speed for 1 hour. During this time, you have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Strength|Strength]] ([[Strength|Athletics]]) checks you make to climb. The potion is separated into brown, silver, and gray layers resembling bands of stone. Shaking the [[Glass Bottle|bottle]] fails to mix the colors.
