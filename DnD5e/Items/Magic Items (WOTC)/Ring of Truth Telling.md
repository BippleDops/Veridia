# Ring of Truth Telling

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items, Ring

**Rarity:** Uncommon

**Attunement**: *requires attunement*

## Description

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on Wisdom ([[Insight]]) checks to determine whether someone is lying to you.
