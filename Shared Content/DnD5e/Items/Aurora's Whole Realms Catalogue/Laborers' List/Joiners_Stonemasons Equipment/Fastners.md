## Description
fasteners of all sorts for joining wood, metal, and stone. We offer 1" finishing nails (2 sp/lb) as well as 2" and 3" joiners’ nails (1 sp/lb). Our spikes come in 6" (3 gp/dozen) and 1' (5 gp/dozen) sizes. We supply 8" bolts that can be cut to size in coarse and fine thread size with the following diameters: ¼", ½", ¾", 1"—all at 3 gp/lb. The corresponding nuts to these bolts cost 2 gp/lb. We also provide cylindrical wooden peg stock in ½", 1", and 2" sizes at 5 cp/foot, 1 sp/foot, 3 sp/foot, respectively.

## Additional Details
12" spikes act in all ways as a dagger in combat
