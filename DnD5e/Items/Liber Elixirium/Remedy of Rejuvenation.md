# Remedy of Rejuvenation
## Overview
For the eradication of mortal wounds, deep lacerations in skin and muscle, damage to internal organs, serious head wounds and broken bones, returning the victim from the brink of death.

**Hue:** Umber

**Aroma:** Spicy

**Texture:** Watery

**Taste:** Spicy

**Recipe**

4 oz. Ashoka Bark powder

1 oz. Malachite, powdered

1 pint spring water

3/4 oz. Demon [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items (WOTC)/Horn|horn]], powdered

1. Place Ashoka Bark, Amber and water in a small [[Cauldron|cauldron]], and bring to a boil. Solution will be [[Light|light]] green.
1. Add 1/4 oz. of Demon [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items (WOTC)/Horn|horn]] every 30 seconds as mixture boils.
1. After final 1/4 oz. is added, boil for an additional 5 minutes.
1. Solution should now be umber in hue, with a spicy aroma.
1. If the mixture is not this hue and aroma, discard immediately.
1. Let mixture cool and settle naturally for 4 hours.
1. Pour into [[Vial|vial]] and place stopper in the [[Vial|vial]] immediately.
