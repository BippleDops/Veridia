# Clothes of Mending

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items

**Rarity:** common

## Description

This elegant outfit of traveler's clothes magically mends itself to counteract daily wear and tear. Pieces of the outfit that are destroyed can't be repaired in this way.
