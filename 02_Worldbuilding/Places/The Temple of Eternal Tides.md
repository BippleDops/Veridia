# The Temple of Eternal Tides
*Where Sea and Sky Meet in Sacred Harmony*

## Overview
The Temple of Eternal Tides serves as the primary religious center for ocean worship, conducting rituals that maintain the balance between surface and underwater realms. The temple houses diplomatic missions between surface kingdoms and [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Abyssos Prime|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Abyssos Prime]], making it a crucial political and spiritual nexus.
