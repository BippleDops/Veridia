# Mordenkainen's Defense Against Slime (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *abjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a bit of mold)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
You or one willing creature you touch has resistance to the natural attacks of fungal plant creatures and all types of oozes.
