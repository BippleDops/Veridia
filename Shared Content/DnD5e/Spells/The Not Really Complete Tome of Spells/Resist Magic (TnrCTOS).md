# Resist Magic (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *5th-level*

**School**: *abjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a piece of string, a block of wood, and an unlit candle)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
For the duration, you or one creature you touch gains advantage on saving throws to resist magical spells.
