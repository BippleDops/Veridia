# X-L-12 The Deep Mother’s Bridge of Dreams

## Read-Aloud
> A bridge appears where no road could stand, woven from moonlight and lullabies and the memory of tides.

## Setup
- Her will forms a path between worlds

## Mechanics
- Religion DC 18 accept/bless/bargain
- Group Sanity DC 18 endure the crossing

## Outcomes
- Success: Blessed passage; gain boon and burden
- Partial: Flickering path; hazard checks every 10 minutes
- Fail: Lost in dreams; rescue arc

## Integrations
- [[Deep Mother]]; Sanity; campaign arc pivot

## Quick-Run
- Religion→Sanity; choose bargain; set path stability
