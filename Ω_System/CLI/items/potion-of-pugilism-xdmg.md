# Potion of Pugilism
*Potion, uncommon*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/img/potion-of-pugilism.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

After you drink this potion, each [Unarmed Strike](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) you make deals an extra `d6` Force damage on a hit. This effect lasts 10 minutes.

This potion is a thick green fluid that tastes like spinach.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 289*
