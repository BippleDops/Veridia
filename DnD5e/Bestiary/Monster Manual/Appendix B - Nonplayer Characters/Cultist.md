## Description
Cultists swear allegiance to dark powers such as elemental princes, demon lords, or archdevils. Most conceal their loyalties to avoid being ostracized, imprisoned, or executed for their beliefs. Unlike evil [[Acolyte|acolytes]], cultists often show signs of insanity in their beliefs and practices.
