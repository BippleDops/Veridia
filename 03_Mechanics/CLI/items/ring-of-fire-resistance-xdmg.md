# Ring of Fire Resistance
*Ring, rare*  
![](/03_Mechanics/CLI/items/img/ring-of-resistance.webp#right)


You have [Resistance](/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to fire damage while wearing this ring. The ring is set with garnet.
