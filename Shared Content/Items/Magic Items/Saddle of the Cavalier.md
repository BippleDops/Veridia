# Saddle of the Cavalier

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *Uncommon*

## Description
While in this saddle on a mount, you can't be dismounted against your will if you're conscious, and attack rolls against the mount have disadvantage.
