# Oil of Sharpness

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *very rare*


## Description
This clear, gelatinous oil sparkles with tiny, ultrathin silver shards. The oil can coat one [[17 - Damage and Healing|slashing]] or piercing weapon or up to 5 pieces of [[17 - Damage and Healing|slashing]] or piercing Ammunition. Applying the oil takes 1 minute. For 1 hour, the coated item is magical and has a +3 bonus to [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] and damage rolls.
