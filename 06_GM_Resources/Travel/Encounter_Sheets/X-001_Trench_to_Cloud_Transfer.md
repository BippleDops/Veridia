# X-001 Trench-to-Cloud Transfer

## Read-Aloud
> The portal irises open with a breath, dragging the sea into the sky. Your sub’s ballast sings as the hull becomes a keel.

## Setup
- Cross-vehicle procedure; sub to airship profile mid-storm

## Mechanics
- Anchorite DC 15 stabilize portal; Helm DC 15 adapt controls; Engineer DC 15 re-route power

## Outcomes
- Success: Seamless transition; no time loss
- Partial: Turbulence (1d6); -1 Maneuver 1h
- Fail: Stall/spin; crew Con DC 14 vs. vertigo; minor damage 2d6

## Integrations
- [[03_Mechanics/Vehicles/Vehicle_Core_Rules|Vehicle Core Rules]]; anchors; altitude inversion

## Quick-Run
- Call three checks; apply result; proceed into sky encounter
