## Description
Your child can emulate his or her favorite ranger with this safe play set. The arrow tips are bean-[[Bags|bags]] affixed securely to the shafts, and the bow is a marvelously resilient willow stem with a 5-lb pull. In addition to letting the child act like the grown-ups, the set teaches good missile weapon etiquette and care. Always supervise your child when playing with our weapons, especially since they ARE harmless. Made in the Dalelands. Play set costs 2 gp and contains bow, three bowstrings, and 5 safety arrows.
