Pathfinder 2e has excellent digital support that is allowed and thus enabled by Paizo. They have a policy of allowing the use of the rules in online digital tools which is amazing. 

The Obsidian.md community are maintaing the PF2e data here: https://github.com/ObsidianTTRPGProject/ObsidianTTRPGShare

You are free to use this legally. 

## What's Available?
- Rules 
- Monsters
- Spells
- Basically all the mechanics

## What's Not Available?
- Adventures
Paizo make their money off Adventures. As such you will need to enter these yourself. It's quick though if you copy and paste from the official PDF's then it does not take long at all. You can easily do an entire chapter in a single sitting.
