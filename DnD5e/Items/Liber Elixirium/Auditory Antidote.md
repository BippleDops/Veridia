# Auditory Antidote
## Overview
For the complete restoration of auditory senses lost through either natural or magical means.

**Hue:** Brown

**Aroma:** Peppery

**Texture:** Syrupy

**Taste:** Sour

**Recipe**

3 1/2 oz. Hawkweed stems, fresh, chopped small

2 oz. Jet, powdered

2 1/2 cups cherry brandy

1 1/2 oz. [[Bat]]’s ear, powdered

1. Place cherry brandy and Hawkweed stems into an [[Alembic|alembic]].
1. Distill contents until 4 oz. of refined liquid is obtained.
1. Pour refined liquid into a small [[Cauldron|cauldron]] and bring to a boil.
1. Stir in powdered Jet and powdered [[Bat]]’s ear until dissolved.
1. Remove from heat and stir. Solution will become thick and syrupy as it cools.
1. Pour into [[Vial|vial]] and place stopper in the [[Vial|vial]].
