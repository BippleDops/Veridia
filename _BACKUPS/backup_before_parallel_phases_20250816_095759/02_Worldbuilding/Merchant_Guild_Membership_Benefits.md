--- #### Apprentice Trader (Entry Level)

**Cost:** 50 pearls/crystals
**Benefits:**
- 5% discount on standard shipping rates
- Access to guild bulletin board
- Basic market price reports (weekly)
- Shared warehouse space (1 cubic meter)
- Guild identification papers #### Journeyman Merchant (Established)

**Cost:** 200 pearls/crystals
**Requirements:** 1 year apprentice status, 50 successful trades
**Benefits:**
- 10% discount on shipping, 5% on goods
- Priority cargo booking
- Market price reports (daily)
- Dedicated warehouse space (5 cubic meters)
- Access to guild lending services
- Dispute resolution support #### Master Trader (Professional)

**Cost:** 500 pearls/crystals
**Requirements:** 3 years journeyman, 200 successful trades
**Benefits:**
- 15% discount on all services
- Expedited customs processing
- Real-time market data access
- Private warehouse suite (20 cubic meters)
- Guild insurance coverage
- Cross-realm banking privileges
- Exclusive high-value cargo contracts #### Guild Magnate (Elite)

**Cost:** By invitation only
**Requirements:** Exceptional trading record, guild recommendation
**Benefits:**
- Custom negotiated rates
- Personal trade representative
- Market manipulation alerts
- Private dock facilities
- Political influence access
- Exclusive auction participation
- Route planning consultation #### Crisis Response

- Emergency evacuation insurance
- Cargo protection during conflicts
- Alternative route planning
- Loss compensation programs *Join today and expand your trading horizons across both realms*
