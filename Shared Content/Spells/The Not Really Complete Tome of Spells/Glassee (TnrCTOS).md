# Glassee (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *4th-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a small piece of glass)

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
You make an area of metal, stone, or wood of up to 6 square feet transparent as glass. Metal can be up to 4 inches thick; stone can be up to 1 foot thick, and wood can be up to 2 feet thick. You can choose to make the area transparent to everyone, make it trans-parent only to you, or to create a one-way mirror. This spell does not work through lead.
