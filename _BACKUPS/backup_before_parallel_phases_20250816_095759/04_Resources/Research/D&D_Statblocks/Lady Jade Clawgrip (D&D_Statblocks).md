Lady Jade Clawgrip serves as the "Scale of Acquisition" on the [[Silverscale Consortium|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/Silverscale Consortium's Board of Scales, specializing in hostile takeovers, corporate warfare, and the complete destruction of business competitors. Her silver dragon heritage gives her both supernatural abilities and an instinctive understanding of greed and acquisition, making her the Consortium's most feared negotiator and its most effective weapon against economic rivals. ## Adventure Hooks
