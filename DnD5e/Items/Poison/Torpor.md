# Torpor
## Overview
*Torpor (Ingested).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for `dice: 4d6` hours. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature is [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]].
