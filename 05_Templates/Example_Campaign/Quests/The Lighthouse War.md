# The Lighthouse War

## Quest Info
**Quest Giver**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Admiral Cassius Stormhaven]]
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Whisper Rock Lighthouse]]

## Quest Details
The party is hired by Admiral Stormhaven to help defend the Whisper Rock Lighthouse from an attack by the Blackwater Raiders.

## GM Notes
- The battle for the lighthouse is a major turning point in the war between the Shadowhaven Navy and the Blackwater Raiders.
- The party's actions will determine the outcome of the battle and will have a major impact on the future of The Contested Waters.
- The party will have to use all of their skills of combat, strategy, and diplomacy to win the day.
