# Enduring Spellbook

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items

**Rarity:** common

## Description

This spellbook, along with anything written on its pages, can't be damaged by fire or immersion in water. In addition, the spellbook doesn't deteriorate with age.
