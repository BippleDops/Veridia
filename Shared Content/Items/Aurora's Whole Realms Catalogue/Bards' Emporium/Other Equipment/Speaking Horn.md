## Description
Even for a popular bard, an appreciative audience can grow only so large due to the limits of the human voice. However, with this polished cone, a bard can send his or her voice far and clear. Available in rigid birchwood (3 gp) for urban bards, and leather (5 gp) with removable iron hoops for easy folding and storing.
