## Description
When you hit a fiend or an [[14 - Monsters|undead]] with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]], that creature takes an extra
`dice: 2d6` (2d6) [[17 - Damage and Healing|radiant]] damage. If the target has 25 hit points or fewer after taking this damage, it must succeed on a [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 15 [[Wisdom|Wisdom]] saving throw or be destroyed. On a successful save, the creature becomes [[Frightened|frightened]] of you until the end of your next turn.

While you hold this weapon, it sheds [[9 - The Environment|bright light]] in a 20-foot radius and [[9 - The Environment|dim light]] for an additional 20 feet.
