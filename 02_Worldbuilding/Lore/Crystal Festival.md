# Crystal Festival

*"Where light dances through crystal, two worlds become one."*

## Overview
The Crystal Festival is the premier annual celebration uniting both [[Aquabyssos]] and [[Aethermoor]] in a spectacular display of crystalline artistry, magical demonstrations, and inter-realm cultural exchange. The festival has become increasingly important as [[The Convergence Point]] approaches, serving as a symbol of cooperation between worlds. Held simultaneously across multiple locations, the festival showcases the beauty and power of crystals while fostering understanding between surface and underwater civilizations.

## Mechanics
### Requirements
- Coordination between both realm governments
- Extensive security arrangements for mixed populations
- Crystal artist participation from both realms
- Magical infrastructure for simultaneous celebrations

### Effects
- **Cultural Unity**: Promotes understanding between realms
- **Economic Boost**: Major increase in trade and tourism
- **Artistic Innovation**: Showcases new crystal-working techniques
- **Diplomatic Opportunity**: Facilitates political negotiations

### Duration
Week-long celebration held annually during optimal magical alignment.

## Adventure Hooks
1. **Festival Security**: Protect important guests during celebrations, particularly from [[The Crimson Hand]] infiltrators
2. **Crystal Competition**: Participate in artistic or magical contests, potentially involving [[The Seven Shards]]
3. **Diplomatic Crisis**: Prevent incident that could damage relations, possibly orchestrated by [[The Silverscale Conspiracy]]
4. **Memory Theft Ring**: Investigate attempts to steal valuable festival memories for [[Umbral Exchange]] trading
5. **Shadow Corruption Outbreak**: Deal with shadow corruption manifesting during crystal ceremonies

## Campaign Use
Provides colorful setting for social encounters, cultural exploration, and diplomatic adventures. The festival serves as a nexus where multiple campaign elements intersect: [[The Seven Shards]] may be displayed, [[The Crimson Hand]] agents operate, [[Memory Alteration Consequences]] affect visitors, and [[The Convergence Point]] influences magical displays. Perfect venue for introducing cross-realm NPCs and advancing multiple plot threads simultaneously.
