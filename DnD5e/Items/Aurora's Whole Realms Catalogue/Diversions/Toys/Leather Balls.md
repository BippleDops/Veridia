## Description
These strong, sturdy cowhide balls are available in 2" juggling sizes, 4" handball sizes, and cloth-wrapped 12" sizes for small children. The most durable of these balls comes from Ordulin, and Aurora’s has an exclusive license with Anaster’s Leatherworks to distribute balls made from their soft, supple leathers beyond the Sembian borders. 

- 2" ball—1 sp 
- 4" ball—2 sp 
- 12" ball—2 gp
