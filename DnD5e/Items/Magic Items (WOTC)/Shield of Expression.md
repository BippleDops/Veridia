# Shield of Expression

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Armor, Shield

**Rarity:** common

**Weight**: 6 lbs.

## Description

The front of this shield is shaped in the likeness of a face. While bearing the shield, you can use a bonus action to alter the face's expression.
