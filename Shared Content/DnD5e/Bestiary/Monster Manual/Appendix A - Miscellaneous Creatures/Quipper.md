## Description
A quipper is a carnivorous fish with sharp teeth.

Quippers can adapt to any aquatic environment, including cold subterranean lakes. They frequently gather in swarms; the statistics for a [[Swarm of Quippers|swarm of quippers]] appear later in this appendix.
