# Serpent's Fang

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items, Melee Weapon, Martial Weapon, Longsword

**Rarity:** rare

**Weight**: 3 lbs.

**Damage:** 1d8 slashing versatile (1d10)

## Description

This single-edged magic sword is made from the scrimshawed fang of a giant serpent. Its hilt changes shape to adjust to the grasp of any creature that picks it up. The weapon deals an extra `dice: 1d10` poison damage to any target it hits.
