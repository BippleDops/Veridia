### Seasonal Wind Patterns

**Spring Winds:**
- Direction: Variable, shifting
- Strength: Moderate (15-25 mph)
- Hazards: Sudden storm formation
- Best for: Training flights, local travel

**Summer Winds:**
- Direction: Steady westerly
- Strength: Strong (25-35 mph)
- Hazards: Heat thermals, turbulence
- Best for: Long-distance trade

**Autumn Winds:**
- Direction: Easterly, consistent
- Strength: Moderate (20-30 mph)
- Hazards: Visibility reduction
- Best for: Precision navigation

**Winter Winds:**
- Direction: Northern, harsh
- Strength: Severe (35-50 mph)
- Hazards: Ice formation, equipment failure
- Best for: Emergency travel only
