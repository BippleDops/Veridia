# Periapt of Proof against Poison

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *rare*


## Description
This delicate silver [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items for Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD/Chain|chain]] has a brilliant-cut black gem pendant. While you wear it, [[12 - Poisons|poisons]] have no effect on you. You are immune to the [[Poisoned|poisoned]] condition and have immunity to [[17 - Damage and Healing|poison]] damage.
