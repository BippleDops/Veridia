# Glassteel (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *7th-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a piece of glass and a piece of steel)

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
Up to 100 pounds of glass or crystal that you touch gains the tensile strength and du-rability of steel.
