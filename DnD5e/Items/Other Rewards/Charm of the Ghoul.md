# Charm of the Ghoul
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
This charm allows you to use an action to eat a mouthful of flesh from a humanoid that has been dead no longer than 1 day. When you do, you regain `dice: 3d8 + 3` hit points. Once used three times, the charm goes away.
