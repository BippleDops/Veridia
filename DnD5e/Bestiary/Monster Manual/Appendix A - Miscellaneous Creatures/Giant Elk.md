## Special Traits
***Charge.*** If the elk moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with a ram attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (`dice: 2d6`) damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

## Actions
***Ram.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (`dice: 2d6+4`) bludgeoning damage.

***Hooves.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. Hit: 22 (`dice: 4d8+4`) bludgeoning damage.

## Description
he majestic giant elk is rare to the point that its appearance is often taken as a foreshadowing of an important event, such as the birth of a king. Legends tell of gods that take the form of giant elk when visiting the Material Plane. Many cultures therefore believe that to hunt these creatures is to invite divine wrath.
