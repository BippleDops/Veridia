## Description
Closely tracking time is crucial to the success of chemical research. We offer various sizes of time glasses: half minute (5 gp), minute (3 gp), five minute (7 gp), ten minute (10 gp), hour (7 gp), and two hour (15 gp) glasses. All of these timepieces are cast and ornately painted in Calimshan, then filled with precise sand measures in Waterdeep.

## Additional Details
Half-minute, minute, five minute, and hour glasses are required for a complete laboratory
