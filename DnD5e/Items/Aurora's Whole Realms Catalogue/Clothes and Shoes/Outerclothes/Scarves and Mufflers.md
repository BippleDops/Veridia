## Description
Used to add color to an outfit, or just to keep extra warm, our scarves and mufflers all come at nominal cost. Scarves are available in the solid colors listed under [[Sashes]] (1 sp), or in a variety of print designs (10 gp) if you buy silk. All of our mufflers are very warm and are available in wool (1 sp) and linen (2 sp). Normal color options.
