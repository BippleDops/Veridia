### Environmental Modifiers
#### Pressure/Altitude Disadvantage
- **Severe Disadvantage**: +50% to encounter budget
- **Moderate Disadvantage**: +25% to encounter budget
- **Slight Disadvantage**: +10% to encounter budget
- **Neutral Environment**: No modifier
- **Advantageous Environment**: -10% to encounter budget

#### Familiar Territory
- **Home Territory**: -15% to encounter budget
- **Frequently Visited**: -10% to encounter budget
- **Neutral Territory**: No modifier
- **Foreign Territory**: +10% to encounter budget
- **Hostile Territory**: +20% to encounter budget

### Party Composition Adjustments

#### High Damage Party
- **All DPS Classes**: +25% to encounter HP/AC
- **Mostly DPS**: +15% to encounter HP/AC
- **Balanced**: No adjustment
- **Support Heavy**: -10% to encounter HP/AC

#### High Control Party
- **Heavy Control**: Add legendary resistances, condition immunities
- **Moderate Control**: Add some resistances
- **Low Control**: Standard statblocks work well

#### High Healing Party
- **Abundant Healing**: Increase encounter duration, add time pressure
- **Moderate Healing**: Slight increase to damage output
- **Limited Healing**: Consider encounter spacing

## Combat Encounter Types

### Standard Combat
- **Purpose**: Resource management, tactical challenge
- **Duration**: 3-6 rounds
- **Complexity**: 1-3 enemy types
- **Environment**: Neutral to slightly challenging

### Pressure Cooker
- **Purpose**: High stakes, dramatic tension
- **Duration**: 2-4 rounds
- **Complexity**: Single powerful foe or unified group
- **Environment**: Time pressure or dangerous conditions

### Attrition Battle
- **Purpose**: Long-term resource drain
- **Duration**: 6-10 rounds
- **Complexity**: Waves of enemies or regenerating foes
- **Environment**: Defensive positions or ongoing hazards

### Puzzle Combat
- **Purpose**: Tactical thinking over raw power
- **Duration**: 4-8 rounds
- **Complexity**: Environmental interactions required
- **Environment**: Complex terrain, multiple objectives

## Encounter Modification Quick Reference

### Increase Difficulty
- **Add Minions**: +25% HP pool, swarm tactics
- **Environmental Hazards**: Ongoing damage, movement restriction
- **Time Pressure**: Limited rounds to complete objectives
- **Resource Restriction**: Limited spell slots, equipment failure
- **Reinforcements**: Additional enemies arrive mid-combat

### Decrease Difficulty
- **Reduce Numbers**: Fewer enemies or lower stats
- **Environmental Aid**: Helpful terrain features
- **NPC Assistance**: Friendly forces join battle
- **Resource Boost**: Extra healing, magical aid
- **Escape Routes**: Multiple ways to retreat or bypass
