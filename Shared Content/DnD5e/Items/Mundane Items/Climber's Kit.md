# Climber's Kit

## Overview
**Price**: 25 gp

**Weight**: 12 lb.

**Climber’s Kit.** A climber’s kit includes [[3 - Weapons|special]] [[Piton|pitons]], boot tips, gloves, and a harness. You can use the climber’s kit as an action to anchor yourself; when you do, you can’t [[9 - The Environment|fall]] more than 25 feet from the point where you anchored yourself, and you can’t [[8 - Movement|climb]] more than 25 feet away from that point without undoing the anchor.
