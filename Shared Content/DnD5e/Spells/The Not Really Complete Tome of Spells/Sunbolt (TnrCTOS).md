# Sunbolt (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *4th-level*

**School**: *evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 120 feet

**Components**: V, S, M (a small sunstone worth at least 10 gp)

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You hurl a mote of pure sunlight at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 4d10 radiant damage, and the target and all creatures within 20 feet must make a Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1 minute.

If the creature has the trait Sunlight Sensi-tivity or Sunlight Hypersensitivity, they must succeed on a second Constitution saving throw or take double damage.
