## Description
This little blade—so small that it hides easily between the knuckles of a thief—has given the cutpurse his name. Masters of this tiny and keen blade can garner a day’s wages in a mere hour. Of course, novices may end up missing a finger or two. (1 sp)

## Additional Details
+5% to Pick Pockets; cannot be used with rosin, powder, or wired cane
