# Ring of Resistance

## Overview

**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Rings*

**Rarity**: *Rare*

**Attunement**: *requires attunement*

## Description

You have [[17 - Damage and Healing|resistance]] to one damage type while wearing this ring. The gem in the ring indicates the type, which the GM chooses or determines randomly.
