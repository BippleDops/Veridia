# D-104 Whale Graveyard

## Read-Aloud
> Ribcages arch like cathedrals from the silt. Every beam of your lamps seems smaller beneath so much death.

## Setup
- Zone: Abyss • Category: Discovery/Horror

## Mechanics
- Group Sanity DC 15
- Medicine/Nature DC 14 to harvest safely
- Religion DC 14 to appease spirits (optional)

## Outcomes
- Success: Reagents worth 200–600 gp; lore insight
- Partial: Crew superstition (morale -1) for 24h
- Fail: Short-term madness; refuse to harvest

## Integrations
- [[Sanity System]]; [[Crafting Transcendence]]; lore hooks

## Quick-Run
- Sanity→Harvest→apply morale or rewards
