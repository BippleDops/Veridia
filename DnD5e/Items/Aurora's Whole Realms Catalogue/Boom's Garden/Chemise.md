## Description
This low-slitted chemise shows off shoulders and sternums quite nicely. A favorite of musclebound warriors who want a bit more protection than their abdominal muscles can provide, these chemises look sharp on shaved and oiled skin. We offer tight-fitting and supple leather (7 gp), [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items for Dungeons & Dragons 5e SRD/Chain|chain]] mail for 45 gp, and lace mail (an airy chainmail that shows off the skin beneath) for 60 gp.
