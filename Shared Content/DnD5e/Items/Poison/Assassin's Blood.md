# Assassin's Blood
## Overview
*[[Assassin]]**'s Blood (Ingested).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 6 (`dice: 1d12`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage and is [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 24 hours. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and isn't [[Poisoned|poisoned]].
