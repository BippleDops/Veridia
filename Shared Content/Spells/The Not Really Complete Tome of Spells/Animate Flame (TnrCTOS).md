# Animate Flame (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *4th-level*

**School**: *evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 300 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
You animate a Medium-size nonmagical flame, causing it leave its source of fuel and move in your direct with a speed of 30 feet. It does not require fuel during this time.

The animated fire causes 1d10 fire damage to any creature it touches and it automat-ically ignites combustible material.
