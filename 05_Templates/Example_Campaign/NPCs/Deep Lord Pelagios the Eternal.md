# Deep Lord Pelagios the Eternal

## General Info
**Race**: Merfolk
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 1000
**Occupation**: Sovereign of the Ancient Mer-Folk Empire
**Location**: [[The Sunken City of Aeridor]]

## Description
Deep Lord Pelagios is the ancient and powerful ruler of the merfolk. He is a wise and just ruler, but he is also very protective of his people and his kingdom.

## GM Notes
- He is a potential ally for the party, but he is also very suspicious of surface dwellers.
- He is a rival of [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/NPCs/Pirate King Razor Blackheart.md]] and the Crimson Tide.
- He is the guardian of the Heart of the Sea, a powerful magical artifact.
