A sled dog (use the [wolf](/compendium/bestiary/beast/wolf.md) stat block in appendix A of the Monster Manual) costs 50 gp and can pull 360 pounds.

Sled dogs must take a short rest after pulling a sled for 1 hour; otherwise, they gain one level of [exhaustion](/rules/conditions.md#exhaustion).
