# Barkskin
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of oak bark)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin assumes a bark-like appearance, and the target has an [[armor-class-xphb (variant-rules)]] of 17 if its AC is lower than that.
