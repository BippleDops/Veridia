## Description
A giant lizard can be ridden or used as a draft animal. [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Races/Lizardfolk]] also keep them as pets, and subterranean giant lizards are used as mounts and pack animals by [[Drow|drow]], [[Duergar|duergar]], and other Underdark dwellers.
