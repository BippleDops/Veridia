# Purple Worm Poison
## Overview
*[[Purple Worm]]* *[[Poisoned|Poison]]** (Injury).* This [[Poisoned|poison]] must be harvested from a dead or [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] [[Purple Worm|purple worm]]. A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 42 (`dice: 12d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
