# Potion of Flying

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *Very Rare*


## Description
When you drink this potion, you gain a [[13 - Movement and Position|flying]] speed equal to your walking speed for 1 hour and can [[14 - Monsters|hover]]. If you’re in the air when the potion wears off, you [[9 - The Environment|fall]] unless you have some other means of staying aloft. This potion’s clear liquid floats at the top of its container and has cloudy white impurities drifting in it.
