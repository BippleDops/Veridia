# Malice
## Overview
*Malice (Inhaled).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [[Poisoned|poisoned]] for 1 hour. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature is [[Blinded|blinded]].
