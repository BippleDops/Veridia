# Pale Tincture
## Overview
*Pale Tincture (Ingested).* A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or take 3 (`dice: 1d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage and become [[Poisoned|poisoned]]. The [[Poisoned|poisoned]] creature must repeat the saving throw every 24 hours, taking 3 (`dice: 1d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage on a failed save. Until this [[Poisoned|poison]] ends, the damage the [[Poisoned|poison]] deals can't be healed by any means. After seven successful [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]], the effect ends and the creature can [[Heal|heal]] normally.
