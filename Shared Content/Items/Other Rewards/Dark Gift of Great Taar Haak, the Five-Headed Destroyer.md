# Dark Gift of Great Taar Haak, the Five-Headed Destroyer
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
Taar Haak's gift is great strength. This dark gift grants its beneficiary the benefit of a [[Belt of Giant Strength|Belt of Fire Giant Strength]]. This benefit lasts for 10 days, after which the dark gift vanishes.

The beneficiary of this dark gift the following flaw: "I like to bully others and make them feel weak and inferior."
