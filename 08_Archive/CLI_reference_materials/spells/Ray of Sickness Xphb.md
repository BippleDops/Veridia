# Ray of Sickness
*1st-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You shoot a greenish ray at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `2d8` Poison damage and has the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions#Poisoned|Poisoned]] condition until the end of your next turn.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `d8` for each spell slot level above 1.
