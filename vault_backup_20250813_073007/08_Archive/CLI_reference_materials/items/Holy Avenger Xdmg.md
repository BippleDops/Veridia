# Holy Avenger
*Legendary (requires attunement by a paladin)*  
![](/03_Mechanics/CLI/items/img/holy-avenger.webp#right)


You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a Fiend or an Undead with it, that creature takes an extra `2d10` Radiant damage.

While you hold the drawn weapon, it creates a 10-foot [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb|Emanation]] originating from you. You and all creatures [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb|Friendly]] to you in the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb|Emanation]] have [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb|Advantage]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. If you have 17 or more levels in the Paladin class, the size of the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb|Emanation]] increases to 30 feet.
