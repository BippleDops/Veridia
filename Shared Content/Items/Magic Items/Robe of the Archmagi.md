## Description
This elegant garment is made from exquisite cloth of white, gray, or black and adorned with silvery runes. The robe’s color corresponds to the [[2 - Alignment|alignment]] for which the item was created. A white robe was made for good, gray for [[2 - Alignment|neutral]], and black for evil. You can’t attune to a *robe of the archmagi* that doesn’t correspond to your alignment.

You gain these benefits while wearing the robe:
- If you aren’t wearing [[2 - Armor|armor]], your base [[2 - Armor|Armor Class]] is 15 + your [[Dexterity|Dexterity]] modifier.
- You have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[6 - Saving Throws|saving throws]] against spells and other magical effects.
- Your spell save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] and [[2 - Casting a Spell|spell attack]] bonus each increase by 2.
