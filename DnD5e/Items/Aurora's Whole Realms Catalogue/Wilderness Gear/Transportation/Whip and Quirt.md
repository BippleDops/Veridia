## Description
Unpleasant though it may be, whips and quirts are sometimes necessary to move a stubborn team or mount forward. Our whip is 20 feet long, reaching even to the first pair of horses in a six-horse team. Our quirt fits comfortably in the hand and can spur any horse on to greater speed.
