# Potion of Animal Friendship

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Potions*

**Rarity**: *Uncommon*


## Description
When you drink this potion, you can cast the **[[Animal Friendship|animal friendship]]** spell (save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 13) for 1 hour at will. Agitating this muddy liquid brings little bits into view: a fish scale, a hummingbird tongue, a [[Cat|cat]] claw, or a squirrel hair.
