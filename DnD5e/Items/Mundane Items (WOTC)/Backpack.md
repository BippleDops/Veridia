# Backpack
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: adventuring gear
**Properties**: 
**Weight**: 5 lb.
**Value**: 2 gp
## Description: 
A backpack can hold one cubic foot or 30 pounds of gear. You can also strap items, such as a bedroll or a coil of rope, to the outside of a backpack.
