# Charm of the Slayer
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Charm

## Overview: 
One sword in your possession becomes a [[dragon slayer]] or [[giant slayer]] (DM's choice) for the next 9 days. The charm then vanishes from you and the weapon returns to normal.
