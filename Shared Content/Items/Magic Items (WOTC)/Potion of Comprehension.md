# Potion of Comprehension

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items, Potions

**Rarity:** common

## Description

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of a [[Comprehend Languages (Ritual)|Comprehend Languages]] spell for 1 hour. This liquid is a clear concoction with bits of salt and soot swirling in it.
