## Description
Whether you need an easy way to haul a slain deer back to camp or a fast method for taking a wounded friend from the fray, you will want to be carrying our body [[Sleigh|sleigh]]. The durable canvas [[Sleigh|sleigh]] wraps in moments about a downed creature, and the leather [[Harness|harness]] [[Straps|straps]] easily to your shoulders. When in your [[Rucksack|rucksack]], the body [[Sleigh|sleigh]] weighs a mere 8 pounds, and when hauling a body, it allows you to move rapidly. (8 gp)

works for up to L creatures; user can haul in lbs 10 times Str at ¾ movement rate, 20 times Str at 1/2 movement rate, and 25 times Str at ¼ movement; can pull for number of rounds equal to Con before exhaustion
