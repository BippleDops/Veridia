# Crampons
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: adventuring gear
**Properties**: 
**Weight**: ¼ lb.
**Value**: 2 gp
## Description: 
A crampon is a metal plate with spikes that is strapped to the sole of a boot. A creature wearing crampons can't fall prone while moving across slippery ice.
