# Plate Armor of Etherealness
*Heavy armor, legendary (requires attunement)*  


- **Armor Class**: 18
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 65.0 lbs.

While you're wearing this armor, you can take a [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/actions#Magic|Magic]] action and use a command word to gain the effect of the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/spells/etherealness-xphb|Etherealness]] spell. The spell ends immediately if you remove the armor or take a [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/actions#Magic|Magic]] action to repeat the command word. This property of the armor can't be used again until the next dawn.
