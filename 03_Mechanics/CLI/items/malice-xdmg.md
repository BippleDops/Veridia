# Malice
*Adventuring gear, poison (inhaled)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/03_Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 250 gp

A creature subjected to Malice must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for 1 hour. The creature also has the [Blinded](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Blinded) condition while [Poisoned](/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 91. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*
