# Central Location

## Overview
Central Location is a city located in the Eastern Provinces. Recently established for its magical properties.

## Geography & Climate

### Location
- **Region**: [[Eastern Provinces]]
- **Coordinates**: Eastern part of the region
- **Elevation**: Sea level
- **Accessibility**: Dangerous approach

### Terrain
The surrounding area consists of rolling hills, with scattered lakes. The immediate vicinity features magical anomalies.

### Climate
- **Season Variation**: Harsh winters
- **Precipitation**: Constant mist
- **Temperature Range**: Extreme variations
- **Notable Weather**: Aurora displays

## Architecture & Layout

### Architectural Style
Buildings in Central Location follow mixed cultural influences. The predominant materials are enchanted wood.

### City Layout
The city is organized into 7 distinct districts:

1. **Central District**: Heart of administration and commerce
2. **Residential Quarter**: Where the population makes their homes
3. **Market District**: Trade and commerce center
4. **Temple Ward**: Religious and spiritual center
5. **Craft Quarter**: Workshops and artisan studios
6. **Noble Estate**: Exclusive area for the wealthy
7. **Foreign Quarter**: Where outsiders reside

### Defensive Structures
- **Walls**: Stone walls 20 feet high
- **Gates**: 1 main entrances, constantly monitored
- **Watchtowers**: None needed
