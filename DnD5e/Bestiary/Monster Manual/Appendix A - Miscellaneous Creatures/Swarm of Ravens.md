## Special Traits
***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny raven. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

## Actions
***Beaks.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target in the swarm's space. Hit: 7 (`dice: 2d6`) piercing damage, or 3 (`dice: 1d6`) piercing damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer.
