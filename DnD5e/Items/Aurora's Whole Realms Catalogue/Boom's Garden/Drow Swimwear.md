## Description
Through dark connections [[Boom's Garden|Boom’s Garden]] has acquired the much-talked-about drow spiderweb swimwear. These fashionable gray suits fit tightly and, due to their special fabric, slide smoothly through water. However, swimmers needn’t fear that the suits will become transparent when wet, for the location of the weblines prevents such embarrassment. (15 gp)
