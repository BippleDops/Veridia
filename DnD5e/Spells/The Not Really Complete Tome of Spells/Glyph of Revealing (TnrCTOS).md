# Glyph of Revealing (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *5th-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (60-foot radius)

**Components**: V, S, M (ink made out of the juice of crushed eyebright flowers, white chalk, and powdered quartz)

**Duration**: 10 minutes


## Effects
You inscribe a special rune on a flat, hori-zontal surface. All magical runes, marks, glyphs, symbols, and other forms of written magic glow, allowing them to be read or identified without being accidentally triggered.
