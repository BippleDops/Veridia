# Exterminate (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *necromancy*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (10-foot radius)

**Components**: V, S, M (a pinch of lavender and dried garlic)

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
When you cast this spell, all creatures of Tiny size with 1 hit point within 10 feet of you instantly die. Optionally, one Tiny crea-ture with 5 hit points or fewer that is within range and that you can see can be instantly killed.
