# Brazier of Commanding Fire Elementals

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

## Description
While a fire burns in this brass brazier, you can use an action to speak the brazier’s command word and summon a [[Elemental, Fire|fire elemental]], as if you had cast the [[Conjure Elemental|conjure elemental]]** spell. The brazier can’t be used this way again until the next dawn.

The brazier weighs 5 pounds.
