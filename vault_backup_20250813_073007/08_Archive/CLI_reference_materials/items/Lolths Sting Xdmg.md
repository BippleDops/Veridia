# Lolth's Sting
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  


- **Properties**: [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/item-properties#Poison|Poison]]
- **Cost**: 200 gp

A creature subjected to Lolth's Sting must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions#Poisoned|Poisoned]] condition for 1 hour. If the creature fails the save by 5 or more, the creature also has the [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions#Unconscious|Unconscious]] condition while [[/03_Mechanics/CLI/conditions#Poisoned|Poisoned]] in this way. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
