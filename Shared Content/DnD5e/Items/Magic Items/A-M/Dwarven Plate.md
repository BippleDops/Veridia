# Dwarven Plate

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

## Description
While wearing this [[2 - Armor|armor]], you gain a +2 bonus to [[2 - Armor|AC]]. In addition, if an effect moves you against your will along the ground, you can use your [[12 - The Order of Combat|reaction]] to reduce the distance you are moved by up to 10 feet.
