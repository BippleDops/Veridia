# Merchant Princes
*Gold Flows Like Water, Power Follows the Current*

## Overview
The Merchant Princes control [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor]]'s economy through their domination of crystal trade and shipping routes. Based in [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Port Meridian]], this council of the five wealthiest merchants effectively rules through economic pressure. They care little for ideologyâ€”only profit and the stability needed to ensure it.

## The Council of Five

### Current Members
1. **[[Lord Aurelius Goldwave]]** - Chairman, shipping magnate
2. **[[Lady Venetia Silkwind]]** - Textiles and luxury goods
3. **[[Master Chen Wei]]** - Exotic imports and information
4. **[[Captain Rodrigo Ironanchor]]** - Naval fleet owner
5. **[[Madame Rosalind Goldleaf]]** - Banking and finance

### Council Structure
- Rotating chairmanship (yearly)
- Unanimous vote for major decisions
- Individual freedom for separate ventures
- Shared intelligence network
- Combined military force when needed

## Economic Empire

### Assets Controlled

### The Crystal Exchange
- Sets crystal prices continent-wide
- Controls refinement standards
- Issues crystal-backed currency
- Manipulates supply and demand
- Influences international rates

## Current Schemes

### The Grand Monopoly
Attempting to control all crystal trade:
- Buying out competitors
- Controlling shipping routes
- Manipulating prices
- Creating artificial scarcity

### The Currency Revolution
> [!warning] DM Only
> Planning to replace royal currency with crystal-backed merchant notes, effectively controlling the economy

### The Port Meridian Expansion
- Building new harbors
- Creating free trade zones
- Establishing merchant law
- Constructing private navy base
