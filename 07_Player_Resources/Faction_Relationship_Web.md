### Faction Goals & Methods

**Parliament of Echoes:**
- Goal: Maintain democratic goverce
- Method: Legal proceedings, public transparency
- Challenge: Shadow infiltration

**Royal Court of Winds:**
- Goal: Protect Aethermoor sovereignty
- Method: Military strength, diplomatic pressure
- Challenge: Purist Coalition influence

**Shadow Conspiracy:**
- Goal: Control both realms
- Method: Infiltration, consciousness manipulation
- Challenge: Exposure and pursuit

**Crystal Wardens:**
- Goal: Prevent corruption spread
- Method: Research, containment, cleansing
- Challenge: Limited resources

**Purist Coalition:**
- Goal: Prevent "contamination" between realms
- Method: Political pressure, extremist actions
- Challenge: Popular opposition

**Deep Mother Cultists:**
- Goal: Facilitate Deep Mother's awakening
- Method: Sabotage, corruption spreading
- Challenge: Detection and elimination

**Merchant Guilds:**
- Goal: Maintain profitable trade
- Method: Economic leverage, neutrality
- Challenge: Political instability

### Player Action Consequences

**Supporting Parliament →** Gains democratic allies, faces Shadow retaliation
**Supporting Royal Court →** Gains military support, potential realm bias
**Supporting Merchants →** Economic advantages, political neutrality
**Supporting Wardens →** Anti-corruption resources, dangerous enemies
**Opposing Shadow →** Makes powerful enemies, gains public support
**Opposing Purists →** Faces extremist attacks, gains moderate support
**Opposing Cultists →** Spiritual protection, cosmic-level threats
