# Bladeboots
## Overview
**Price**: 15 gp.

**Weight**: 2 lbs.

### Bladeboots (Pic)

## Description
Captured thieves are typically stripped of their weapons, but not of their shoes. If a thief has a shoe for a weapon, odds for escape improve significantly. Solid leather construction, a broad steel blade, and a heel-click trigger mechanism add kick to any escape. (15 gp)

## Additional Details
