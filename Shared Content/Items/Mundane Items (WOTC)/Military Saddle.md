# Military Saddle
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: tack and harness
**Properties**: 
**Weight**: 30 lb.

## Description: 
A military saddle braces the rider, helping you keep your seat on an active mount in battle. It gives you advantage on any check you make to remain mounted.
