## Description
Doors have two sides—if you can’t pick the lock, you might try removing the hinges. Our hinge-removing set contains all the tools you need to remove hinges on anything from [[Jewelry|jewelry]] boxes to castle keeps. (30 gp)

## Additional Details
Requires Dex check +1 round for small, 1 turn for door-size, and 10 turns for large hinges
