# The Brotherhood of the Black Anchor
*In Shadow We Sail, In Silence We Strike*

## Overview
The Brotherhood of the Black Anchor operates as a sophisticated smuggling and piracy network that competes directly with both [[The Crimson Fleet|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Crimson Fleet]] and [[The Whisper Syndicate|02 Worldbuilding/Groups/The Whisper Syndicate]] for control of maritime criminal activities, specializing in stealth operations and information trafficking.
