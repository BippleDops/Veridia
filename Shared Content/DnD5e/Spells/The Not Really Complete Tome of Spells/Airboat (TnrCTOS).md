# Airboat (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *8th-level*

**School**: [[Transmutation (D&D 5e SRD)|transmutation]]**
*

**Special Nature**: [[1 - What Is a Spell_ (D&D 5e SRD)|ritual]]

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 5 miles

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: [[2 - Casting a Spell (D&D 5e SRD)|Concentration]], up to 12 [[7 - Time (D&D 5e SRD)|hours]]


## Effects
You must be outside to cast this spell. You point to a cloud, which immediately descends towards you and changes shape until it becomes a ship made of clouds. You and up to eight other creatures of your choice may board the airboat, which flies at a speed of 100 feet under your tele-pathic [[Command (D&D 5e SRD)|command]].

You are considered to have proficiency with the airboat. The airboat has an [[2 - Armor (D&D 5e SRD)|AC]] of 13 and 20 hit points.

***At Higher Levels.*** When cast with a 9th-level spell slot, you may bring up to sixteen other creatures onboard with you.
