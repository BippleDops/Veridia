# Blessing of Valhalla
**Type**: Supernatural Gifts
**Group**: Blessing

## Overview: 
This blessing grants you the power to summon spirit warriors, as if you had blown a [[Horn of Valhalla|Silver Horn of Valhalla]]. Once you use this blessing, you can't use it again until 7 days have passed.
