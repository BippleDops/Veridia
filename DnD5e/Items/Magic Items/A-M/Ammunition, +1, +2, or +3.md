# Ammunition, +1, +2, or +3

## Overview

**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: [[3 - Weapons|Weapons]] *(any Ammunition)*

**Rarity**: *uncommon (+1), rare (+2), or very rare (+3)*

**Attunement**: None

## Description

You have a bonus to [[14 - Actions in Combat|attack]] and damage rolls made with this piece of magic Ammunition. The bonus is determined by the rarity of the ammunition. Once it hits a target, the ammunition is no longer magical.

**Base items.** This item variant can be applied to the following base items:

- [[Arrow]]
- [[Blowgun Needle]]
- [[Crossbow Bolt]]
- [[Energy Cell]]
- [[Modern Bullet]]
- [[Renaissance Bullet]]
- [[Sling Bullet]]
