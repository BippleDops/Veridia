# Merger Events Pack A — 50 Ready-to-Run Encounters

Use with [[Complete Reality Merger System]], [[03_Mechanics/Vehicles/Merger_Hybrid_Vehicles|Merger Hybrid Vehicles]], [[Sanity System]], and [[Memory Trading Economy System|Memory Economy]].

---

### M-001: Physics Denial
- Type: Overlapping → Paradox • Category: Reality/Horror
- Trigger: Gravity points inward; floors become walls.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 15 align; Helm DC 15 reorient; Group Sanity DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Zone stabilizes (Integrity +1 local). Partial: Partial reflow; -1 Maneuver this scene. Fail: Crew long-term madness 1; loose gear causes 1d6 to a segment.
- Integration: Anchors/RSB; Sanity.
- Variants: Denial flips every minute until resolved.

### M-002: Time Echo Conversation
- Type: Oscillating • Category: Paradox/Story
- Trigger: Crew overhears their future selves.
- Checks: Sanity DC 14; Insight DC 14 to parse.
- Outcomes: Success: Gain advantage on one future check (tie to prophecy). Partial: Confusion; no benefit. Fail: Short-term madness.
- Integration: Temporal Mechanics; Sanity.
- Variants: Echo contradicts current mission orders.

### M-003: Duplicate Crew Arrival
- Type: Overlapping • Category: Identity
- Trigger: A second copy of a crew member boards.
- Checks: Sanity DC 15; Social DC 14 to de-escalate; Arcana DC 15 to identify origin.
- Outcomes: Success: Peaceful integration; gain skilled helper (temporary). Partial: Tense standoff; -1 Cohesion. Fail: Fight; injury; paradox mark.
- Integration: Sanity; Memory (divergent memories).
- Variants: Duplicate is from a failure timeline.

### M-004: Endless Hatch
- Type: Anchored • Category: Navigation Puzzle
- Trigger: One hatch leads to another copy endlessly.
- Checks: Intelligence (Investigation) DC 15; Anchorite DC 14 to mark true path.
- Outcomes: Success: Escape loop; mark insight. Partial: Delay 1d4 hours. Fail: Crew exhaustion +1; Sanity DC 14.
- Integration: Anchors; Sanity.
- Variants: Loop relocates within ship.

### M-005: Color Subtraction
- Type: Flicker • Category: Sensory
- Trigger: Colors drain; grayscale reality.
- Checks: Sanity DC 14; Perception DC 14 to spot true threats.
- Outcomes: Success: Resist; no penalties. Partial: Disadvantage on Perception 1 hour. Fail: Long-term madness 1.
- Integration: Sanity; Combat disadvantage.
- Variants: Color returns as blood-red only (horror).

### M-006: Language Inversion
- Type: Oscillating • Category: Communication
- Trigger: Speech reverses meaning.
- Checks: Sanity DC 14; Insight DC 14 to adapt.
- Outcomes: Success: No mishaps. Partial: One command misheard (GM tweak). Fail: Critical misorder; -1 round of confusion in combat.
- Integration: Crew roles; Sanity.
- Variants: Written language unaffected/affected.

### M-007: Identity Bleed
- Type: Overlapping • Category: Horror
- Trigger: Crew memories bleed into each other.
- Checks: Group Sanity DC 15; Willing memory trade check.
- Outcomes: Success: Gain temporary proficiency share. Partial: 1 short-term madness. Fail: Permanent swap of a minor memory between two crew.
- Integration: Memory Economy; Sanity.
- Variants: Bleed includes NPC from earlier arc.

### M-008: Door Opens Elsewhere
- Type: Anchored • Category: Navigation
- Trigger: Random door opens onto Aethermoor/Aquabyssos.
- Checks: Anchorite DC 15 to close; Sanity DC 14; choice to explore.
- Outcomes: Success: Close safely. Partial: Small incursion (creature). Fail: Door sticks open; encounter.
- Integration: Cross-world; Anchors; Sanity.
- Variants: Door opens to dream or time-displaced scene.

### M-009: Reality Predator
- Type: Overlapping • Category: Creature
- Trigger: Predator that eats integrity.
- Checks: Combat; Anchorite can repel with DC 15.
- Outcomes: Success: Beast repelled/slain; Integrity +1. Partial: Integrity 0 change; minor hull. Fail: Integrity -1; Sanity checks.
- Integration: Reality; Combat.
- Variants: Predator is immune to certain damage types.

### M-010: Deep Mother Glance
- Type: Any • Category: Cosmic
- Trigger: A shared sense of being seen.
- Checks: Group Sanity DC 16; Religion DC 15 to interpret.
- Outcomes: Success: Boon (choose: +1 Sanity pool or prophecy token). Partial: Ambiguous omen; RP. Fail: Long-term madness 1; ship Sanity -1.
- Integration: Sanity; Prophecy; Vessel Horror.
- Variants: Glance requests a bargain.
