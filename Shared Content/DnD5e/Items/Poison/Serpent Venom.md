# Serpent Venom
## Overview
*Serpent Venom (Injury).* This [[Poisoned|poison]] must be harvested from a dead or [[Incapacitated|incapacitated]] [[Giant Poisonous Snake|giant poisonous snake]]. A creature subjected to this [[Poisoned|poison]] must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (`dice: 3d6`) [[Poisoned|poison]] damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
