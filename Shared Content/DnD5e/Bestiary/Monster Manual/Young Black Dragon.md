***Acid Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The dragon exhales acid in a 30-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 49 (`dice: 11d8`) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
