# The Shadow Markets
*Where Forbidden Goods Find Willing Buyers*

## Overview
The Shadow Markets operate as a vast underground network of illegal trade beneath [[Port Meridian]], controlled by [[The Whisper Syndicate]] and serving as the primary distribution hub for all manner of forbidden goods, from corrupted crystals to stolen artifacts to living beings.

## Operations & Connections
The markets specialize in corrupted crystal trade, stolen artifacts from [[The Sundered Vaults]], weapons from [[The Crimson Foundries]], and information brokerage. The network connects to [[The Bone Colosseum]] via hidden tunnels and serves as money laundering operation for [[Vex Shadowthorn]]'s criminal empire.

## Key Connections
- **[[The Whisper Syndicate]]** - Primary controllers and operators
- **[[Vex Shadowthorn]]** - Ultimate authority over all operations
- **[[The Bone Colosseum]]** - Connected via underground tunnels
- **[[Jasper "Three-Eyes" Flint]]** - Information and intelligence broker
- **[[The Grand Bazaar of Echoing Coins]]** - Competing legitimate marketplace
- **[[The Crimson Foundries]]** - Source of illegal weapons
- **[[Memory Merchant Valeria]]** - Trades in memories and experiences
- **[[The Iron Pits of Malachar]]** - Source of blood-forged metals
- **[[The Carrion Court]]** - Trades in necromantic components
- **[[Captain Lyanna Brightshield]]** - Primary law enforcement threat
- **[[Independent Smugglers]]** - Transportation and logistics
- **[[Marcus "The Shade" Blackwood]]** - Security enforcement
