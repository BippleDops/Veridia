## Special Traits
***Axiomatic Mind.*** The duodrone can't be compelled to act in a manner contrary to its nature or its instructions.

***Disintegration.*** If the duodrone dies, its body disintegrates into dust, leaving behind its weapons and anything else it was carrying.

## Actions
***Multiattack.*** The duodrone makes two fist attacks or two javelin attacks.

***Fist.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (`dice: 1d4`) bludgeoning damage.

***Javelin.*** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (`dice: 1d6+1`) piercing damage.

## Description
The blocky duodrones supervise units of [[Monodrone|monodrones]] and can perform up to two tasks at a time.
