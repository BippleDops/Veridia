## Description
Among the most ancient of alchemical instruments, the pestle and mortar are indispensable for crushing and powdering small quantities of solid materials. Both our 4 oz (3 gp) and 6 oz (6 gp) pestle and mortar sets are made of fine-quality black marble, highly polished on the sections you will handle and left raw on the grinding surfaces. (3 gp)

## Additional Details
2 pestle and mortar sets are needed for a complete laboratory
