## Description
This economical [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items (WOTC)/Candle|candle]] mold will make four foot-long tapers from four feet of thin wicking and a pound of wax or tallow. A favorite among innkeeps and custodial priests, these [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Mundane Items (WOTC)/Candle|candle]] [[Molds|molds]] can keep a whole mansion in stock, and cheaply too.
