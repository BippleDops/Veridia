- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V

A target that is held shut by a mundane lock or that is stuck or barred becomes unlocked, unstuck, or unbarred. If the object has multiple locks, only one of them is unlocked. If the target is held shut by [[arcane-lock-xphb (spells), that spell is suppressed for 10 minutes, during which time the target can be opened and closed.
