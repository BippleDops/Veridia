# S-004 Cloud Canyon

## Read-Aloud
> Walls of cloud rise like alabaster cliffs, their bellies flashing with distant lightning. A narrow corridor invites you in—or swallows you whole.

## Setup
- Band: Mid • Category: Terrain/Weather
- Pirates may lurk in lee-side downdrafts

## Mechanics
- Thread the Maze: Helm DC 14
- Map the Twists: Sensor DC 13
- Lightning Near-Miss: Structure Save DC 14 on failure

## Outcomes
- Success: Safe passage; optional stealth advantage
- Partial: Near-miss (1d6 lightning); minor delay
- Fail: Direct strike (2d6); Disadvantage next Maneuver

## Stat Blocks
### Lightning Vein (Hazard)
AC —; HP —; Save DC 14; On fail: 2d6 lightning to nearest segment; Systems check DC 13

- Add Gravity lens pockets at High band (Helm DC +2)
