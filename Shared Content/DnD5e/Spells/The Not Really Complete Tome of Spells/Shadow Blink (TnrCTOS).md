# Shadow Blink (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *5th-level*

**School**: *conjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 200 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
You gain the ability enter a shadow and move from inside it to inside another shad-ow within 60 feet. Both shadows must be at least the same size as you. You must use 5 feet of movement to enter a shadow. You can use this transportation ability once per round for the duration.
