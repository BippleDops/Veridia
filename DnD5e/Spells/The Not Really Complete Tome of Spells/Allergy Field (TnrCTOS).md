# Allergy Field (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *cantrip*

**School**: *Transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 60 feet

**Components**: V, S, M (a pinch of ragweed)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
You indicate a point within range. All creatures within 10 feet of that point must make a Constitution at advantage saving throw or be incapacitated for the next minute due to uncontrollable sneezing. At the be-ginning of its turn, a creature may make a Constitution saving throw, ending the effect on a success. A creature who is immune to being poisoned cannot be affected by this spell.
