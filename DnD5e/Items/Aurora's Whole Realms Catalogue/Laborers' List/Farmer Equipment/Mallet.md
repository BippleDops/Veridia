## Description
Anyone who works on a farm knows that many problems can be dismissed by a large mallet swung liberally. Our mallets have oaken heads with flared striking surfaces reinforced with bands of steel. We offer a 20 pound mallet (5 gp) of solid oak, as well as a 30-pound mallet of oak core-filled with lead (9 gp).
