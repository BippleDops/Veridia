# The Golden Trade Route
*The Lifeblood of Continental Commerce*

## Overview
The Golden Trade Route is the primary overland commercial highway connecting [[Port Meridian|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Port Meridian]], [[Crystalhaven|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Crystalhaven]], and [[The Sundered Peaks|02 Worldbuilding/Places/The Sundered Peaks]], facilitating the flow of goods, information, and political influence across the continent while facing constant threats from bandits, monsters, and criminal organizations.
