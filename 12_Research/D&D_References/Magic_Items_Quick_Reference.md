### Item Activation Quick Guide

```
ACTIVATION TYPES:

• COMMAND WORD     - Speak activation phrase
• USE ACTIVATION   - Physical interaction
• WORN             - Effects while equipped
• CONSUMABLE       - Single use, destroyed
• CHARGED          - Limited uses, recharges

COMMON ACTIVATION PHRASES:

• Aquabyssos:  "Depths preserve" / "By the tide"
• Aethermoor:  "Winds guide" / "Sky's blessing"
• Universal:   "Activate" / "Commence"
• Emergency:   "Help" / "Emergency" / "Mayday"
```

### Cost Reference

```
TYPICAL MARKET PRICES:

Common Items:       10-50 gold/pearls/crystals
Uncommon Items:     100-500 gold/pearls/crystals
Rare Items:         1,000-5,000 gold/pearls/crystals
Very Rare Items:    10,000+ gold/pearls/crystals

BLACK MARKET MULTIPLIERS:
• Restricted items: x2-3 normal price
• Forbidden items: x5-10 normal price
• Emergency purchase: x2 normal price
• Bulk discount: -10% per 5+ items
```

*Keep this reference during shopping and equipment planning*
*Emergency items should always be carried*
*Check with GM for availability and local pricing variations*
