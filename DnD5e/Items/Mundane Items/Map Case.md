# Map Case

## Overview
**Price**: 1 gp

**Weight**: 1 lb.

**Map Case or Scroll Case.** This cylindrical leather case can hold up to ten rolled-up sheets of [[Paper|paper]] or five rolled-up sheets of [[Parchment|parchment]].
