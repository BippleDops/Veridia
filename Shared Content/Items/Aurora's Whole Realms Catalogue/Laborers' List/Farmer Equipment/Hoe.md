## Description
A simple tool with a thousand uses, the hoe ranks with the shovel in importance among the farmer’s tools. Our hoes feature 6’ long hardwood handles to minimize back pain and iron heads (3 gp). We also offer a heartier steel version (5 gp) that will handle even [[Clay|clay]] soil easily.
