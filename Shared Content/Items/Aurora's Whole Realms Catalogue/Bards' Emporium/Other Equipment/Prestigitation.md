## Description
Bards need not know magic to amaze a crowd, for sleight of hand can transform confusion into magic. We offer all manner of devices for deluding an audience: marked cards (3 gp), card strung together (4 gp), hollow-topped hats (3 gp), pocketed scarves (1 gp), foldable canes (4 gp), flowering rods (4 gp), and retractable knives (3 gp).

## Additional Details
each requires 1 day practice and a Dex check
