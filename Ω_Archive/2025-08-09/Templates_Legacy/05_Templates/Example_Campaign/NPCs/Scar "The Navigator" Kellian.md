# Scar "The Navigator" Kellian

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 40
**Occupation**: Operations Chief of the Shadowhaven Smugglers Ring
**Location**: [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/Locations/Shadowhaven]]

## Description
Scar Kellian is a grizzled and experienced sailor who is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the Shadowhaven Smugglers Ring. He is a master navigator and is known for his ability to find safe passage through even the most treacherous waters.

## GM Notes
- He is fiercely loyal to [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Vex Nightwhisper]].
- He is a rival of [[05_Templates/Example_Campaign/NPCs/Captain Blackwater]] and the Blackwater Raiders.
- He is a good source of information about the city's criminal underworld.
