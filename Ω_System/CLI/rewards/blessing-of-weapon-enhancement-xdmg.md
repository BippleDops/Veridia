# Blessing of Weapon Enhancement

One nonmagical weapon in your possession becomes a [+1 Weapon](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/1-weapon-xdmg.md) while you wield it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 99*
