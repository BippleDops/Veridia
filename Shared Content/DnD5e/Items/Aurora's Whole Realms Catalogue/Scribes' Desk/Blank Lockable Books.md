## Description
Any of our books above may optionally be purchased with a steel-reinforced leather strap and lock.


| **Lock Quality** | **Price** |
|---|---|
| fair | 65 gp |
| good | 100 gp |
| excellent | 200 gp |

Furthermore, you may choose a steel link strap for an additional 35 gp.

## Additional Details
A good lock gives a -5 % for a [[Pick]] Locks attempt; an excellent lock gives -15 %.
