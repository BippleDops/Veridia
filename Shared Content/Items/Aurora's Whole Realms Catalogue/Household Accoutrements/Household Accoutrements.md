# Household Accoutrements
## Overview
Adventuring through a dungeon and living in one are two very different propositions. Though rugged warrior types may wonder at the usefulness of the following [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Items|items]], such folk have the reputation of keeping homes that look like bears’ dens. I will not begrudge their desire to skip these pages. But for those whose lives hold room for elegance and beauty, my catalogue provides delights you will find in no dragon’s lair. All of our household accoutrements are constructed of finest materials by the greatest craftsmen of Toril.
