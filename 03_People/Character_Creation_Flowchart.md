### Realm-Specific Considerations

**Aquabyssos Characters:**
● Pressure Adaptation Required
  - Native: Automatic to 1000 fathoms
  - Adapted: Requires equipment beyond 500 fathoms
  - Visitor: Limited to 200 fathoms safely

● Cultural Knowledge
  - Parliament structure
  - Merchant guild customs
  - Depth-based social hierarchy
  - Crystal corruption awareness

● Starting Equipment
  - Pressure gauge
  - Basic breathing apparatus
  - Depth-rated clothing
  - Communication crystal

**Aethermoor Characters:**
● Altitude Adaptation Required
  - Native: Automatic to 12,000 feet
  - Adapted: Requires equipment beyond 8,000 feet
  - Visitor: Limited to 4,000 feet safely

  - Royal court etiquette
  - Wind current navigation
  - Sky-based transportation
  - Crystal technology basics

  - Altimeter
  - Wind direction compass
  - Altitude-rated gear
  - Emergency descent equipment

### Campaign Integration Questions

1. **What brings your character to the current situation?**
   - Personal quest
   - Professional duty
   - Running from something
   - Seeking answers

2. **What faction interests your character most?**
   - Parliament of Echoes (democracy)
   - Royal Court of Winds (tradition)
   - Merchant Guilds (prosperity)
   - Crystal Wardens (protection)
   - Shadow Conspiracy (power)
   - Independent (freedom)

3. **What is your character's opinion on the two realms?**
   - Pro-unity (realms should merge)
   - Pro-separation (realms should stay apart)
   - Neutral (focus on current problems)
   - Undecided (learning through experience)

4. **How does your character feel about corruption?**
   - Terrified (avoids at all costs)
   - Curious (wants to study it)
   - Determined (fights against it)
   - Resigned (accepts it as reality)

### Starting Hooks by Background

- Investigate trade route disruptions
