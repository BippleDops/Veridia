# Call of Despair (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *8th-level*

**School**: *enchantment*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self (150-foot radius)

**Components**: V, S, M (a small brass gong)

**Duration**: 10 minutes


## Effects
All creatures who are not your allies within range must make a Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Creatures who failed their saving throws will also have disadvantage on all saving throws, ability checks, and initiative rolls for the remainder of the spellâ€™s duration.
