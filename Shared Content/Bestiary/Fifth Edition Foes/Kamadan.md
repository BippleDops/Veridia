*This creature resembles a large, leopardlike cat with green eyes. From its shoulders sprout six serpents that are blackish-green in color, each about twice the length of an adult human. The serpents’ eyes are reddish-yellow.*

**Alignment**: Unaligned

**Armor Class**: 14 (natural armor)

**Hit Points**: 30 (`dice: 4d10+8`)

**Speed**: 40 ft.

**Challenge**: CR 3

**Experience**: 700 XP

## Other Details
**Ability Scores**: STR 18 (4), DEX 15 (2),  CON 15 (2),  INT 5 (-3), WIS 12 (1), CHA 9 (-1)

**Skills**: Stealth +5

**Senses**: darkvision 60 ft.

**Languages**: None

**Size**: Large

**Type**: Monstrosity

**Creature Tags**: Gnome

## Actions
**Multiattack:** The kamadan attacks once with claws, twice with snake appendages, and either bites once or uses sleep breath.

**Bite:*** Melee Attack*: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 9 (`dice: 1d10+4`) piercing damage.

**Claws:** *Melee Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 9 (`dice: 1d10+4`) slashing damage.

**Snakes:** *Melee Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (`dice: 1d10`) piercing damage.

**Sleep Breath (Recharge 5—6):** *Area Attack:* automatic hit, range 30 ft. cone, creatures in the cone. Hit: every creature in the cone must make a successful DC 12 Constitution saving throw or become unconscious for `dice: 2d4` rounds.

## Ecology
**Environment**: Temperate and warm land
