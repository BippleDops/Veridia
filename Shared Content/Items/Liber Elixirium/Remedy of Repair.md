# Remedy of Repair
## Overview
For the relief of moderate lacerations, large bruises, and multiple non-venomous insect bites and stings.

**Hue:** [[Light]] Pink

**Aroma:** Faintly Flowery

**Texture:** Creamy

**Taste:** Sweet

**Recipe**

1 oz. Serapus Lingua Flower, dried

2 oz. Red Sinel, powdered

2 cups black leaf tea leaves

1 1/2 cups distilled water

3/4 oz. Bearâ€™s Claw, powdered

1. Place 4 oz. of loose black tea leaves in a small [[Cauldron|cauldron]].
1. Add 1 1/2 cup of water and bring to a boil.
1. Remove from heat and let tea steep until it is a dark brown color.
1. Place the remaining ingredients in a separate [[Cauldron|cauldron]].
1. Strain tea through a cloth into the second [[Cauldron|cauldron]], capturing the leaves and residue to be discarded afterwards.
1. Bring to a boil for 10 minute, until liquid turns [[Light|light]] pink.
1. Immediately place [[Cauldron|cauldron]] in [[Basin|basin]] of cool water to quench for 10 minutes.
1. Pour into [[Vial|vial]] and place stopper in the [[Vial|vial]].
