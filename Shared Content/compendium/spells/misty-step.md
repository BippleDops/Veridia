# Misty Step
*2nd-level, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Briefly surrounded by silvery mist, you teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see.

**Classes**: Druid (Land), Paladin (Ancients), Paladin (Vengeance), Ranger (Fey Wanderer), Ranger (Horizon Walker), Ranger (Revised) (Fey Wanderer), Ranger (Revised) (Horizon Walker), Ranger (Spell-less) (Fey Wanderer), Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
