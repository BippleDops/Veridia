## Actions
***Multiattack.*** The scorpion makes three attacks: two with its claws and one with its sting.

***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (`dice: 1d8+2`) bludgeoning damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 12). The scorpion has two claws, each of which can grapple only one target.

***Sting.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (`dice: 1d10+2`) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (`dice: 4d10`) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
