# Scale Mail
**Type**: Mundane Item
**Group**: medium armor
**Properties**: AC 14 + Dex (max 2)
**Weight**: 45 lb.
**Value**: 50 gp
## Description: 
This armor consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish. The suit includes gauntlets.The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
