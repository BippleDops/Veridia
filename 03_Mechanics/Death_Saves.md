# Death Save Tracker

## Active Death Saves

| Character | Successes | Failures | Status |
|-----------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Example | ○○○ | ○○○ | Unconscious |

## Quick Rules
- Roll d20 at start of turn
- 10+ = Success
- 9- = Failure
- 20 = Wake with 1 HP
- 1 = 2 failures
- 3 successes = Stable
- 3 failures = Death
