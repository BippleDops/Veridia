# Enspelled Armor (Level 8)
*Legendary (requires attunement)*  

Bound into this armor is a level 8 spell. The spell is determined when the armor is created and must belong to the Abjuration or Illusion school of magic. The armor has 6 charges and regains `d6` expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the armor, you can expend 1 charge to cast its spell.

The spell's saving throw DC is 18, and its attack bonus is `+10`.
