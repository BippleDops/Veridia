## Description
This tube holds milky liquid with a strong alcohol smell. You can use an action to pour the contents of the tube onto a surface within [[3 - Weapons|reach]]. The liquid instantly dissolves up to 1 square foot of adhesive it touches, including **[[Sovereign Glue|sovereign glue]]*.*
