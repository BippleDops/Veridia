# Harbor Master Luna Freedrift
*The Current's Voice, She Who Opens All Ports, The People's Harbor*

## Overview
Harbor Master Luna Freedrift controls [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Freedom Harbor]], the largest free port in [[02_Worldbuilding/Lore/Aquabyssos World Overview]]. Officially appointed by the [[02_Worldbuilding/Places/Parliament of Echoes]], she secretly serves as a key leader in [[02_Worldbuilding/Groups/Free Current]], using her position to protect refugees, coordinate resistance activities, and maintain the only truly neutral trading post in the underwater kingdom. Her harbor serves as sanctuary for those fleeing political persecution, while her information network rivals that of the greatest spymasters.

## Appearance
- **Build**: Athletic and graceful at 5'7" with swimmer's muscle definition
- **Age**: 42 (appears younger due to sea-elf heritage)
- **Distinctive Features**:
  - **Tide-touched eyes**: Sea-green eyes that shift color with her emotions
  - **Flowing hair**: Silver-streaked auburn hair usually braided with sea-glass beads
  - **Webbed fingers**: Subtle webbing between fingers, mark of sea-born heritage
  - **Coral tattoos**: Living coral tattoos on arms that actually grow and change
  - **Harbor master's coat**: Distinctive blue coat with gold trim, symbol of her office
- **Scars**: Rope burns on hands, knife scar along left temple, bite marks from sea creatures

## Personality
- **Public**: Professional, fair-minded, incorruptible harbor official
- **Private**: Passionate freedom fighter, protective guardian, strategic revolutionary
- **Core Values**: Freedom of movement, protection of the innocent, fair trade
- **Leadership Style**: Leads by example, never asks others to take risks she won't
- **Inner Conflict**: Balances official duties with revolutionary activities

## Background & History
### Early Life (42-25 years ago)
- Born to a surface elf trader and deep-sea merfolk diplomat
- Grew up traveling trade routes between surface and depths
- Witnessed the Parliament's increasing authoritarianism firsthand
- Lost family business to corrupt officials and punitive taxation
- Learned that survival required understanding both law and rebellion

### Rise to Power (25-10 years ago)
- Started as dock worker and shipping clerk
- Used natural charisma and organizational skills to rise through ranks
- Appointed Harbor Master after exposing predecessor's corruption
- Gradually transformed Freedom Harbor into true neutral territory
- Began secretly coordinating with Free Current resistance cells

### The Current Harbor Master (10 years to present)
- Established Freedom Harbor's reputation for neutrality and safety
- Built extensive information network throughout Aquabyssos
- Coordinates refugee evacuation routes and safe house networks
- Maintains careful balance between official duties and revolutionary activities
- Has survived multiple attempts to remove her from office

### Special Abilities
**Harbor Authority.** Within Freedom Harbor, Luna can command the assistance of harbor guards, customs officials, and loyal merchants. She has legal authority to detain ships and arrest individuals.

**Information Network.** Luna can use her action to consult her network of informants, functioning as a *legend lore* spell with no casting time or material components (3/day).

**Sea-Born Heritage.** Luna can breathe underwater indefinitely and has advantage on all swimming checks. She can communicate simple concepts with sea creatures.

**Sneak Attack.** Luna deals an extra 21 (6d6) damage when she hits with a weapon attack and has advantage on the roll, or when her target is within 5 feet of an ally.

**Cunning Action.** Luna can take a Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as a bonus action.

**Evasion.** When Luna is subjected to an effect that allows a Dexterity save for half damage, she takes no damage on a success and half damage on a failure.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Luna makes two attacks with her rapier or shortbow, or she can make one attack and use a cunning action.

**Rapier of Harbor Authority.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) additional damage if target is trespassing in her harbor.

**Shortbow +1.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d6 + 6) piercing damage.

**Harbor Master's Command (3/Day).** Luna issues an authoritative command to one creature within 60 feet. Functions as *command* spell (save DC 18) but works on any creature that recognizes her authority.

**Call for Backup (1/Day).** Luna signals for assistance. 2d4 harbor guards arrive in 1d4 rounds to assist her.

### Legendary Actions (3 per turn)
- Move without provoking opportunity attacks
- Make a weapon attack
- Issue a Harbor Master's Command (costs 2 actions)
- Activate information network to learn one fact about current situation (costs 2 actions)

- **Sanctuary Bells**: Warning system that grants temporary asylum to anyone who reaches them
- **The Current's Flow**: Hidden passage system throughout harbor for emergencies
- **Trade Plaza**: Open-air market where surface and deep-sea goods are exchanged

### Special Rules
- **Neutral Territory**: No political violence or arrests allowed within harbor limits
- **Fair Trade Laws**: All merchants must be treated equally regardless of origin
- **Sanctuary Rights**: Anyone can claim temporary asylum while in the harbor
- **Information Freedom**: News and communication cannot be censored within harbor
- **Cultural Exchange**: All cultures and traditions are welcome and protected
