## Usage Notes

### For Game Masters
- Update this template monthly during active political ripple periods
- Focus on consequences that create future adventure opportunities
- Remember that political changes often create unintended side effects
- Consider how political changes affect different social classes differently

### Customization Guidelines
- Adapt faction assessment section to match campaign's political landscape
- Modify terminology to fit campaign setting (kingdoms vs. republics vs. empires)
- Scale magnitude and visibility ratings to fit campaign scope
- Include setting-specific political institutions and processes

### Integration with Other Systems
- Link to economic consequence tracking for comprehensive impact assessment
- Connect with NPC relationship systems for political alliance tracking
- Integrate with faction standing mechanics for numerical progression
- Coordinate with session planning for political adventure opportunities
