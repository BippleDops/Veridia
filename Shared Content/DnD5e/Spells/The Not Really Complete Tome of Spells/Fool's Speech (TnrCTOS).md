# Fool's Speech (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *3rd-level*

**School**: *illusion*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S, M (a small bone whistle)

**Duration**: 1 hour


## Effects
While casting this spell, you touch up to eight willing targets. This gives you and your allies the ability to speak in a secret language that is incomprehensible gibberish to everyone else. This language cannot be under-stood even when using spells such as *com-prehend languages* or *tongues*.
