## Actions
***Multiattack.*** The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (`dice: 2d10+5`) piercing damage plus 5 (`dice: 1d10`) lightning damage.

***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (`dice: 2d6+5`) slashing damage.

***Lightning Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The dragon exhales lightning in an 60-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 55 (`dice: 10d10`) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
