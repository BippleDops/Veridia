# Cloak of Arachnida

## Overview

**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *very rare*

**Attunement**: *requires attunement*

## Description

This fine garment is made of black silk interwoven with faint silvery threads. While wearing it, you gain the following benefits:

- You have [[17 - Damage and Healing|resistance]] to [[17 - Damage and Healing|poison]] damage.
- You have a [[8 - Movement|climbing]] speed equal to your walking speed.
- You can move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving your hands free.
- You can’t be caught in webs of any sort and can move through webs as if they were [[13 - Movement and Position|difficult terrain]].
- You can use an action to cast the **[[Web|web]]** spell (save [[4 - Ability Checks|DC]] 13). The web created by the spell fills twice its normal area. Once used, this property of the cloak can’t be used again until the next dawn.
