# Detect Secret Passages and Portals (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *divination*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute


## Effects
For the duration, you can sense the pres-ence of secret doors, compartments, and so on that have been deliberately constructed to escape detection within 10 feet of you.
