# Power Word Banish (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *9th-level*

**School**: *conjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V

**Duration**: Instantaneous


## Effects
You utter a word of great power that forces one elemental, fiend, or celestial that you can see within range to immediately re-turn to its home plane.
