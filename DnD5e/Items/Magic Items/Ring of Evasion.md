## Description
This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `dice: 1d3` (1d3) expended charges daily at dawn. When you fail a [[Dexterity|Dexterity]] saving throw while wearing it, you can use your [[12 - The Order of Combat|reaction]] to expend 1 of its charges to succeed on that saving throw instead.
