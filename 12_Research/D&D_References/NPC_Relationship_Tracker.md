### Social Interaction Guide

```
RELATIONSHIP IMPROVEMENT:

• Give gifts appropriate to their interests
• Provide useful information
• Help with their problems/goals
• Show respect for their values
• Remember personal details
• Be reliable and trustworthy
• Defend them when they're absent

RELATIONSHIP DAMAGE:

• Break promises or commitments
• Betray their trust
• Insult their values or beliefs
• Ignore their warnings or advice
• Forget important personal details
• Put them in danger unnecessarily
• Associate with their enemies
```
