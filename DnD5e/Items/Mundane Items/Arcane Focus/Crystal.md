# Crystal

## Overview
**Price**: 10 gp

**Weight**: 1 lb.

**Arcane Focus.** An [[Arcane Focus|arcane focus]] is a [[3 - Weapons|special]] item— an [[Orb|orb]], a crystal, a [[Rod|rod]], a specially constructed [[Staff|staff]], a [[Wand|wand]]—like length of wood, or some similar item— designed to channel the power of arcane spells. A [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]], [[Warlock|warlock]], or [[Wizard|wizard]] can use such an item as a [[7 - Spellcasting|spellcasting]] focus.
