# Call Upon Faith (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *cantrip*

**School**: *Evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: 1 round


## Effects
You call upon your god to bless you. If you have been true to faith (as determined by the DM) you may make one roll in your next round at advantage.
