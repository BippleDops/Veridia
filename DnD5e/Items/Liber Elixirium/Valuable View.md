# Valuable View
## Overview
For the complete restoration of eyesight, lost through either natural or magical causes.

**Hue:** Lemon Chiffon

**Aroma:** Neutral

**Texture:** Silky

**Taste:** Tasteless

**Recipe**

3 oz. Rue petals, dried

1 oz. [[Tiger]] Eye gem, ground

4 oz. [[Owl]]’s blood

1 [[Cyclops]] eye, whole

1 [[Eagle]] eye, whole

1. Place Rue petals, [[Tiger]] Eye powder, and [[Cyclops]] eye in a cloth [[Sack|sack]] and tie securely with string.
1. Pour [[Owl]]’s blood into a [[Cauldron|cauldron]] and heat until it simmers.
1. Steep cloth [[Sack|sack]] in the [[Owl]]’s blood for 12 minutes.
1. Carefully lift [[Sack|sack]] from the [[Cauldron|cauldron]] and pour the contents back into [[Cauldron|cauldron]]. Mixture will bubble violently and begin to emit a foul odor.
1. Once bubbling subsides, quickly add [[Eagle]] eye and stir gently until liquid is [[Light|light]] yellow and the foul odor dissipates.
1. Pour into [[Vial|vial]] and place stopper in the [[Vial|vial]] immediately.
