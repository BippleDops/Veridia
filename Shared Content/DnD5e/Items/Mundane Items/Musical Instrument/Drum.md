# Drum

## Overview
**Price**: 6 gp

**Weight**: 3 lb.

**Musical Instrument.** Several of the most common types of [[Musical Instrument|musical instruments]] are shown on the table as examples. If you have proficiency with a given musical instrument, you can add your [[3 - Proficiency Bonus|proficiency bonus]] to any [[4 - Ability Checks|ability checks]] you make to play music with the instrument. A [[Bard|bard]] can use a musical instrument as a [[7 - Spellcasting|spellcasting]] focus. Each type of musical instrument requires a separate proficiency.
