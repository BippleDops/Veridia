# Cracked Driftglobe

**Type:** Magic Item

**Group:** Wondrous Items

**Rarity:** uncommon

**Weight**: 1 lbs.

## Description

This small sphere of thick glass weighs 1 pound. If you are within 60 feet of it, you can speak its command word and cause it to emanate the [[light]] spell.
