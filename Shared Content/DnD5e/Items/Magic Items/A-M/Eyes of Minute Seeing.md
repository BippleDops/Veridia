# Eyes of Minute Seeing

## Overview
**Type**: Magic Item

**Group**: *Wondrous item*

**Rarity**: *Uncommon*

## Description
These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. While wearing them, you can see much better than normal out to arange of 1 foot. You have [[2 - Advantage and Disadvantage|advantage]] on [[Intelligence|Intelligence]] ([[Intelligence|Investigation]]) checks that rely on sight while searching an area or studying an object within that range.
