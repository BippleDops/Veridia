# The Sky–Sea Bridge

A road appears where there was only air and pressure and hope.

What you notice
- A hum that matches your heartbeat
- Steps that feel like flying

What you can try
- Walk steady and sing
- Watch the edges

Possible outcomes
- A new way opens
- It sways
- It breaks
