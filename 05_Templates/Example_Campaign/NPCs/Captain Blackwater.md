# Captain Blackwater

## General Info
**Race**: Human
**Gender**: Male
**Age**: 50
**Occupation**: Pirate Captain
**Location**: [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/The Contested Waters.md]]

## Description
Captain Blackwater is a ruthless and cunning pirate who preys on the merchant vessels that travel through The Contested Waters. He is a dangerous foe, but he is also a man of his word and will honor any deals that he makes.

## GM Notes
- He is the leader of the Blackwater Raiders, a notorious band of pirates.
- He is a rival of [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/NPCs/Captain Valerius.md]].
- He has a secret base on [[01_Campaigns/Campaign_Name/Locations/Merchant's Rest Island.md]].
