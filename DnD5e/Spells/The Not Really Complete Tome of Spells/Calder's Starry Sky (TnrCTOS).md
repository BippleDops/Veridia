# Calder's Starry Sky (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *illusion*

**Special Nature**: *ritual*

**Casting Time**: 1 minute

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V, S, M (an unblemished silver mirror)

**Duration**: 3 hours


## Effects
A completely accurate starmap of the local sky is projected upwards from the mirror. It can be used to study the sky or, if cast out-side, the aid in navigation during the day or on overcast nights.
