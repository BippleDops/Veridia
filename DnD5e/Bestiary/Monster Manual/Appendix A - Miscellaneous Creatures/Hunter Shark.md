## Description
Smaller than a [[Giant Shark|giant shark]] but larger and fiercer than a [[Reef Shark|reef shark]], a hunter shark haunts deep waters. It usually hunts alone, but multiple hunter sharks might feed in the same area. A fully grown hunter shark is 15 to 20 feet long.
