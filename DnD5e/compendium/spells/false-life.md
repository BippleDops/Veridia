# False Life
*1st-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a small amount of alcohol or distilled spirits
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Bolstering yourself with a necromantic facsimile of life, you gain 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points for the duration.

**Classes**: Artificer, Artificer (Revisited), Cleric (Death), Cleric (Grave), Sorcerer, Warlock (Undead), Warlock (Undying), Wizard

Source: Player's Handbook p. 239
