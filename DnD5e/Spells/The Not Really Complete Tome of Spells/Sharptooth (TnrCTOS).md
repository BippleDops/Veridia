# Sharptooth (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *1st-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
This spell can only be cast upon a crea-ture with a bite attack. For the duration, one creature you touch deals +1 damage with its bite attack.
