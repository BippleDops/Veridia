## Description

These furred boots are snug and feel quite warm. While you wear them, you gain the following benefits:

- You have [[17 - Damage and Healing|resistance]] to [[17 - Damage and Healing|cold]] damage.
- You ignore [[13 - Movement and Position|difficult terrain]] created by ice or snow.
- You can tolerate temperatures as low as −50 degrees Fahrenheit without any additional protection. If you wear [[3 - Weapons|heavy]] clothes, you can tolerate temperatures as low as −100 degrees Fahrenheit.
