# Chromatic Blade (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *5th-level*

**School**: *evocation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S, M (a dagger set with gems of seven colors, worth at least 100 gp)

**Duration**: 1 minute


## Effects
You create a magical blade of shimmering, weightless force that constantly changes hue. You may choose to wield the blade or give it to an ally. The blade inflicts 4d8 force damage.
