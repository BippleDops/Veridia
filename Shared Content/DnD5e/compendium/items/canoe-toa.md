# Canoe
*Ship, Vehicle (water)*  

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 100.0 lbs.

A canoe can be purchased in Port Nyanzaru for 50 gp. It holds up to six Medium creatures and has a maximum speed of 2 mph. It is otherwise identical to a [rowboat](/compendium/items/rowboat.md).

*Source: Tomb of Annihilation p. 31*
