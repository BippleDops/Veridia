# Impeding Permission (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *5th-level*

**School**: *enchantment*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 300 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour


## Effects
You choose one creature in range that you can see and that has an Intelligence of 5 or greater. That creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. If it fails, it is charmed by you for the duration. Creatures immune to being charmed are not affected by this spell. The creature can take no action without first gaining your permission.
