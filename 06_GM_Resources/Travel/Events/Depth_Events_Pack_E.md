# Depth Events Pack E — Legendary Tier (12)

High stakes, epic consequences. Use with all core systems; expect campaign-shaping outcomes.

### D-L-01: Leviathan Waking
- Trigger: A great one stirs.
- Stakes: City-scale shockwaves; eco upheaval.
- Checks: Command DC 18; Helm DC 18; Anchorite DC 17.
- Outcomes: Success: Soothe back to sleep; global rumor; faction shifts. Partial: Coastal damage; moral choice. Fail: Catastrophe; arc opens to “Age of Leviathans.”

### D-L-02: Abyssal Crown Summit
- Trigger: Factions convene in trench cathedral.
- Stakes: War or unity.
- Checks: Diplomacy DC 17; Law/History DC 16; Tactics DC 16 security.
- Outcomes: Success: Accord; war clock -2 phases. Partial: Fragile truce. Fail: Assassination; war clock +2.
