# Dusts of Death (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *6th-level*

**School**: *transmutation*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Touch

**Components**: V, S M (a pinch of sand)

**Duration**: 1 hour


## Effects
You turn a pinch of sand into an incredibly toxic poison. If introduced into food and drink and then consumed, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. The target takes 10d10 poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one, and if still alive, is poisoned for 1 day.
