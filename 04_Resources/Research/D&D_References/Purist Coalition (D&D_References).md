The Purist Coalition opposes all use of Aether Crystals, viewing them as divine punishment for mortal hubris. They seek to destroy all crystals and return [[Aethermoor|02 Worldbuilding/Places/Aethermoor]] to its pre-Shattering state. What began as peaceful protest has evolved into an organized resistance with extremist cells conducting terrorist attacks. ### The Three Pillars

1. **Purification**: Cleanse the land of crystal corruption
2. **Redemption**: Save those who can abandon crystal use
3. **Retribution**: Punish those who spread corruption ### Militant Actions
