# Ballista
## Overview
*Large object*

**Armor Class**: 15

**Hit Points**: 50

**Damage Immunities**: [[Poisoned|poison]], psychic

A ballista is a massive crossbow that fires [[Heavy|heavy]] bolts. Before it can be fired, it must be loaded and aimed. It takes one action to load the weapon, one action to aim it, and one action to fire it.

**Bolt**. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, [[Range|range]] 120/480 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3dl0) piercing damage.
