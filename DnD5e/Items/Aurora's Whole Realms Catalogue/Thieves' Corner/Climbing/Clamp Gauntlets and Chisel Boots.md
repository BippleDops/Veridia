## Description
These ingenious [[2. Mechanics Reference Almanac/Items/Items|items]] were invented by the same gnomish craftsman who made the [[Thieving Helmet|thieving helmet]]. By means of narrow-tipped fingers and toes and a ratcheted clamp mechanism, in the gauntlets, thieves can find a handhold on most external wall [[Surfaces|surfaces]]: cut-stone, riverstone, or wood. Either size: 25 gp.

## Additional Details
+10 bonus to Climb Walls; -5 penalty to attack rolls; halves movement; cannot be used with other climbing gear
