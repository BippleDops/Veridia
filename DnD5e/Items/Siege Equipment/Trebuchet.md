# Trebuchet
## Overview
*Huge object*

**Armor Class**: 15

**Hit Points**: 150

**Damage Immunities**: poison, psychic

A trebuchet is a powerful [[Catapult|catapult]] that throws its payload in a high arc, so it can hit targets behind cover. Before the trebuchet can be fired, it must be loaded and aimed. It takes two actions to load the weapon, two actions to aim it, and one action to fire it.

A trebuchet typically hurls a [[Heavy|heavy]] stone. However, it can launch other kinds of projectiles, such as barrels of [[Oil|oil]] or sewage, with different effects.

**Trebuchet Stone**. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit. [[Range|range]] 300/1,200 ft. (can't hit targets within 60 feet of it. one target. Hit: 44 (`dice: 8d10`) bludgeoning damage.
