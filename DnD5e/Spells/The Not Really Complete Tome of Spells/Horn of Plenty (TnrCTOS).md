# Horn of Plenty (TnrCTOS)

## Overview
**Level**: *6th-level*

**School**: *conjuration*

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes


## Effects
You turn a hollowed-out horn into a mag-ical cornucopia. Each minute, it produces enough raw fruits, vegetables, and grain to feed six humanoids or two steeds for a day, or enough water, wine, or ale to slake the thirst of six humanoids or two steeds for a day. Each minute, you may choose whether you wish to produce food or drink.
