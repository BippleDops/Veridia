# Appendix B - Nonplayer Characters
## Overview
This appendix contains statistics for various humanoid nonplayer characters (NPCs) that adventurers might encounter during a D&D campaign, including lowly [[Commoner|commoners]] and mighty [[Archmage|archmages]]. These stat blocks can be used to represent both human and nonhuman NPCs.
